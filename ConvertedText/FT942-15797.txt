FT  13 APR 94 / International Capital Markets: Options trading put back


   By REUTER


   MILAN


Options trading on the Italian futures market (MIF) has been put back at
least a week because of technical problems, Reuter reports from Milan.
Screen-based options trading on Italian fixed-rate bonds (BTPs) was due to
start on April 15 but will now start on April 22 or April 29.
