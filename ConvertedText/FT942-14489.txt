FT  20 APR 94 / Observer: Rip-off yarns


Does crime pay, even 20 years on? The BBC and London University are charging
Pounds 25 per head for a conference this month on the Watergate affair. Star
attractions include John Dean, President Nixon's former legal counsel, and
Jeb Magruder, billed as 'the man who gave the Watergate burglars their
orders'.
