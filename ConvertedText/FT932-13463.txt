FT  23 APR 93 / Press advertising growth forecast


NEWSPAPER advertising levels are about to show considerable growth, the
Newspaper Society's annual conference was told yesterday by Mr Mike
Waterson, research adviser to the Advertising Association.
Two thirds of the newspaper-buying public have experienced problems when
buying papers ranging from late delivery to titles being sold out, according
to an NOP survey.
