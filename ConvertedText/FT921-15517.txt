FT  09 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Cassidy Brothers ahead by 44%


Cassidy Brothers, the USM-quoted toy maker, improved pre-tax profits by 44
per cent from Pounds 458,154 to Pounds 658,898 in the six months to October
31.
Turnover advanced to Pounds 4.07m (Pounds 3.59m). Tax was little changed at
Pounds 206,000 (Pounds 200,500) leaving earnings per share at 8.39p (4.77p).
A maiden interim dividend of 0.75p is declared.
