FT  27 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Torday & Carlisle


Directors of Torday & Carlisle, which has interests in diesel engines,
marine and industrial components and commercial signs, is withdrawing Oldham
Signs from sale.
They said a number of offers had been received but it was not possible to
conclude a transaction which they believed to be in the best interests of
shareholders. They have decided on a restructuring of the business.
