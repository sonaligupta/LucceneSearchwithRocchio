FT  22 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Victoria Carpet falls to Pounds 227,000


Pre-tax profits of Victoria Carpet Holdings fell from Pounds 883,000 to
Pounds 227,000 for the six months ended September 30. Turnover declined from
Pounds 17.19m to Pounds 16.37m.
The results reflected the recession, and in particular the sharp downturn in
the housing market.
Earnings dropped to 2.03p (8.48p) per share.
