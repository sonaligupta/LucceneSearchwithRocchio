FT  04 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Betterware sales increase by 50%


Betterware, the household goods retailer which specialises in direct home
shopping, has announced a 50 per cent increase in trading during the first
quarter of the current financial year.
The company is to invest Pounds 9m on a new distribution centre to cope with
the increase in demand.
To assist marketability of its shares, Betterware has obtained shareholder
approval for a 3-for-2 scrip issue.
