FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Regent Corporation advances to Pounds 0.3m


Regent Corporation, the property developer, announced pre-tax profits of
Pounds 302,000 for the six months to September 30 on sales of Pounds 1.68m.
In the previous first half pre-tax profits were Pounds 32,000 on sales of
Pounds 142,000.
Earnings per share this time came through at 0.91p (0.59p).
Regent said yesterday that it anticipated the early resumption of dividend
payments.
