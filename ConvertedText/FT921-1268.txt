FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Arthur Wood ahead 12% to Pounds 261,000


Arthur Wood & Son (Longport), the earthenware manufacturer, lifted pre-tax
profits by 12 per cent, from Pounds 234,244 to Pounds 261,431, over 1991.
The increase was struck on turnover ahead some 7 per cent at Pounds 3.7m. Mr
Anthony Wood, chairman, said that trading conditions 'remained extremely
difficult'.
Exports expanded by 23 per cent but only accounted for 12.9 per cent of
total turnover.
Earnings per share were 9.28p (8.36p). The single distribution is again
4.1p.
