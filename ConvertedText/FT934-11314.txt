FT  02 NOV 93 / Czech bank merger


   By REUTER


   PRAGUE


A merger between two Czech commercial banks, Investicni Banka and Postovni
Banka, should be approved soon, a national bank spokesman said yesterday,
Reuter reports from Prague. Investicni Banka last month bought just under 70
per cent of Postovni Banka's shares.
