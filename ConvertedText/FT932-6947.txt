FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Baltic


BALTIC has completed the sale of Capital Asset Finance, its subsidiary
engaged in the leasing of Fork Lift trucks, at net asset value (Pounds
242,000 at December 31). Proceeds have resulted in a repayment of
inter-company loans of about Pounds 20m.
