FT  16 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Simon Engineering


SIMON ENGINEERING, through its Simon-Macawber offshoot and its contract
management partner, ATSI of Buffalo, New York, has won a Dollars 16.6m
(Pounds 11m) contract from Bethlehem Steel Corporation, Pennsylvania for the
Simon-Macawber/British Steel coal injection system.
