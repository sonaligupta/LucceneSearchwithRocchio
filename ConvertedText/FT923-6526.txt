FT  24 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Housing scheme


Work has started on phase two of an initiative by Gwalia Housing Society
which will eventually create 800 replacement homes in a Swansea community.
The Pounds 7.7m project is being designed and built by WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION.
The scheme will provide 185 brick-clad homes on an estate in Blaenymaes.
