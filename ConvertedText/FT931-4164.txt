FT  12 MAR 93 / Ulster homes body sheds 700 jobs


MORE than 700 maintenance staff with the Northern Ireland Housing Executive
are to lose their jobs.
The executive's direct labour organisation is to close because of its
failure to win sufficient work through competitive tendering.
About 550 permanent and 180 temporary workers will be affected.
