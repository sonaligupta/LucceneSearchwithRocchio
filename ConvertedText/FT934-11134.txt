FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Allen to acquire Andrew Sykes arm


Allen is acquiring Centahire, a tool hiring subsidiary of Andrew Sykes
Group, for an estimated Pounds 713,000.
Centahire, based in Peterlee, County Durham, has six depots in the north
east. In the year to March 31 1993, it made an operating loss of Pounds
97,777 on turnover of Pounds 1.16m.
