FT  22 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: IRA terrorists jailed


IRA terrorists Gerard Mackin, 33, and Derek Doherty, 23, were each jailed
for 25 years at the Old Bailey for plotting a bombing campaign in north
London. Jurors could not agree a verdict on Thomas McAuley, who was also
accused. He will face a retrial.
