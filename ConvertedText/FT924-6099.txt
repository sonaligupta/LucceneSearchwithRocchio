FT  23 NOV 92 / Survey of Eastern France (10): Financing of TGV link
disputed with Paris


Eastern France faces a continuing wrangle with Paris over the financing of a
new Trains a Grande Vitesse high speed rail link from the French capital
through Strasbourg and on to Germany's Inter-City Express network. The line,
due to open early in the next decade, is urgently wanted across the eastern
region. Alsace sees the line as an important step in the economic
development of the upper Rhine valley and a catalyst to trade between France
and eastern Europe.
