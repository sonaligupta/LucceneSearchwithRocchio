FT  16 DEC 94 / Rosyth yard wins Pounds 100m contract


The Rosyth dockyard in Fife has won a Pounds 100m contract to refit the
Royal Navy nuclear submarine HMS Superb, it announced yesterday.
The 2 1/2 -year contract follows on from a similar contract for a sister
submarine of the Swiftsure class, Sovereign, which is nearing completion.
Babcock Rosyth Defence, managers of the yard, which employs about 3,500,
said work would start immediately and last until mid-1997, providing work
for up to 1,000 people.
