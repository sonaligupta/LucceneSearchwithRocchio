FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: IES at Pounds 271,000


IES Group, the security company traded under Rule 4.2, reported pre-tax
profits of Pounds 271,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.61m in the nine months
since its share placing in December.
Earnings for the period to September 30 were 9.9p.
