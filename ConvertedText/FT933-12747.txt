FT  22 JUL 93 / International Company News: Net at Borland advances to
Dollars 6m


   By LOUISE KEHOE


BORLAND International, the US personal computer software company, reported a
strong advance in first-quarter earnings, as sales of its new programs for
use with Microsoft's Windows operating system took off, writes Louise Kehoe.
Net income for the quarter was Dollars 6.2m, a 259 per cent improvement on
last time's Dollars 1.7m. Per-share earnings were 22 cents, against 6 cents.
First-quarter revenues were Dollars 123.4m, up 7.5 per cent from Dollars
114.8m last year.
