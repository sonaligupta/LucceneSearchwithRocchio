FT  16 DEC 94 / Labour confident of victory


Ian Pearson, Labour candidate in the Dudley West by-election, campaigns for
votes for the final time yesterday. Voting began slowly in the Black Country
constituency but improved later. Labour was expected to win the seat which
has had a Tory MP since 1979.
