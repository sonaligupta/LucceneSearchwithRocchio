FT  22 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Java


Net asset value per share of Edinburgh Java Trust stood at 46.9p at June 30,
against 61.39p six months earlier and 39.3p at end-June 1993.
The company incurred a deficit of Pounds 88,000 (Pounds 16,000) for the
period, giving losses per share of 0.2933p (0.0908p).
The trust, managed by Edinburgh Fund Managers, was formerly known as EFM
Java Trust; its investments are concentrated in Indonesia.
