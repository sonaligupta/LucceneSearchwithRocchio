FT  24 AUG 92 / Former SNP leader dies


MR Donald J. Stewart, the former parliamentary leader of the Scottish
Nationalist party, died yesterday, aged 71.
The former MP for the Western Isles was the constituency's first SNP member
and the first to win a seat for the party at a general election.
He died in hospital a week after a heart attack.
