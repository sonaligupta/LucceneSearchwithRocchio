FT  27 SEP 94 / Plane wreckage recovered


The wreckage of a Hercules L100 - 30 transport aircraft (on left) which
crashed into Hong Kong harbour last Friday is lifted by crane on to a barge
next to the colony's airport as another aircraft takes off yesterday. Six
Indonesian crew died in the crash
