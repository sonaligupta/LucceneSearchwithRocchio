FT  29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Keller 1.3 times subscribed


   By ANDREW BOLGER


Keller Group, the international ground engineering company, said its placing
and intermediaries offer had been 1.3 times subscribed, writes Andrew
Bolger.
The flotation priced the shares at 130p, giving the group a market value of
Pounds 72.8m.
It placed firm 19.2m shares with institutional investors and 14.99m shares
conditionally, subject to recall for the intermediaries offer. Dealings are
expected to start on May 5.
