FT  23 APR 93 / International Company News: Chairman of Upjohn dies


   By Our Financial Staff


MR Theodore Cooper, chairman and chief operating officer of Upjohn, the US
pharmaceuticals company, died yesterday, aged 64, Our Financial Staff
writes. In January he was diagnosed as suffering from a form of bone cancer.
Last week, when Mr Cooper's condition took a sudden turn for the worse, the
company named Mr Ley Smith, president and chief operating officer, as acting
chairman.
