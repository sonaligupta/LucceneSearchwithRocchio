FT  04 AUG 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Table tennis title for China


Deng Yaping and Qiao Hong of China won the gold in the women's table tennis
doubles. The silver went to compatriates Chen Zihe and Gao Jun. The bronze
was shared by Li Bun Hui and Yu Sun Book of North Korea and Hong Cha Ok and
Hyun Jung Hwa of South Korea.
