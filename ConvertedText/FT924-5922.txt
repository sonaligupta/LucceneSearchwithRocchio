FT  24 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Hewetson down and reduces interim


Hewetson, which serves the construction industry, suffered a 32 per cent
fall in interim pre-tax profits and is cutting the dividend by 69 per cent.
However, the group had some success with gearing, reducing it from 48 per
cent to 28 per cent.
In the six months to September 30 turnover fell 26 per cent to Pounds 14.3m
(Pounds 19.2m) but the operating surplus dropped 43 per cent to Pounds
650,000 (Pounds 1.15m). After reduced interest charges pre-tax profit worked
through at Pounds 476,000, against Pounds 703,000 which was struck after
exceptional charges of Pounds 154,000.
Earnings per share came to 2.09p (4.6p) and the interim distribution is 0.5p
(1.6p).
