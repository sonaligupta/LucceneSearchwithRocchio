FT  23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Mortgage net assets up 5.5%


Scottish Mortgage and Trust had a net asset value of 183.2p at September 30
 -a rise of 5.5 per cent on the 173.7p at the trust's March year-end. The
interim dividend goes up from 1.3p to 1.35p.
