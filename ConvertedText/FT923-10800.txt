FT  28 JUL 92 / Chinese bus plant leased


   By REUTER


   BEIJING


CHINA has leased a state-owned factory to a foreign company for the first
time in four decades, marking another milestone in its efforts to turn
around loss-making industries, Reuter reports from Beijing.
The Economic Information Daily said yesterday that a plant in Sichuan
province manufacturing buses had been leased for 15 years to a Hong Kong
company for Dollars 11.5m.
