FT  25 MAR 92 / World Trade News: Japan bidding


   By REUTER


   WASHINGTON


An Dollars 800m (Pounds 462m) Japanese airport project has been approved for
bidding by US groups under a Japanese-American construction-trade accord,
the US Commerce Department said, Reuter reports from Washington.
Architecture and engineering contracts on a Fukuoka airport terminal make up
the first of six new market-opening projects to be announced.
