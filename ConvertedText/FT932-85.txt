FT  30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: NatWest disposal in Australia


National Westminster Bank has sold the retail banking assets and liabilities
of NatWest Australia Bank in South Australia and Western Australia to
Challenge Bank.
The assets comprise commercial loans, mortgages, leases and overdrafts. NWAB
corporate banking and treasury operations were unaffected by the
transaction.
