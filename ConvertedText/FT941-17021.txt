FT  06 JAN 94 / International Company News: Procter deal


   By REUTER


Procter & Gamble of the US has acquired an additional 13 per cent stake in
the cleaning products maker Compania Quimica, a week after buying 74 per
cent of the Argentine company, Reuter reports.
No price or further details of the transaction was given.
