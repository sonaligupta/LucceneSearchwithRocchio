FT  22 OCT 92 / UK Company News: English National net assets lower


English National Investment Company reported falls in net asset value for
its preferred ordinary and deferred ordinary shares of 11.9 per cent and
15.4 per cent respectively.
At September 30, net asset value per preferred ordinary was 290.5p, against
329.96p a year earlier, and 215.5p (254.96p) per deferred ordinary.
Earnings per share for the six months came to 7.72p (7.33p) per preferred
ordinary and 5.27p (4.88p) per deferred ordinary.
The respective interim dividends are maintained at 5p and 2.55p.
