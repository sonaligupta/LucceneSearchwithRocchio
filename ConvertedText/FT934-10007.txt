FT  08 NOV 93 / People: Finance moves


Francois Defferriere (below left) has been appointed md of FIMAT FUTURES UK,
the UK arm of Fimat Banque; he moves from Japan where he has been
establishing Fimat's Asia operations; he replaces Brian Kaye who becomes
chairman of both Fimat Futures UK and Cannon Bridge Clearing.
