FT  16 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Hampden nears breakeven


Hampden Group, the USM-traded homecare and DIY retailer, managed to approach
breakeven in the second half of the year to January 1, leaving pre-tax
losses at Pounds 220,000 against profits of Pounds 482,000. Interim losses
had been Pounds 198,000.
Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 30m (Pounds 29.8m). Losses per share came out
at 1.47p (2.08p earnings). The dividend is being passed. Last time there was
a total of 1p.
