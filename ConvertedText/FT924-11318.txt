FT  27 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Geared Income


Geared Income Investment Trust had a net asset value of 68.71p at September
30 - a decline of some 30 per cent on the 97.55p at the same stage of 1991.
Net revenue increased to Pounds 865,521 (Pounds 796,699) over the six months
to end-September, equivalent to earnings of 3.93p (3.62p) per share.
A second interim dividend of 1.75p makes an unchanged 3.25p so far this
year.
