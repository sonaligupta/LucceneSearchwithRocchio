FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Symonds achieves measure of recovery


Symonds Engineering, the general and precision engineer, achieved a measure
of recovery with pre-tax profits rising 49 per cent to Pounds 124,000 in the
six months to end-September.
Improved margins were reflected in the advance; turnover was down from
Pounds 2.92m to Pounds 2.67m but trading profits rose from Pounds 100,000 to
Pounds 161,000. Interest charges virtually doubled to Pounds 38,000 to check
the advance at pre-tax level.
Earnings per share amounted to 0.892p (0.656p). The interim dividend is
maintained at 0.3p. Pre-tax profits in the half-year to September 30 1989
were Pounds 223,000.
