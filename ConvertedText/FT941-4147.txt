FT  12 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Thomas Walker improves to Pounds 48,195


Thomas Walker, the Birmingham-based maker of metal smallwares, reported
pre-tax profits of Pounds 48,195 for the six months to end-December, against
a deficit of Pounds 71,400.
Turnover grew from Pounds 1.7m to Pounds 1.94m. Earnings per share came out
at 0.587p (1.1591p losses) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.18p.
