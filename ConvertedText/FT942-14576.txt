FT  20 APR 94 / Kigali shelled


   By REUTER


   KIGALI


Rwandan troops shelled Kigali's refugee-packed national stadium yesterday,
killing nine, and the UN evacuated some of its forces without a ceasefire in
sight, Reuter reports from Kigali.
