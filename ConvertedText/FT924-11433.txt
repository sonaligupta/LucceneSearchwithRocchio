FT  26 OCT 92 / International Company News: Bridgend falls to Pounds 0.3m
and halves dividend


Profits before tax of the Bridgend Group, which has interests in the
distribution and services and leisure industries, fell from Pounds 403,000
to Pounds 295,000 for the first six months of 1992.
The interim dividend is being halved to 0.2p and the final payment will
depend on the group's trading performance at year-end.
First half turnover was static at Pounds 8.18m, against Pounds 8.2m, and
earnings per share of 0.7p compared with 1.05p last time.
