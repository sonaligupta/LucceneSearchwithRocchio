FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Grainger Trust


Grainger Trust, the property company, hoisted pre-tax profits from a
restated Pounds 1.74m to Pounds 5.65m for the year to September 30. The
result included an exceptional credit of Pounds 1.9m.
Earnings were 18.9p (5.9p). A final dividend of 4.55p makes a total of 5.9p
(5.25p).
