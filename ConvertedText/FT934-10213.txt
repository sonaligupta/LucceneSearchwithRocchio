FT  06 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Serif deficit cut to Pounds 494,000


Serif, the USM-quoted specialist printer and packaging group, yesterday
reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 494,000 for the six months to June
30.
The improvement, from a deficit of Pounds 571,000 last time, was achieved on
sales down by Pounds 3.46m to Pounds 8.7m. Last year's figure, however,
included sales generated by the bingo division of Cowells, which was sold in
July 1992.
Losses arising on the sale and termination of operations totalled Pounds
215,000 (Pounds 414,000), of which Pounds 72,000 was provided for in 1992.
Interest and similar charges came to Pounds 141,000 (Pounds 151,000). Losses
per share came out at 2p (2.3p).
