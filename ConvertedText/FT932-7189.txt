FT  25 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Downiebrae requests halt to dealing


Downiebrae Holdings, the Glasgow engineering group which announced on May 21
that it was in talks that could lead to a substantial acquisition, yesterday
requested that its shares be temporarily suspended pending an announcement.
At midday - the time of the suspension - the shares were quoted at 77p.
