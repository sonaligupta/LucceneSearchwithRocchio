FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Bolton Group


Mr Ivor Goletka is not receiving any compensation for his loss of office at
Bolton Group, the property investment group.
He lost the chairmanship to Mr Mark Keegan in March 1993 and left the board
in March this year. He still holds a 4.8 per cent stake.
