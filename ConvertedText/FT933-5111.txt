FT  04 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Gains reversed


In the first three days of September the UK stock market gave up a large
part of recent weeks' gains. On the last day of August the FT-SE 100 index
reached a new intra-day peak of 3,115.1. But the index closed that day at
3,100 and dropped to 3,057.3 yesterday. Weekend, Page II; Share prices, Page
