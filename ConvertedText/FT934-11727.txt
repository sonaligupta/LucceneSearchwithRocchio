FT  29 OCT 93 / International Company News: FT in Taiwan


The Financial Times has signed an information co-operation agreement with
the China Times Group of Taiwan. Under it, all newspapers in the China Times
group will have access to editorial material from the FT. The agreement, the
first between the FT and a newspaper group in Taiwan, was formally signed at
a ceremony in Taipei yesterday.
