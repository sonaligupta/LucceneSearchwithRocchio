FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: Seafield directors face challenge


Seafield, the Dublin-based transport, warehousing, and property company,
announced yesterday that various shareholders had requested an extraordinary
general meeting both to consider the removal of Mr Brian Chilver, chairman,
and Mr Richard Hayes, another director, from the board, and to consider the
appointment of Mr Robert Cosby and Mr Anthony Stuart Wilson as directors.
