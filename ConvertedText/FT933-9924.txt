FT  06 AUG 93 / International Company News in Brief: Coco-Cola


   By REUTER


   PARIS


Coca-Cola will invest Dollars 1.5bn in eastern Europe between 1992 and 1995,
according to Mr Ralph Cooper, the chief of the company's European
operations, Reuter reports from Paris.
Mr Cooper said plants and distributions centres built in east Germany had
been a valuable starting block for the company in eastern Europe.
