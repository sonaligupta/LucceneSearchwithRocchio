FT  11 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: First Chicago offers Dollars
100m of 10-year notes


   By BARBARA DURR


   CHICAGO


FIRST Chicago, one of the mid-west's largest banks, announced that it is
offering Dollars 100m of 8 7/8 per cent subordinated notes, due on March 15
2002.
The 10-year notes are non-callable and are priced at par to yield 8 7/8 per
cent.
Proceeds from the sale will be used for general corporate purposes, the bank
said. The notes were issued through an underwriting group consisting of
Salomon Brothers and the First Boston Corporation.
