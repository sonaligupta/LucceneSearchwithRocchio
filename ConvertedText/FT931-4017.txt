FT  12 MAR 93 / International Company News: Argentine offer advisers picked


   By STEPHEN FIDLER


SG WARBURG and Lehman Brothers have been appointed as advisers on the sale
to the public of minority shareholdings in about 20 of Argentina's
recently-privatised companies - including the gas and electricity utilities,
writes Stephen Fidler.
