FT  05 MAR 94 / Airline in trouble


   By REUTER


   LUSAKA


Zambia Airways, the national airline, owes Dollars 45.5m, is virtually
bankrupt and may have to close, Mr William Harrington, transport minister,
told parliament, Reuter reports from Lusaka.
