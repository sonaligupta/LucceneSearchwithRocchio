FT  27 SEP 94 / International Company News: Kuwait bankers approve bonus


   By REUTER


   KUWAIT


The Commercial Bank of Kuwait said its net profit rose 8.4 per cent to
KD11.5m (Dollars 38.7m) in 1993 from KD10.6m in 1992, Reuter reports from
Kuwait.
It said in its 1993 annual report that net income from banking activities
rose to KD21.5m from KD21.1m in 1992.
The bank approved a dividend of 10 per cent in bonus shares worth a total of
KD6.84m.
