FT  27 MAY 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


GOLD shares were slightly higher but off the day's peaks as the bullion
price slipped back amid fears of large-scale selling overseas. The index put
on 4 to 1,860 while industrials added 7 to 4,525. The overall index lost 11
to 3,999.
