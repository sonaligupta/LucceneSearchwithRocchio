FT  28 JAN 94 / World Trade News: US steel contract for Davy


   By ANDREW BAXTER


Davy International, part of Trafalgar House of the UK, is to supply
engineering, procurement and construction management for a Dollars 165m
steel billet mill which Republic Engineered Steel is building at Canton,
Ohio. The mill will have an annual capacity of 750,000 tonnes and is due for
completion next year. Davy said the contract would provide important work
for its Pittsburgh office following a Dollars 150m coke oven project awarded
last year by Bethlehem Steel.
