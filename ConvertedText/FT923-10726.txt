FT  29 JUL 92 / International Company News: Bank Leu fails to meet forecast


   By IAN RODGER


   ZURICH


BANK LEU, a Swiss private and commercial bank within the CS Holding group,
said its operating profit in the first half was slightly below expectations,
but gave no figures.
The bank said operating income was satisfactory in the second quarter, but
operating profit was down.
Total assets at June 30 1992 eased to SFr12.98bn (Dollars 9.40bn) from
SFr13.12bn at the end of the first quarter.
