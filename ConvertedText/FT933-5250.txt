FT  04 SEP 93 / Tesco recalls lager bottles


A NEW warning over lager bottles containing glass fragments was last night
issued by Tesco.
The supermarket giant said a small number of six-bottle packs of Premium
Spanish lager sold in its stores may contain fragments of glass.
Customers with packs stamped 'LOTE 3091' stamped are asked to return them to
any Tesco store.
