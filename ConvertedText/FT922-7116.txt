FT  20 MAY 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


JOHANNESBURG eased on profit-taking while gold shares were a touch firmer as
the bullion price tested Dollars 340. The gold index rose 3 to 1,138 and the
overall index was down 6 at 3,682. The industrial index shed 7 to 4,561.
