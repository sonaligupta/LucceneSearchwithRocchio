FT  25 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Possible bid for Harrison Industries


The board of Harrison Industries said it had noted the recent rise in its
share price and stated that preliminary talks were being held with a
potential purchaser which could lead to an offer being made for the company.
The board said it had been informed that any offer from this party would
only be at a substantial discount to the market price. The shares closed
1.5p down at 6p.
