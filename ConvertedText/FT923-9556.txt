FT  04 AUG 92 / ICI unions accept 5.1% pay deal


UNIONS representing 45,000 workers at Imperial Chemicals Industries
yesterday accepted an offer which will add 5.1 per cent to pay rates.
The deal, which will be backdated to June 1, raises the possibility of ICI
workers receiving increases of more than 11 per cent this year.
