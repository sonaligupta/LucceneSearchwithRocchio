FT  03 SEP 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil


Brazilian stocks climbed by 7.1 per cent in heavy trade after Wednesday's
6.4 per cent advance, the Bovespa index finishing 733 higher at 10,994 on
the proposition that the country's biggest political party, the PMDB, would
continue to support President Itamar Franco.
