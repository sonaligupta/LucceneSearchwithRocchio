FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Courtyard Leisure


Courtyard Leisure, the USM-quoted wine bar and restaurant operator, reported
pre-tax losses reduced from Pounds 197,693 to Pounds 79,055 for the half
year to September 30.
The improvement was achieved on sales down from Pounds 979,233 to Pounds
769,913, although last year's figure included the operations of Benjamin
Stillingfleet, sold in September.
Losses per share emerged at 0.57p (1.47p).
