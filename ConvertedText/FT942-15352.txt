FT  15 APR 94 / UK Company News: Tudor shows losses of Pounds 865,000


Tudor, the USM-traded floor and wall tile distributor, incurred losses of
Pounds 865,000 pre-tax for 1993 reflecting full provision for the closure of
its crystal operation and costs of reorganising the tile business.
Turnover rose Pounds 1m to Pounds 17.7m. The deficit was up from Pounds
104,000 last time and came after closure costs of Pounds 150,000 and
exceptional charges of Pounds 243,000 (Pounds 74,000). The dividend is again
0.25p.
