FT  07 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil


Brazilian stocks rose another 5.5 per cent as domestic and foreign investors
continued to pump new money into the market on hopes that congress will
approve the government's economic plan by late January. The Bovespa index
was 2,312 higher at 44,417 at the close, with oil company Petrobras
outperforming. The index has climbed 9.3 per cent in dollar terms so far
this month.
