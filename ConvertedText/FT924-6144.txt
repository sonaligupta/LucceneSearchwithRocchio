FT  23 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Boscombe Property half time surge


With gross rental income rising 9 per cent to Pounds 434,000, Boscombe
Property lifted pre-tax profit from Pounds 183,000 to Pounds 275,000 for the
six months ended September 30 1992.
Earnings per share surged from 134.98p to 203.09p, and the interim dividend
is increased from 80p to 100p.
