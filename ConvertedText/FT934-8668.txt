FT  13 NOV 93 / Pinochet's police sentenced


   By REUTER


   SANTIAGO


A Supreme Court judge yesterday sentenced the head of Chile's feared
military secret police and his deputy during the Pinochet regime to prison
for ordering the assassination of opposition leader Orlando Letelier in
Washington in 1976, Reuter reports from Santiago. Judge Adolfo Banados
sentenced General Manuel Contreras to seven years and Brigadier Pedro
Espinoza to six.
