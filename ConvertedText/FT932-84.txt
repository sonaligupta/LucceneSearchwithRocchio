FT  30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sindall loss deepens to Pounds 4.74m


WILLIAM SINDALL, the civil engineering contractor, saw pre-tax losses deepen
from Pounds 4.19m to Pounds 4.74m in 1992.
Turnover fell to Pounds 45.4m, against Pounds 64.1m.
Losses per share were 83.3p (64.5p). After the interim was passed, a nominal
0.1p final dividend is proposed. Last time there was a total of 1.5p.
