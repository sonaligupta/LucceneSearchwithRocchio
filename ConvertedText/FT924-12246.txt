FT  21 OCT 92 / Funds into India


   By REUTER


   NEW DELHI


Foreign investment in India will rise to an annual level of at least Dollars
500m-Dollars 750m over the next four years from an estimated Dollars
200m-Dollars 300m in the current financial year, Finance Minister Manmohan
Singh said yesterday, Reuter reports from New Delhi.
