FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Merger notice for ICI sale


A MERGER notice has been issued by the director general of fair trading in
respect of the proposed acquisition of Imperial Chemical Industries' dry ice
business, which operates under the Drikold name, by Norsk Hydro.
The period for consideration of the notice expires on June 22 and
representations about the purchase may be made by June 8.
