FT  24 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea


TEA There was a strong demand,reports the Tea Broker's Association. Bright
liquoring East Africans were often 4-8p dearer with quality, while coloury
mediums were also well supported and generally 2-4p higher. The remainder
ruled firm except the plainest, which tended easier. Offshore; there was
good general demand in the cif section with selected brighter lines dearer,
others firm and only one container unsold. Fob teas, all from Mauritius,
were neglected. The highest price realised this week was 200p for a Rwanda
pd. Quotations: quality 160p, good medium 114p, medium 95p, low medium 80p
