FT  30 JAN 92 / The Bush Budget: Main Points of Budget


Economic recovery to begin in spring; 3 per cent growth over medium term.
Budget deficit to rise to Dollars 400bn this year and not dip much below
Dollars 200bn over next five years.
Change in withholding tax rules to provide Dollars 25bn short-term stimulus.
Increase in personal tax exemption of Dollars 500 per child from this
October.
Proposed cap to control spending on mandatory 'entitlement' programmes.
Defence budget cut a further Dollars 50bn over next five years; big weapons
programmes cancelled.
