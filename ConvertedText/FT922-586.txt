FT  26 JUN 92 / Growth of cash in circulation slows


BANK OF England figures yesterday disclosed a sharp slowdown in the growth
of banknotes and coins in circulation during June.
Notes in circulation increased by only 0.5 per cent in the week to June 24
compared with the same week last year after a 0.8 per cent annual increase
in the previous week.
