FT  23 JUL 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices


Demand for pepper improved further during the week and most suppliers
immediately increased their prices or even withdrew from the market, reports
Man Producten. Stock positions in consuming markets are now extremely low
and the availability of pepper in a most origin countries is also very
limited on the basis of the present price levels. Old crop stocks in hands
of holders in Malaysia and Brazil are held very tightly. Spot black pepper
is now quoted at about USDollars 1,900 a tonne and white at Dollars 3,100.
In the US market the spot black pepper price has reached the highest level
for some years a 92 cents a pound.
