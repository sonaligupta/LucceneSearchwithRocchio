FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Kelt Energy


KELT ENERGY: As a result of the subscription and open offer Kelt will issue
12.5m new ordinary shares of which CP International Securities will
subscribe for 12.14m, representing 14.4 per cent of the company's enlarged
share capital. Applications under the open offer were received in respect of
353,154 new ordinary shares.
