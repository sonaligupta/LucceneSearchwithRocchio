FT  04 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Latin American net assets improve


Net asset value per share of the Latin American Investment Trust stood at
189 cents at June 30. That compared with 185 cents 12 months earlier and
with 183 cents at the December 31 1992 year end.
For the six months to end-June the trust swung from an attributable deficit
of Dollars 399,000 to revenue of Dollars 804,000 (Pounds 532,000). Earnings
per share emerged at 1.07 cents (losses 0.53 cents).
