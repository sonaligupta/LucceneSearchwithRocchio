FT  10 JAN 92 / BCCI guilty plea to racketeering


   By REUTER


   WASHINGTON


THE Bank of Credit and Commerce International yesterday formally pleaded
guilty to federal charges of racketeering, as part of a proposed
plea-bargain under which it will forfeit a record Dollars 550m (Pounds 302m)
in seized assets, Reuter reports from Washington.
Judge Joyce Hens Green said her decision on whether to approve or reject the
controversial deal would be announced on January 24.
