FT  26 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System


In a week where Scandinavian currencies briefly leapt into the limelight
after general elections in Sweden and Denmark, the Danish krone displaced
the escudo from sixth place in the EMS grid. The Danish currency's spread
against the peseta, the weakest currency in the system, rose from 1.5 per
cent to 1.67 per cent. Currencies, Page 33
