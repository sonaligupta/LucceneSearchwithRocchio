FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: London Secs in asset talks with Nu-Swift


London Securities, the property investment company, has begun preliminary
talks on acquiring certain of Nu-Swift's property assets.
Nu-Swift, which holds a 29.2 per cent stake in London Securities, provides
fire protection equipment and services and cleaning and maintenance
services.
