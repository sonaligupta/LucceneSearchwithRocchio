FT  18 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): Morgan Crucible - Other Companies


The speciality materials group, is forecast to report pre-tax profits up
from Pounds 61.3m to between Pounds 63m and Pounds 65m when it releases its
full year figures today.
The thermal ceramics business will have suffered badly from the recession in
continental Europe but the group figures will be boosted by currency
movements, recovery in the US and growth in east Asia. A sparkling
performance is expected from Holt Lloyd, the car carebusiness the group has
been planning to sell.
