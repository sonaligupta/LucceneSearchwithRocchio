FT  08 NOV 94 / Scottish poll shows Labour fall


Labour support in Scotland has continued to fall, a System Three poll in
today's Glasgow Herald newspaper shows. The poll puts Labour on 47 per cent,
down from 51 per cent last month and 55 per cent in September.
The Scottish National party is down 2 points to 27 per cent while the
Conservatives are up 2 points to 13 per cent and the Liberal Democrats up 1
point to 10 per cent. The Scottish Green party has 3 per cent.
