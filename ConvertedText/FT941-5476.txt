FT  05 MAR 94 / Eastleigh Tories choose candidate


The Conservatives have chosen Mr Stephen Reid, leader of Basingstoke and
Deane Borough Council, to fight the Eastleigh by-election in Hampshire
caused by the death of Mr Stephen Milligan.
Mr Reid will be defending a Conservative majority of 17,702 over the Liberal
Democrats.
