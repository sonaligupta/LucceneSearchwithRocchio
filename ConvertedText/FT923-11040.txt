FT  27 JUL 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Bombs hit power line


Two bombs knocked out the main Franco-Spanish electricity line at Belesta in
the eastern Pyrenees during the opening ceremony at the weekend, French
police said yesterday.
The Spanish Basque separatist organisation ETA, which had vowed to disrupt
the Games, is suspected, said police.
