FT  27 JUN 92 / Newcastle quay deal signed


THE AGREEMENT for the Pounds 183m development of Newcastle upon Tyne's East
Quayside was signed yesterday by Mr John Redwood, inner-cities minister.
The deal is between Tyne and Wear Development Corporation and Newcastle
Quayside Developments, a wholly owned subsidiary of Amec, the construction,
engineering and development group.
