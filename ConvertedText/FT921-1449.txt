FT  24 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Wife cleared of murder


June Scotland, 52, who battered her husband to death with a rolling pin and
buried him in the back garden at their home in Stevenage, Herts, was
acquitted of murder and sentenced to two years' probation. She admitted
manslaughter.
