FT  01 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Anorexic girl can be treated


The Court of Appeal ruled that a 16-year-old anorexic girl can be treated
for her illness against her wishes. The girl, who weighs 5st 7lb, is in the
care of a local authority which wants to send her to a unit specialising in
eating disorders.
