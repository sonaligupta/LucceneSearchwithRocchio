FT  08 NOV 94 / Indonesia state budget set to grow


   By REUTER


   JAKARTA


Indonesia's state budget is targeted to grow 10-15 per cent in the 1995-96
fiscal year, Mr M. Muhammad, finance minister, said yesterday. Indonesia's
budget in the current fiscal year is Dollars 32bn (Pounds 20bn), up 11.1 per
cent on last year.
