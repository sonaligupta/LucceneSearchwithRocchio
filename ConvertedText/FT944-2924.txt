FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: TBI Pounds 24m buy


TBI, the property group, is acquiring a Pounds 24.1m portfolio of 11
investment properties. The net price, after assumption of Pounds 12.9m of
bank debt, is Pounds 11.2m.
The principal vendor is Mr Peter Thomas, brother of Mr Stanley Thomas, TBI's
deputy chairman who takes over as chairman on January 1.
The consideration is being met by the issue of 28m new shares at 40p.
