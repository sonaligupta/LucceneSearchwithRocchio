FT  23 APR 93 / World News In Brief: Brutality 'rife' at institution


Boredom and brutality were rife at Feltham Young Offenders' Institution in
west London, where four inmates, including a 15-year-old boy, committed
suicide in seven months, according to a report commissioned by the Howard
League for Penal Reform.
