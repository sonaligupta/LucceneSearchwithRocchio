FT944-6011

FT  01 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Thai Airways International - Contracts and
ventures


   By REUTER


   BANGKOK


Thai Airways International will take delivery of three new Airbus Industrie
A330-300s in December after Airbus delayed delivery of the aircraft, the
first of seven on order, for technical reasons earlier this year. Thai also
has an option to buy an eighth aircraft.
