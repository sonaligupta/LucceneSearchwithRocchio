FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Holmes Protection


Holmes Protection, the US security group quoted in the UK, reported pre-tax
profits of Dollars 1m (Pounds 600,000) for the nine months to September 30,
compared with Dollars 239,000.
Turnover was down 4 per cent to Dollars 38.8m (Dollars 40.4m). The board
attributed the increase in profits on lower revenue to the Dollars 1.33m
reduction in operating costs. Earnings per share came out at 1.9 cents (0.2
cents).
