FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Berkeley Eastoak Investments


BERKELEY GROUP associate, Berkeley Eastoak Investments, has sold part of its
commercial investment property portfolio in two separate transactions for
Pounds 90m cash. The properties, which were mainly out-of-town retail
investments, were acquired at a cost of Pounds 62m.
