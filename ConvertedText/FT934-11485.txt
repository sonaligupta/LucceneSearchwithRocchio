FT  30 OCT 93 / Central Americans in trade pact


   By DAVID SCANLAN


   SAN JOSE, COSTA RICA


The pending North American Free Trade Agreement has helped push central
American nations into a trade pact of their own, David Scanlan reports from
San Jose, Costa Rica. Signatories are Costa Rica, Nicaragua, Honduras, El
Salvador and Guatemala.
