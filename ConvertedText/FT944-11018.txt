FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Filtronic Comtek


Filtronic Comtek, the mobile telecommunications equipment manufacturer, is
to build two new factories.
The first is on a 2.5 acre site acquired from Salts Estates in Yorkshire for
Pounds 615,000; the second is a 60,000 sq ft factory on a site in Maryland,
US, which is being acquired for Dollars 525,000 (Pounds 326,000).
Filtronic came to the market at the beginning of October. The new site
announcements mark the inception of the investment and expansion promised
then.
