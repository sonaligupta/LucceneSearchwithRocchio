FT  06 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: S. G. Warburg


S. G. Warburg, the merchant bank, is expected to report on Tuesday a jump in
interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 127m from Pounds 51.2m. Hambros' interims
on Wednesday will be less impressive with pre-tax profits at about Pounds
35m (Pounds 25m).
