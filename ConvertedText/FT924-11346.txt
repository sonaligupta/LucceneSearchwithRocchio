FT  27 OCT 92 / World News In Brief: Aid for Kurds


Overseas development minister Baroness Chalker announced the provision of a
further Pounds 2.6m (Dollars 4.2m) for the Kurds of northern Iraq, bringing
the UK's humanitarian aid for Iraqi civilians since 1991 to more than Pounds
55m.
