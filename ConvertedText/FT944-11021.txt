FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: BPP acquisition


BPP Holdings, the education and training group, has acquired 51 per cent of
Hazell Carr Training for an initial Pounds 1.44m, split as to Pounds 438,000
in cash and Pounds 1m in loan notes.
An additional sum capped at Pounds 1.7m, to be met by the issue of loan
notes, is also payable.
HCT publishes study material and provides face to face tuition courses and
marking services to students studying for examinations of the Institute of
Actuaries and the Faculty of Actuaries. The business operated as a
partnership until October 28.
