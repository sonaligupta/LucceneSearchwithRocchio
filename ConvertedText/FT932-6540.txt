FT  27 MAY 93 / New premier for Bavaria


   By QUENTIN PEEL


   BONN


Edmund Stoiber, the Bavarian interior minister, was yesterday nominated by
the ruling Christian Social Union group to take over as state premier, after
Mr Max Streibl announced his long-awaited resignation, writes Quentin Peel
from Bonn. Mr Streibl had admitted accepting free holidays from a business
friend.
