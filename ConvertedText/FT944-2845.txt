FT  15 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Tapie bankrupt


Any presidential ambitions of flamboyant French politician Bernard Tapie
were dealt a severe blow yesterday when he and his wife were declared
personally bankrupt. Tapie, who has said he does not plan stand for the
French presidency next year, is likely to appeal.
