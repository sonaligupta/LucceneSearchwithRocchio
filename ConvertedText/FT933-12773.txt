FT  22 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Yeoman net assets improve to 400p


Net asset value of the split-capital Yeoman Investment Trust amounted to
400.4p per capital share at June 30, up from 343.4p at the December year-end
and 161.2p at end-June 1992.
The trust, managed by Abbey Life Investment Services, reported net revenue
of Pounds 2.03m (Pounds 1.58m) for the six months to June 30, equivalent to
earnings of 8.26p (6.43p) per income share.
The interim dividend is unchanged at 5.5p.
