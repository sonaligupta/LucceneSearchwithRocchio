FT  23 APR 93 / UK Company News: Yorklyde share price jumps 25p


Shares in Yorklyde jumped 25p to 408p yesterday as the fine woollen and
worsted cloth maker announced a 45 per cent growth in pre-tax profits and a
1-for-1 scrip issue.
On turnover ahead 18 per cent at Pounds 13.3m (Pounds 11.3m) profits for the
year to January 31 rose from Pounds 1.52m to Pounds 2.2m.
Earnings improved to 31.2p (21.2p). The final dividend is 6.4p for a total
of 10.4p (9.7p).
