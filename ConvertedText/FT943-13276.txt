FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Paramount


In addition to the firm placing of 38.4m new ordinary shares and irrevocable
undertakings in respect of 4.72m shares, acceptances to rights issue have
been received in respect of a further 3.57m (15.75 per cent of the balance
in issue). The remaining 19.1m (28.98 per cent) of the issue has been taken
up by sub-underwriters.
