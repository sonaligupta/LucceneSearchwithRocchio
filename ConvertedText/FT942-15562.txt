FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: Exceptionals hold Mid-States


Pre-tax profits of Mid-States, the US automotive parts distributor quoted on
the USM, fell from Pounds 4.21m to Pounds 3.47m in 1993 after accounting for
exceptional provisions of Pounds 2.4m.
Turnover rose by Pounds 13m to Pounds 63.1m. Earnings per share emerged at
5.3p (7.3p). There is again no final dividend. The company hopes to raise
between Pounds 13.6m and Pounds 17m via a placing of shares on Nasdaq and at
the same time moving to a full listing in London.
It has also acquired two businesses in Mississippi for a total of Dollars
2.8m (Pounds 1.9m) cash.
