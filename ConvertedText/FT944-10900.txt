FT  09 NOV 94 / France detains Islamic militants


French police launched a crackdown on suspected Islamic militants yesterday,
seizing a cache of firearms and detaining more than 90 people, including
alleged supporters of an Algerian guerrilla organisation. One of the
suspects is pictured being taken into Paris police headquarters.
