FT  05 MAR 94 / Trade pact sealed


   By DAVID SCANLAN


   SAN JOSE


Mexico and Costa Rica have signed a free trade accord after three years of
negotiations, writes David Scanlan in San Jose. More than 8,300 products
will be traded tariff-free immediately, with others to follow over 10 years.
