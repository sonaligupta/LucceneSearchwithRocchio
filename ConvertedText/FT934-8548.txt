FT  15 NOV 93 / Observer: Iron lady


Last Thursday's issue of the United Arab Emirates' Khaleej Times carried a
report of the BBC's documentary on the downfall of Lady Thatcher. The paper
notes that in the months leading up to her shock resignation she treated
some ministers like doormats and was showing 'signs of metal fatigue'.
