FT  06 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Hillsborough plea rejected


The High Court refused a request from the families of six victims of the
Hillsborough football stadium disaster for a new inquest into their deaths.
Ninety-six people were crushed to death in April 1989 at Sheffield
Wednesday's ground.
