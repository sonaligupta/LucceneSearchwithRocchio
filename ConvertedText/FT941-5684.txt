FT  04 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Reward demanded over Munch painting


Norway's culture minister said a man had offered to arrange the return of
Edvard Munch's painting 'The Scream', stolen last month from the National
Gallery in Oslo, for a reward of more than Dollars 1m.
