FT  20 APR 94 / World News in Brief: King awarded Dollars 3.8m


A Los Angeles jury awarded Dollars 3.8m (Pounds 2.6m) in compensatory
damages to Rodney King, the black motorist whose beating by white police
ultimately led to the city's 1992 riots. Punitive damages have yet to be
decided.
