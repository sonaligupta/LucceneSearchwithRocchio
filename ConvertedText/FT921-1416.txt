FT  24 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Swing to Labour


A MORI poll covering the 10 key Tory marginal seats in the Yorkshire region
shows a significant 8.5 per cent swing to Labour since the 1987 general
election.
The survey, for Yorkshire Television, revealed the most urgent issue in the
campaign, according to those interviewed, was the National Health Service,
followed by education then jobs. Taxes and the poll tax were low priorities.
Mori interviewed 1,059 adults.
