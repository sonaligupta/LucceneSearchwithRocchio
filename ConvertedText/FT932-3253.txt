FT  15 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Wintrust


Pre-tax profits at Wintrust, the merchant banking group, fell from Pounds
3.02m to Pounds 2.02m in the year to end-March.
The ratio of loans and advances to capital improved from 1.7:1 to less than
1.4:1 and the capital assets ratio moved from 36 per cent to 42 per cent.
Earnings per share were 11.19p (19.54p) basic and 12.2p (19.16p) fully
diluted. The final dividend is 6.3p for an unchanged total of 9.3p.
