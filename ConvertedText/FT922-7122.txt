FT  20 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute


   COTTON Liverpool


Spot and shipment sales for the week ended 15 May amounted to 510 tonnes
against 146 tonnes in the previous week. Sales were moderate with deals
mainly ocurring in West African and Australian growths.
