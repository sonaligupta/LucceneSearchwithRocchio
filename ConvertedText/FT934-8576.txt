FT  15 NOV 93 / Construction Contracts: Kier London takes on health refit


KIER LONDON, part of the employee-owned Kier Group, has secured two health
service contracts worth Pounds 2.9m.
At Lewisham Hospital, work has started on the Pounds 450,000 conversion of
doctors' quarters to form an outpatients department and offices for Lewisham
Hospital NHS Trust.
Kier is also converting a dental hospital at Camden to provide clinics,
offices and ancillary accommodation for Bloomsbury and Islington Health
Authority.
