FT  26 OCT 92 / International Company News: Jarvis falls to losses of Pounds
1.72m


JARVIS, the construction and property group, incurred a pre-tax loss of
Pounds 1.72m for the half year ended June 30 and is omitting its interim
dividend.
For the same period of the previous year the company returned profits of
Pounds 71,000 and paid a 0.25p dividend.
Turnover fell to Pounds 48m (Pounds 61m) and losses per share worked through
at 9.5p (earnings 0.3p).
The directors said a further loss would be incurred in the second half but
added that it would be at a lower level than for the opening six months.
