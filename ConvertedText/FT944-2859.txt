FT  15 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


Gold shares rallied from the day's lows in afternoon trade to end mildly
softer after bullion rose to Dollars 379 an ounce, but other stocks remained
depressed by a lack of buyers.
The overall index relinquished 22.5 at 5,637.4, industrials fell 43.5 to
6,753.9 and golds slipped 7.3 to 1,891.9.
