FT  04 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: BES sponsors launch three schemes -
At a Glance


Business Expansion Scheme sponsors, Neill Clerk have launched new schemes.
Pembroke Residences offers a cash-backed fixed exit price after five years
of 122p for every 100p invested, equating to 14 per cent per annum for a
higher-rate taxpayer.
The cash-backed fixed return for Oxford Isis Residences is 122p or 60p plus
2.5 p for every 1 per cent rise in the FT-SE 100 index, with a lock-in at 25
per cent growth. Second Greenwich Student Halls pays a fixed return of 123p
or 75p plus 1p for every 1 per cent rise in the index with lock-ins at 25
per cent, 50 per cent and 75 per cent rises, plus a 2p bonus. More BES, page
