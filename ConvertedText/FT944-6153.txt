FT  30 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust Lloyd's turns in Pounds 1.06m


Maiden interim pre-tax profits at Abtrust Lloyd's Insurance Trust were
Pounds 1.06m for the 10 months to September 30 and Pounds 557,000 for the
six months to the same date.
Gross revenue was Pounds 1.51m for the 10 months with franked investment
income of Pounds 1.12m, and unfranked investment income of Pounds 252,000.
Net asset value per share was 84.15p. Earnings were 2.88p per share and the
interim dividend is 2.5p.
