FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Ossory sells retail park for Pounds 10.5m


Ossory Estates has sold the South Wirral Retail Park in Bromborough,
Cheshire, to Boots for Pounds 10.5m at a net yield of 9.5 per cent.
Gross rental income from the fully let park - from tenants such as Halfords,
Dixons, Do-it-all and Texas Homecare which have 25-year leases from 1990 -
exceeds Pounds 1m per year.
