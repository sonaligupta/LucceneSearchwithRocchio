FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Enterprise


At the June 30 1993 year end the net asset value per share of Fleming
Enterprise Investment Trust stood at 199.96p, against 170.7p six months
earlier and 163.2p a year ago.
Net revenue was Pounds 1.99m (Pounds 1.78m) for earnings per share of 4.99p
(4.46p).
The final dividend is 3.4p for a total of 4.7p (4.5p).
