FT  05 JUN 92 / Demomstrators clash with Tokyo police


About 500 demonstrators clash with police outside the Diet building in Tokyo
last night in a protest against a bill to allow Japanese troops to act as
United Nations peace-keepers. The opposition Socialist party forced repeated
delays in a marathon debate on the Peace-keeping Operations Bill.
