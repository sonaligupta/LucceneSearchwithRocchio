FT  23 JUL 93 / People: Derek Edwards


Derek Edwards, who was appointed chairman of Bridon in February and of
Marling Industries only last month, suffered a heart attack and died on July
21. He was 62. He joined Pillar Holdings in 1961 and became a main board
director when the company was taken over by RTZ in 1970. Edwards was a
non-executive director of National Express and Henlys Group, a past council
member of the CBI, a member of the Gloucestershire Health Authority and a
member of the court of Bristol University.
