FT  01 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Debenham Tewson hit by recession


Pre-tax profits of Debenham Tewson & Chinnocks Holdings, the property
adviser, fell from Pounds 4.06m to Pounds 1.59m for the year to April 30.
Turnover declined to Pounds 34.8m (Pounds 40.8m) with consultancy accounting
for 77 per cent of the total.
Earnings per share fell to 2.76p (7.57p) and the proposed final dividend is
cut to 1.5p (3.6p) for a 2.5p (6p) total.
