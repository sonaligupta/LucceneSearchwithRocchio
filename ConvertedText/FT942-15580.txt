FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: N American Small


Net asset value per share of the North Atlantic Smaller Companies Investment
Trust improved from a restated 206p to 264p over the 12 months to January
31.
The figure was based on loan stock being converted and options unconverted.
Fully diluted net assets rose from 194p to 256p.
Available revenue declined to Pounds 120,000 (Pounds 243,000), equal to
earnings of 1.38p (2.28p). The single dividend is omitted - 1.2p was paid
previously.
