FT  11 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): The Savoy Hotel - Other companies


The group which includes the Connaught and Claridge's, as well as the Savoy,
is expected to report little better than break-even figures in its annual
results on Wednesday, compared with a loss of Pounds 1.43m in 1992. The
dividend on the A shares is expected to be held at 7p.
