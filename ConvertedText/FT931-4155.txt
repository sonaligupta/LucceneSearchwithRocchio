FT  12 MAR 93 / NCP trial jury retires


THE JURY in the National Car Parks industrial espionage trial retired
yesterday and spent the night in a hotel after failing to reach verdicts.
Mr Gordon Layton, the chief executive of NCP, and Mr Simon Hewitt, a former
manager of KAS, the private security firm, both deny conspiring to defraud
Europarks, a rival car parking business.
