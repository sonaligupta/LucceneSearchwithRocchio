FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Benchmark


A stronger performance by its overseas division enabled Benchmark Group, the
property investor, to swing from losses of Pounds 12.18m to profits of
Pounds 77,000 pre-tax for the year to June 30.
Earnings per share emerged at 0.048p (losses 7.725p).
Turnover was little changed at Pounds 16.3m although last time's figure of
Pounds 16.7m included Pounds 15.7m from discontinued operations. The 1992-93
results were prepared in accordance with FRS 3.
