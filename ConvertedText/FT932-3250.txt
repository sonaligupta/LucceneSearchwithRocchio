FT  15 JUN 93 / UK Company News: FKI acquisition


FKI is buying the Forge France Group of companies, a French lifting
components company, for FFr29m cash (Pounds 3.5m). FKI has also agreed to
purchase further inventories over the period up to May 1994 at valuation
following a stock take.
