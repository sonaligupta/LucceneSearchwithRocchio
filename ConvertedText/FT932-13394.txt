FT  23 APR 93 / Observer: Open question


Who better to give an object lesson in open government than Britain's
Whitehall mandarins' trade union, officially known as the First Division
Association? Delegates at its conference will be debating a resolution
suggesting that civil servants ought to set an example for the rest of the
populace by declaring whether they are freemasons.
