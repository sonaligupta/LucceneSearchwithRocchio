FT  13 APR 94 / Children of 11 working at factory


Children as young as 11 were found working in a backstreet factory in
Preston, Lancashire, making fake designer jeans, trading standards officers
said yesterday.
The operation was discovered in raids on two units in an old mill at the
weekend. Lancashire trading standards officer Mr Jim Potts said at least six
children aged between 11 and 14 were found at the factory.
