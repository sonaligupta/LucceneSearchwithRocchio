FT  01 JUL 92 / Telford expansion


NEC, THE Japanese electronics group, plans to produce mobile phones for the
new panEuropean cellular network early next year at its plant in Telford,
Shropshire.
The plant already manufactures the current generation of analogue mobile
phones for the British, Italian and Spanish markets.
