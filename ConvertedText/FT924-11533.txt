FT  26 OCT 92 / Construction Contracts: Glyndebourne project


AMEC group company MATTHEW HALL has been awarded a Pounds 2.5m contract for
building services work on the new Glyndebourne Opera House in Sussex.
Working through Bovis Construction for Glyndebourne Productions, the company
has been appointed to install, commission and test the building's
mechanical, electrical, public health and fire systems.
The contract forms part of a major construction programme to provide larger
and improved facilities for the opera company.
The architect is Michael Hopkins & Partners and the structural, mechanical
and electrical consultant is Ove Arup.
