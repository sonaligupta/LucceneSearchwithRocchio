FT  06 NOV 93 / UK Company News: FT-SE Actuaries seminar


The FT-SE Actuaries Classification Committee is holding seminars for
professional investors to discuss the recently announced changes in the
classification of UK equities.
The first seminar will be held at the George Intercontinental Hotel in
Edinburgh on November 9. A London seminar will be held on December 7 at the
Financial Times.
Admission is free. Those interested in attending are asked to contact the
Indices Help Desk at the London Stock Exchange on 071-797-4400.
