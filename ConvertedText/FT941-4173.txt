FT  12 MAR 94 / Eurotunnel service for coaches delayed


Channel tunnel shuttle rail services, yesterday announced a delay of up to
six months in its plans to carry coaches, camper vans and caravans.
It will not carry larger vehicles on its car shuttles until the first
quarter of 1995 to enable it to devote all its capacity to cars in the first
few months. Larger vehicle service had previously been due to start in
September.
Eurotunnel also revealed a month's delay in the publication date of its
rights issue prospectus. The prospectus, expected to seek to raise about
Pounds 500m, is now due in early June.
