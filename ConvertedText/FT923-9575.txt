FT  04 AUG 92 / Japanese envoy for Cambodia


   By REUTER


   TOKYO


Japan is sending a military envoy to Cambodia this week to pave the way for
the arrival of the first contingent of peace-keeping troops, a Defence
Agency official said yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo.
Mr Shigeru Hatakeyama, director-general of the agency's Defence Policy
Bureau, will leave today for a six-day visit.
