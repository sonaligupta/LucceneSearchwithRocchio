FT  22 APR 93 / French premier to hold talks with German Chancellor Kohl


   By JUDY DEMPSEY


   BERLIN


Prime Minister Edouard Balludur of France will today hold talks with
Chancellor Helmut Kohl of Germany, aimed at clarifying, among other things,
what role and status an independent bank should play in France, writes Judy
Dempsey in Berlin.
