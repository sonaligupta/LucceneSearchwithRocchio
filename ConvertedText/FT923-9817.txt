FT  03 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Enterprise Computer


Enterprise Computer Holdings, a supplier of second user IBM mainframe
computers in Europe, returned pre-tax losses of Pounds 8.33m for the 15
months to March 31. In the previous 12 months profits were Pounds 7.53m
restated.
Turnover fell from Pounds 183.1m to Pounds 157.9m. There were exceptional
provisions of Pounds 5.33m and extraordinary charges of Pounds 22m (Pounds
32.1m).
Losses per share emerged at 9.8p (7.4p earnings). There is no final
dividend, which makes the total for the period 1.25p (2.5p).
