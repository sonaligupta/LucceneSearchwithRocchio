FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Unilever


UNILEVER has completed the acquisition of the Bertolli edible oil business
from Cirio-Bertolli-De Rica. The Bertolli business comprises its olive oil
brand, the production and marketing operations in Italy and sales operations
abroad.
