FT  30 APR 94 / UK Company News: Daks Simpson surges to Pounds 5.22m


From turnover up from Pounds 65.5m to Pounds 71.7m Daks Simpson Group, the
clothing concern subsidiary of Sankyo Seiko of Japan, pushed pre-tax profits
up from Pounds 1.4m to Pounds 5.22m for the year ended January 31 1994.
Earnings per share were much higher at 46.66p against 2.86p.
