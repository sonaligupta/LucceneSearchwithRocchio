FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Labyrinth Group


Labyrinth Group, OTC-traded publisher, announced interim pre-tax profits to
June 30 of Pounds 601,000, compared with losses of Pounds 153,000. Turnover
rose from Pounds 823,000 to Pounds 4.17m.
