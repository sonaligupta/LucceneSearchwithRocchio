FT  16 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Obscene caller freed


Bank clerk Christopher Gelder, who was jailed for grievous bodily harm after
making obscene telephone calls, was freed by the Court of Appeal. The law
lords held that jurors had been misdirected, but did not rule on whether
bodily harm could be inflicted over the telephone.
