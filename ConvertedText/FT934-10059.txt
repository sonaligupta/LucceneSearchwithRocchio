FT  08 NOV 93 / EC car sales drop sharply


   By REUTER


   BRUSSELS


New car registrations within the European Community fell by 13.9 per cent in
October compared with the same month last year, the European Automobile
Manufacturers Association (ACEA) said yeterday, Reuter reports from
Brussels.
The latest registration figures confirmed that the market was in deep
recession, an ACEA spokesman said.
