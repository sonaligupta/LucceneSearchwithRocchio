FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Mountview Estates


Pre-tax profits of Mountview Estates, the property trading group, showed a
45 per cent advance from Pounds 3.37m to Pounds 4.9m in the six months to
end-September. Turnover improved by some Pounds 2m to Pounds 9.01m.
Earnings came out at 71.6p (49.3p) and the interim dividend is raised by 2p
to 12p.
