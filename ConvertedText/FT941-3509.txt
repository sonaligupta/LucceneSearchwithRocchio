FT  16 MAR 94 / FT wins four design awards


The Financial Times has won four awards from the Society of Newspaper Design
for outstanding design and illustration for work produced in 1993. James
Ferguson won a bronze medal for a Weekend FT illustration. The Weekend FT
also won Awards of Excellence for its fashion pages and overall design and
Kevin Hauff won an Award of excellence for his illustration for the Serious
Investor supplement.
The SND, based in the US, had more than 7,000 entries from newspapers
worldwide.
