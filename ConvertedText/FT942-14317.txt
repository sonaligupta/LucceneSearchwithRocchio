FT  21 APR 94 / World Trade News: Oil production ship ordered


   By KAREN FOSSLI


   OSLO


Smedvig, a Norwegian drilling and offshore oil and gas technology group, has
contracted Singapore-based Far East Levingston Shipbuilding to build it a
double-hulled oil production ship at a cost of Dollars 200m (Pounds 137m)
for delivery in the second quarter of 1996, Karen Fossli writes from Oslo.
