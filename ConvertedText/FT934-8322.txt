FT  16 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Sharp downturn at Colorgen


Profits before tax of Colorgen, the US-based colour matching specialist
traded on the USM, fell from Dollars 928,000 to Dollars 407,000 (Pounds
273,000) for the year to end-June. Income from operations at Dollars 1.04m
(Dollars 921,000) was in line with forecasts in August.
Sales improved to Dollars 14.2m (Dollars 11m) but operating costs rose to
Dollars 5.92m (Dollars 4.64m). Earnings were 1 cent (5.5 cents).
