FT  12 MAR 93 / Cuts to affect 140 Tube stations


LONDON Underground yesterday detailed the effects of the spending cuts
imposed in the 1992 Autumn Statement.
It wrote to 59 London MPs, 11 MEPs, 31 borough councils, three county
councils and four district councils naming 140 stations that would be
adversely affected by cuts in its investment programme.
