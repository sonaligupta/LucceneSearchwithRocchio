FT  28 JUL 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: UK in rowing semi-final


British rowing star Steve Redgrave exuded Olympic class yesterday, cruising
into the semi-finals of the coxless pairs with Matthew Pinsent. The Britons
beat Slovenian rivals Iztok Cop and Denis Zvegalj with the day's fastest
time.
