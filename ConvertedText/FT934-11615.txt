FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Neepsend


Improved manufacturing efficiency and reduced costs were behind the jump in
pre-tax profits from Pounds 102,000 to Pounds 472,000 at Neepsend, the
engineering group, in the half year to September 30.
Turnover of continuing operations advanced to Pounds 8.63m (Pounds 7.86m).
An increased dividend of 0.5p (0.25p) is payable from earnings of 1.52p
(0.42p). The shares closed 6p up at 37p.
