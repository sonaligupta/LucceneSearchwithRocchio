FT  13 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Labour MP Bob Cryer killed in car crash


Bob Cryer, Labour MP for Bradford South, was killed and his wife was
slightly hurt when their Rover car overturned on the MI near Watford. Mr
Cryer, 59, described by MPs as a committed Socialist and champion of the
under-privileged, was at the forefront of all big Labour campaigns. He
backed fullscale nationalisation and was a fierce anti-nuclear and
anti-Europe campaigner. Obituary. Page 11
