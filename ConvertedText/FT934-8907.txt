FT  12 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico


MEXICO hit a new high on renewed confidence that Nafta will gain US approval
next week. Rises were later pared by profit-taking, leaving the IPC index a
net 21.02 up at 2,056.35, after 2,100. Volume was a heavy 216m shares and
the most active stock was Telefonos de Mexico.
