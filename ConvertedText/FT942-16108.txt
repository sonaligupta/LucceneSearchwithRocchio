FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Black (Peter) Holdings


BLACK (PETER) Holdings, the supplier of personal care, footwear and
accessories, has sold its 13 factory shops to their existing management for
Pounds 3.5m. Proceeds will be used to fund the group's continuing investment
in core manufacturing businesses.
