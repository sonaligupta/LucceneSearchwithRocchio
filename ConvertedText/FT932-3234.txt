FT  15 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Millwall in cash injection talks


Millwall Holdings, the holding company of the First Division football club,
said yesterday that it was involved in preliminary discussions concerning a
cash injection.
The USM-quoted group raised some Pounds 3.2m via a rights issue in April to
ensure completion of a new stadium at Senegal Fields, south London.
The shares rose 1p to 5 3/4p.
