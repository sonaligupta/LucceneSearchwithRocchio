FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical


Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust reported net assets of 47.8p per capital
share as at June 30, up from 35.8p a year earlier.
Figures per income share were 4.9p (3.7p) and per zero dividend preference
share 64.1p (57.4p).
Net revenue for the three months to end-June was Pounds 249,071 (Pounds
269,269) for earnings of 3.1p (3.4p) per income share.
The first interim dividend is maintained at 2.25p.
