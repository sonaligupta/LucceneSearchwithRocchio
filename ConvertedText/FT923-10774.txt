FT  29 JUL 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Unified lead in pentathlon


Eduard Zenovka of the Unified Team moved into first place in the modern
pentathlon after the cross-country running, the fourth of five disciplines.
Zenovka was third in the 4,000m in sweltering heat. Manuel Barroso of
Portugal won the event.
The Unified Team led the team competition with 13,168 points. Poland was
second with 12,996, Hungary third (12,981) and France fourth (12,758).
