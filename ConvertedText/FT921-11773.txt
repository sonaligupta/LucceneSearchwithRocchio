FT  30 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy US acquisition


Cairn Energy's US subsidiary, Cairn Energy (USA), has paid Dollars 1.2m cash
for a 64 per cent controlling stake in Omni Exploration, a publicly quoted
US oil and gas company.
Omni has interests in more than 400 wells, primarily in Texas, and operates
some 200 wells from its operations office in Pampa, Texas.
The company's proved reserves are 1.6m barrels of oil and 26.3bn cu ft of
gas. The operation adds production of about 200 barrels of oil and 1.4m cu
ft of gas per day to the group.
