FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Creston cuts loss to Pounds 57,000 midway


Creston, the construction components group, reduced its pre-tax losses from
Pounds 481,000 to Pounds 57,000 in the half year to December 31. The result
was achieved by contracting the level of trading by the main subsidiary.
Turnover was Pounds 3.46m (Pounds 5.16m). Losses per share were 0.25p
(2.39p).
