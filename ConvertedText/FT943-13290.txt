FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Matthew Clark buys rest of FW Francis


Matthew Clark, the drinks group, has, through its Freetraders subsidiary,
acquired the 49.9 per cent minority holding in FW Francis (Freetraders) -
which trades under the Francis of Guildford banner - for Pounds 1.53m cash.
Concurrent with the deal the vendors have repurchased the Francis of
Guildford property in Worplesdon, Surrey, for its book value of Pounds
525,000.
