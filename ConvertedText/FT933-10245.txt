FT  05 AUG 93 / Countdown to Sellafield reprocessing plant


1977: Go-ahead for Thorp construction after public inquiry and two
parliamentary debates.
February 1992: Construction completed at cost of Pounds 2.85bn.
November 16 1992: Pollution Inspectorate and agriculture ministry begin
eight-week public consultation on environmental impact of Thorp.
January 1993: Date Thorp originally expected to start operation.
Inspectorate extends consultation for two weeks.
May 1993: Inspectorate gives report to Department of the Environment.
Attorney-general says department may have to consider wider economic and
diplomatic justification for plant to be safe from judicial review.
June 15: Cabinet committee backs early start-up of Thorp.
June 28: DoE and agriculture ministry announce second round of consultation,
to consider wider issues.
August 4: Start of consultation.
October 4: Consultation scheduled to end.
