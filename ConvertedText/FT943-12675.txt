FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Black Arrow dips to Pounds 1.6m


Reduced pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.6m, compared with Pounds 2.03m, were
announced by Black Arrow Group for the 12 months to March 31.
However, last time there was an exceptional Pounds 668,000 profit on a
property disposal.
Turnover dipped to Pounds 19m (Pounds 20.4m) with the office furniture
manufacturing, distribution and partitioning side contributing Pounds 17.8m
(Pounds 18.9m), and leasing and instalment finance Pounds 1.29m (Pounds
1.52m).
Mr Arnold Edward, chairman, said trading had improved during the latter part
of the year and the upturn had continued into the first quarter.
Earnings per share slipped to 4.16p (5.02p) but the dividend is held at 2.6p
with an increased final of 2.1p (1.6p).
