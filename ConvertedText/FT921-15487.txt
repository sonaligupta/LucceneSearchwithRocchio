FT  10 JAN 92 / World News in Brief: UN man for Cambodia


A Japanese official will head the UN operation in Cambodia, the biggest of
its kind yet mounted. Yasushi Akashi, a US-educated former diplomat, will be
in charge of a budget of some Dollars 1.5bn (Pounds 820m).
