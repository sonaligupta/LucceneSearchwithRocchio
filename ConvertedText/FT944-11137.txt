FT  08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: ABB Asea Brown Boveri - Contracts and
Ventures


   By REUTER


   STOCKHOLM


Swiss-Swedish engineering group ABB Asea Brown Boveri has been asked to lead
a SKr1.5bn (Dollars 203.5m) gasoil plant project for privately-owned OK
Petroleum. OKP said the project was for environmental improvements at OKP's
refinery in Gothenburg on the west coast of Sweden.
