FT  29 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Jacobs confirms disposal talks


John I Jacobs, the shipowner and shipbroker, has confirmed that it is in
discussions which could lead to the sale of a part of the group.
The statement was made because of recent movements in the share price.
Yesterday, the shares fell 4p to 28p.
