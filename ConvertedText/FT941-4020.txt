FT  14 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Two Bafta wins for Spielberg


Two Steven Spielberg films won British Academy of Film and Television Awards
 -Jurassic Park for best special effects and Schindler's List for best
cinematography. ITV's Prime Suspect 3, starring Helen Mirren, was best drama
serial. Best news coverage was from ITN's News at Ten for reports on the
storming of the Moscow White House and the civil war in Georgia.
