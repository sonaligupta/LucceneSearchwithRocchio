FT  30 JAN 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


JOHANNESBURG's banking sector saw the most activity with ABSA, the finance
group, down 75 cents to R10.65 on news that had bought Bankorp for R1.3bn.
The all-gold index lost 6 to 1,252. The industrial index shed 4 to 4,422,
while the all-share slipped 7 to 3,613.
