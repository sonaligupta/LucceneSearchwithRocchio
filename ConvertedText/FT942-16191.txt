FT  12 APR 94 / Legal quick fix


The Faculty of Advocates, the Scottish legal body, is to offer businesses a
quick way of settling disputes, modelled on the US Alternative Dispute
Resolution service. Mr Andrew Hardie, faculty dean, said the service was
intended to offer quick solutions to businesses locked in commercial
disputes, which might otherwise have to pay huge amounts in fighting court
cases.
