FT  20 APR 94 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands purchase


Park-Lands (London), the 50.1 per cent owned subsidiary of Ex-Lands, is
acquiring Lansdown Industrial Estate, Cheltenham, for Pounds 4.7m cash.
The freehold estate has been independently valued by Drivers Jonas at not
less than the purchase price. Annual net rental income is about Pounds
580,000.
The estate covers some 13.5 acres. The existing buildings provide a gross
usable floor area of about 335,000 sq ft.
