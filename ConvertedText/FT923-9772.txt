FT  03 AUG 92 / BAe wins Pounds 300m order


BRITISH Aerospace has won a Pounds 300m contract to build 13 new Harrier
training aircraft for the Royal Air Force, it was announced yesterday.
The two-seater aircraft, expected to enter service in 1994, include
night-vision equipment which would enable use in frontline activities.
