FT  08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: GE Power Systems - Contracts and ventures


   By REUTER


   NEW YORK


GE Power Systems, a unit of General Electric of the US, has won a Dollars
164m contract to furnish and build a 300MW addition to Riyadh Power Plant 8
for Saudi Consolidated Electric. The expansion is expected to begin
operations next summer.
