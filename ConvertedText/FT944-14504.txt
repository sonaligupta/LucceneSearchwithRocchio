FT  22 OCT 94 / UK Company News: HG Smaller


Net asset value of the Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index Investment Trust
dropped by 5.5 per cent to 126.56p per share in the six months to September
30, against 133.92p at March 31.
Net available revenue increased to Pounds 883,136 (Pounds 636,939) and
earnings per share came out at 1.33p (2.23p). An unchanged interim dividend
of 1p is declared.
