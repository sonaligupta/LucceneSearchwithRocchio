FT  23 APR 93 / Waverley in default


WAVERLEY Fund Management, a member of the financial intermediaries regulator
Fimbra, has been declared in default by the Investors' Compensation Scheme.
This opens the way for investors to claim compensation. Waverley was
suspended by Fimbra last April.
