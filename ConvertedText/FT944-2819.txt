FT  15 DEC 94 / International Company News: Philip Morris appoints chairman


   By AP-DJ


   NEW YORK


Philip Morris, the US tobacco group said Mr William Murray would retire as
chairman, on February 1, AP-DJ reports from New York.
Mr Geoffrey C. Bible, chief executive and president was named as chairman.
Philip Morris said Mr Bible would retain the title of chief executive.
