FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Abbey National


Abbey National Financial Services, the financial advisory subsidiary of
Abbey National, has bought GM Benefit Consulting Group, the pensions
consultancy, for an undisclosed sum.
Net assets of GM Benefit, a Guinness Mahon offshoot, were valued at Pounds
409,000 at May 31.
