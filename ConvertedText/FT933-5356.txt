FT  03 SEP 93 / International Company News: American Trust assets rise


Net asset value per share of American Trust rose to 282.8p as at July 31
1993, compared with 265.2p six months earlier and 200.5p a year before.
Available revenue for the six months to end-July amounted to Pounds 2.4m, up
from Pounds 1.99m, giving earnings per share of 2.9p against 2.41p. In part
to reduce disparity with the final payment, the interim distribution is
lifted from 1.5p to 1.8p.
