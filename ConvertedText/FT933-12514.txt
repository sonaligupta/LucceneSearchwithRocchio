FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust Preferred


Net asset value per ordinary income share of Abtrust Preferred Income
Investment Trust stood at 99.77p at the May 31 year end.
That compared with 71.55p six months earlier and with 82.38p at the previous
year end. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 1.57m, against Pounds 1.29m for the
previous 13-month period. Earnings per share came out at 13.61p (14.33p).
