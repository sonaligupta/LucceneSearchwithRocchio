FT  25 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Printech


Printech International, the Dublin-based computer manual printer, saw
profits advance to IPounds 1.71m (Pounds 1.62m) in the first half of 1992.
The advance from last time's IPounds 1.69m was achieved on turnover of
IPounds 13.5m (IPounds 10.1m).
Mr James Flavin, chairman, blamed the erosion of margins on price pressures
within the computer industry.
Earnings per share were unchanged at 5.1p; the interim dividend goes up from
0.8p to 0.9p.
