FT  23 APR 93 / UK Company News: Savage faces possible bid


Savage Group, the USM-quoted hardware supplier, is facing a possible bid.
Following the rise in the shares, which closed yesterday at 68p, up 19p, the
company was forced to make an announcement that it was involved in talks.
Last month it reported pre-tax profits for the six months to December 31
ahead 19 per cent at Pounds 1.04m on turnover of Pounds 32.3m.
