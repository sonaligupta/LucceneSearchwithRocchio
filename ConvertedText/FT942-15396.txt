FT  15 APR 94 / People: Anglian Water


Robin Gourlay, who became deputy chairman of Anglian Water in February, is
to take over as chairman, probably by the autumn. Gourlay, chief executive
and managing director of BP Nutrition will take over as soon as his
responsibilities at BP have been completed. He replaces retiring chairman
Bernard Henderson.
