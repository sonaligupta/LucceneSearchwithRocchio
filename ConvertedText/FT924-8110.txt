FT  12 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: Polls blow for government


Confidence in the government's handling of the economy has reached a new
low, according to an ICM poll in The Guardian today. Meanwhile, a Harris
poll for ITV's World in Action suggests 37 per cent of people blame the
government for the recession.
