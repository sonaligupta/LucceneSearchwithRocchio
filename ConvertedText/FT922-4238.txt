FT  06 JUN 92 / Finance and the Family: At a glance - Docklands EZ folds


The crisis in the Docklands property market claimed another victim this
week. The sponsors of CET 7, a Docklands enterprise zone trust which was
launched at the end of the last tax year, gave up their attempt to
restructure the fund. Investors' money was returned with interest on
Thursday and should have arrived by now.
