FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Kode Intl shows advance to Pounds 1m


Kode International, the computer maintenance group, lifted pre-tax profits
from Pounds 486,000 to Pounds 1.03m in 1992. The shares responded with a
rise of 13p to 173p.
Acquisitions boosted turnover to Pounds 20.9m (Pounds 15.7m). A final
dividend of 4p raises the total to 5.5p (4p). Earnings per share amounted to
8.6p (3.9p).
