FT  08 NOV 94 / Japan's economy begins to recover


   By REUTER


   TOKYO


Japan's economy is gradually moving towards recovery as brightness continues
to spread, the Economic Planning Agency said in a monthly report issued
early today, slightly raising the tone of its economic assessment, Reuter
reports from Tokyo. The EPA said unemployment remained a problem, mainly in
manufacturing, but the worst was over.
