FT  16 DEC 94 / Rosyth contract


The Rosyth dockyard in Fife has won a Pounds 100m contract to refit the
Royal Navy nuclear submarine HMS Superb, it said yesterday. Babcock Rosyth
Defence, manager of the yard, said work would start immediately and last
until mid-1997.
