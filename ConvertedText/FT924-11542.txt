FT  24 OCT 92 / First Trident submarine leaves VSEL shipyard


Britain's first Trident ballistic-missile submarine, the 16,000-tonne
Vanguard, left the VSEL shipyard in Barrow-In-Furness yesterday for Faslane
in Scotland to begin trails before being handed over to the Royal Navy next
year. Three more boats are on order. The Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament
has called a demonstration in Glasgow today.
