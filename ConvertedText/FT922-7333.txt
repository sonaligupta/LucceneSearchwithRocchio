FT  19 MAY 92 / World Trade News: Car 'peace' talks


   By REUTER


   CHICAGO


DETROIT'S Big Three carmakers and their five Japanese rivals yesterday
agreed to try to bridge their differences, Reuter reports from Chicago. A
working group will include members of the Motor Vehicle Manufacturers
Association and the Japan Automobile Manufacturers Association.
