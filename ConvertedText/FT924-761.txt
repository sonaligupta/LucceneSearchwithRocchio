FT  22 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Fourth interim at TR High Income


TR High Income Trust is to pay a fourth interim dividend of 1.4p, bringing
the total to date for the year to 4.8p.
The trust will be declaring a fifth interim dividend on February 23 1993,
thereby completing the dividend pay-out for the year to December 31 1992.
