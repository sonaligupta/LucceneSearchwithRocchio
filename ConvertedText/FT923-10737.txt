FT  29 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Temple Bar net asset value ahead


Temple Bar Investment Trust had a net asset value of 281.14p at June 30,
against 277.59p a year earlier and 269.81p at its December 31 year end.
Net profits for the half year improved to Pounds 4.38m (Pounds 4.15m) for
earnings per share of 7.642p (7.248p). The interim dividend is unchanged at
4.5p and directors expect to maintain the total at 13p.
