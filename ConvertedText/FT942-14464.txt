FT  20 APR 94 / UK Company News: Newarthill returns to black with Pounds
1.4m


Newarthill, the holding company for Sir Robert McAlpine, the civil engineer,
made pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.38m in the year to October 31, against
losses of Pounds 20.7m.
Turnover fell to Pounds 221.5m (Pounds 268.1m) and operating losses were cut
to Pounds 3.05m (Pounds 18.4m). Behind the pre-tax gain were exceptional
profits of Pounds 9.64m (Pounds 3.13m) and reduced net costs for interest
and write-backs of Pounds 4.6m (Pounds 6.68m).
Earnings emerged at 6.2p (losses 152.4p).
