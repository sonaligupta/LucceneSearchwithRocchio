FT  27 JAN 94 / UK Company News In Brief: Danka Business Systems


DANKA BUSINESS Systems, through its UK subsidiary, Danka UK, has acquired
the photocopying interests of Images, which supplies a full range of
equipment from Toshiba and Canon. Consideration is Pounds 370,000 cash.
