FT  17 JAN 92 / Phone fraud broken


   By ROBERT GRAHAM


   ROME


Italian police have broken a serious fraud in the use of cellular telephones
costing SIP, the state telephone company, L20bn (Dollars 16.75m), writes
Robert Graham in Rome. A total of 28 people have been arrested, mostly in La
Spezia, Pisa and Naples, and 1,000 cellular telphones confiscated.
