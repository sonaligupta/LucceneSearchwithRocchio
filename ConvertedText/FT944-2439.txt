FT  16 DEC 94 / Post Office staff accept 2.8% deal


Post Office workers have voted by two to one to accept a pay deal which will
increase basic pay rates by 2.8 per cent from November 1.
An existing Pounds 1.50 weekly supplement will be consolidated into basic
pay from January 1, increasing pensionable earnings by a further 0.6 per
cent.
