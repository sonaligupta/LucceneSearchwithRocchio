FT  25 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Hibernian


Hibernian Group, the Dublin-based insurance company, announced results ahead
of its expectations for the six months to end-June.
Pre-tax profits surged from IPounds 2.84m to IPounds 8.65m (Pounds 8.18m).
Shareholders' funds increased by 6.3 per cent to IPounds 76.7m against
IPounds 72.2m at the December year-end.
Earnings per share came out at 11p (3.5p) and the interim dividend is
increased to 2.1p (1.9p).
