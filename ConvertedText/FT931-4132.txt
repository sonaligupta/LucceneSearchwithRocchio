FT  12 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Water charges investigated


STEEP INCREASES in water bills in the south-west are being investigated by
ministers, Mr John Major said yesterday. Questioned in the Commons by Mr
Matthew Taylor, Liberal Democrat MP for Truro, the prime minister said that
he and Mr Michael Howard, the environment secretary, were investigating.
