FT  21 OCT 94 / International Company News: Slight advance at McDonald's in
third quarter


   By LAURIE MORSE


   CHICAGO


McDonald's, the world's largest restaurateur and popular hamburger purveyor,
reported third-quarter income of Dollars 349.8m, or 48 cents a share, on
sales of Dollars 6.9bn, up slightly from the previous year's Dollars 310.9m,
or 42 cents a share, on sales of Dollars 6.2bn.
For the first nine months of the year, McDonald's earned Dollars 915.5m, or
Dollars 1.25 a share, compared with last year's Dollars 818.0m, or Dollars
1.10 a share.
Sales for the period were Dollars 19bn, compared with Dollars 17.4bn in the
first nine months of 1993.
McDonald's growth is driven primarily by expansion. During the first nine
months of this year, the company added 672 new restaurants, compared with
576 during the same period last year.
Of the new locations opened this year, 280 were in the US and 392 were
overseas. The company said it had an additional 449 restaurants under
construction at the end of the third quarter. The company currently operates
14,665 restaurants in 74 countries.
