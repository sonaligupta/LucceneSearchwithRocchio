FT  08 NOV 93 / Construction Contracts: Jail hires Amec


AMEC's Scottish building and civil engineering company, AMEC CONSTRUCTION
SCOTLAND, has been awarded a five-year contract to maintain Scotland's
largest jail, Barlinnie.
The award is in accordance with the Scottish Prison Service's move towards
market testing of trades work.
The prison is to phase in market testing for its other work sectors over the
next three years.
Worth some Pounds 6m, the contract will involve general maintenance and
minor buildings works as well as all mechanical and electrical services
associated with the daily running of the jail.
