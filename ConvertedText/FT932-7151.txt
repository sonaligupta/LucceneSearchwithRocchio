FT  25 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea


There was good general demand for landed reports the Tea Brokers
Association. Bright liquoring East African teas and better mediums were
generally several pence dearer but other descriptions proved irregularly
easier. The small quantity of Ceylons on offer sold at easier rates.
Offshore brighter teas were fully firm to dearer but plainer sorts showed an
easier tendency with some withdrawals. Quotations: quality 155, nom good
medium 128, medium 105, low medium 87. The highest price realised this week
was 155p for a Rwanda pd.
