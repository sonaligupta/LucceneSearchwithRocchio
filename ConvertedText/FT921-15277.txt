FT  11 JAN 92 / IPC launches new magazine


IPC Magazines, the Reed International subsidiary, yesterday announced it is
launching a weekly magazine for women on January 20.
The magazine, Puzzle Weekly, with a planned print run of 400,000 is IPC's
seventh weekly publication for women and joins titles such as Me, Chat,
Woman and Woman's Own.
The magazine will combine articles on traditional subjects such as cookery,
heath and travel with a wide variety of puzzles.
