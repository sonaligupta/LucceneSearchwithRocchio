FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bourne End


Bourne End Properties yesterday reported lower losses for the half year to
end-June and said it had requested a temporary suspension of its shares.
Dealings were suspended at 97p pending the outcome of talks which may lead
to 'substantial acquisitions.'
Pre-tax losses were trimmed to Pounds 145,000 (Pounds 543,000). Losses
amounted to 1.7p (6.5p).
