FT  06 JAN 94 / Observer: PC Watch


Alas, political correctitude has reached Smythsons, the Queen's Bond Street
stationers. Former recipients of The Smythson Businessman's Organiser,
staple present for the man who has everything, now have to make do with the
Business Organiser. 'We still refer to it as the Businessman's Organiser
here; it's the same thing,' trills an assistant.
