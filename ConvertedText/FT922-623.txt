FT  26 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Wimbledon


Britain's sole singles survivor, Jeremy Bates, continued his success with a
straight sets win over Spain's Javier Sanchez. In other matches Boris
Becker, Andre Agassi and John McEnroe emerged victorious after hard-fought
matches. In the women's singles, Martina Navratilova, Steffi Graf and
Jennifer Capriati all won.
