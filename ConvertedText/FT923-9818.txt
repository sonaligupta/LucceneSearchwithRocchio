FT  03 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Riva


Assisted by cost reductions and a refinancing Riva Group, the USM-quoted
supplier of electronic point of sale systems, returned profits of Pounds
314,000 in the six months to June 30 compared with previous losses of Pounds
1.07m.
Turnover totalled Pounds 28.6m (Pounds 29.4m). The result was struck after
exceptional redundancy costs of Pounds 174,000 (Pounds 277,000). The net
interest charge was down from Pounds 951,000 to Pounds 484,000.
