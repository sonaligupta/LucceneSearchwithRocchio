FT  30 APR 94 / International Company News: Aran Energy nearly doubled to
IPounds 5.7m


Aran Energy, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration and production
company, reported nearly doubled pre-tax profits of IPounds 5.7m (Pounds
5.5m) for the year to December 31. In 1992 profits were IPounds 3m.
Turnover amounted to IPounds 63.9m (IPounds 45m) and after tax of IPounds
1.32m (nil) net profits were IPounds 4.38m (IPounds 3m).
Earnings per share rose from 1.16p to 1.67p.
