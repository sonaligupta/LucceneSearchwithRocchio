FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: T Clarke declines 24% to Pounds 1.44m


Pre-tax profits of T Clarke, the electrical contractor, fell 24 per cent,
from Pounds 1.89m to Pounds 1.44m, for 1992. Turnover advanced 15 per cent
to Pounds 68.2m, against Pounds 59.2m.
Earnings were 7.34p (9.8p) and the final dividend is cut to 2.81p for a
total of 4.07p (6.552p). The company is a subsidiary of CS Holdings.
