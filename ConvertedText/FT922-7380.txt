FT  19 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Berry Birch surges after low tax charge


Higher turnover and profits, and a more than doubled dividend for the year
ended January 31 1992 are announced by Berry Birch & Noble, financial
adviser and insurance broker.
Turnover rose to Pounds 5.66m (Pounds 5.26m) and pre-tax profit to Pounds
816,000 (Pounds 628,000), while earnings per share came to 10.04p (6.18p)
after a substantially reduced 22.5 (38) per cent tax charge.
There is a final dividend of 2.2p to make 3.2p for the year, against 1.5p.
