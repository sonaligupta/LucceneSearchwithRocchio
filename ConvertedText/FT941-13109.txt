FT  27 JAN 94 / Shopping club


Nurdin & Peacock, the cash and carry operator, has signed contracts with
Wilson Connolly, the property group, to build its fourth UK warehouse club
at Bristol. The 84,000 sq ft store will be called Cargo Club and will sell
discounted goods to members.
