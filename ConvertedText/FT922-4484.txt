FT  05 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Greycoat makes Pounds 19.1m sale


Greycoat, the property company specialising in central London offices, has
sold a building in the City of London to Scottish Amicable Life Assurance
for Pounds 19.1m.
The 46,000 sq ft office building, St Clement's House in Clement's Lane, is
let to Standard Chartered Bank.
There is a provision for a further payment to Greycoat in June 1995,
dependent on the value of the building at that time.
