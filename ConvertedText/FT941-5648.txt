FT  05 MAR 94 / Zambian airline near collapse


   By REUTER


   LUSAKA


Zambia Airways, the national airline, is virtually bankrupt and may have to
close, Mr William Harrington, transport minister, told parliament, Reuter
reports from Lusaka. He said it owed Dollars 45.5m.
