FT  06 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: Gerrard Vivian Gray


GERRARD Vivian Gray, the private client stockbroker, is launching the first
managed portfolio of Lloyd's corporate capital investment trusts. The
service will invest in a diversified range of four to eight of the new
trusts, which invest in the Lloyd's of London insurance market.
Minimum investment is Pounds 20,000. Charges are 2 per cent plus VAT
initially, and 1 per cent plus VAT annually, as well as 1.5 per cent
commission on transactions (less for larger bargains).
