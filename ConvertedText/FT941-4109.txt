FT  12 MAR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


Unrest in the Bophuthatswana homeland helped unsettle share prices and the
overall index fell 13 to 5,124. The fall was sharper on the industrial index
which lost 56 to 5,962.
DeBeers was a casualty with a loss of R1.25 to R111.
Gold fared better with its index adding 35 to 1,933.
