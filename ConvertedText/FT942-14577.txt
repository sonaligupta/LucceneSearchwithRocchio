FT  20 APR 94 / Bond hearing on bankruptcy


   By NIKKI TAIT


Mr Alan Bond, Australian former tycoon, yesterday abandoned a request for a
hearing into his mental fitness and agreed to appear at a bankruptcy
examination next month, writes Nikki Tait. On Mr Bond's admission to
hospital in Perth in February, a psychiatric report said he was suffering
from depression.
