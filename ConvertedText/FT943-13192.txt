FT  21 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Dowding & Mills Dollars 1.7m acquisition


Dowding & Mills, the electrical and mechanical services group, has acquired
Apparatus Sales and Service, based in Salt Lake City, Utah, for Dollars 1.7m
(Pounds 1.11m). The company rewinds and repairs electric motors.
In 1993 Apparatus broke even on turnover of Dollars 2.1m.
