FT  06 NOV 93 / UBS names new chief executive


UBS, the London-based stockbrokers, is splitting the roles of chairman and
chief executive.
Mr Rudolf Mueller, who has held both positions since 1989, will remain
chairman. Mr David Robbins, who has been working for UBS in Zurich, will
become chief executive.
