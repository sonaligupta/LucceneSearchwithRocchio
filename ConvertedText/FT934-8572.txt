FT  15 NOV 93 / People: Zeneca Seeds UK


Tony Hayter, a director of the British Society of Plant Breeders and a
member of the Council of the National Institute of Agricultural Botany, has
been appointed md of ZENECA SEEDS UK on the retirement of Peter Konig.
Hayter became plant breeding and research manager of ICI Seeds UK on its
creation in 1987.
