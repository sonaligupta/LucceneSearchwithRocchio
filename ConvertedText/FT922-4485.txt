FT  05 JUN 92 / UK Company News: United Biscuits in Spanish joint venture


United Biscuits is setting up a sales company to distribute its McVitie's,
Carr's and Ortiz products in Spain.
The company will be jointly owned with Royal Brands, a Spanish food group,
and will also handle that company's brands. It will start operating in
January next year.
McVitie's is the largest UK biscuit maker and the second largest in Europe
with sales of Pounds 787m in 1991. Royal Brands is owned by Tabacalera, the
leading Spanish tobacco group, and is the market leader in the Spanish
biscuit market.
