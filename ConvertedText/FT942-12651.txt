FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News: Arjo cuts bonus


Performance-related bonuses paid to directors at Arjo Wiggins Appleton, the
Anglo-French paper manufacturer, fell from Pounds 280,000 to Pounds 86,000
in 1993, when the company suffered a 24 per cent fall in pre-tax profits to
Pounds 122.1m (Pounds 161.1m).
The salary of Mr Anthony Stenham, chairman, rose to Pounds 323,552 (Pounds
307,166) over the 12 months. The highest paid UK director received Pounds
422,345 which included Pounds 113,080 in respect of an unfunded pension
scheme.
