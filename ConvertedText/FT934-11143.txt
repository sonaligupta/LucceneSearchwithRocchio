FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Hartwell disposes of Ford dealership


Hartwell, the motor distribution group, has sold its Ford dealership in
Lincoln to Pendragon Group.
Hartwell will retain the freehold of the site and it is planned that the
dealership will relocate in a year's time.
