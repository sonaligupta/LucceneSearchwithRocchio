FT  06 AUG 93 / International Company News in Brief: Banco Exterior


   By REUTER


   PARIS


Banco Exterior, the Spanish bank, said consolidated net profit after
minorities rose 7.6 per cent to Pta17.48bn (Dollars 131m) in the first half
of 1993 from Pta16.24bn in the same period the previous year, Reuter reports
from Paris.
Banco Exterior forms part of Argentaria Corp Bancaria de Espana, the
state-run banking group.
