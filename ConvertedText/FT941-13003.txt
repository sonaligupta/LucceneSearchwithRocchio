FT  27 JAN 94 / UK Company News: ACT subsidiary sold to its management


ACT, the Birmingham-based computer services company, has sold its Brann
Direct Marketing subsidiary to members of that company's senior management
for Pounds 8.15m cash.
Net assets of Brann at December 31 1992 amounted to Pounds 6.64m and profits
for that year were Pounds 851,000. The purchase price included settlement of
intercompany debt.
Brann was part of the BIS Group, and was acquired by ACT in July 1993.
The sale proceeds will be applied in satisfying in full the final payment of
the deferred consideration for BIS.
