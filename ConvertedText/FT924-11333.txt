FT  27 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Automotive Prods ahead at Pounds 8.7m


Automotive Products, the BBA Group offshoot, reported pre-tax profits ahead
at Pounds 8.7m, against Pounds 3m, for the first half of 1992.
Turnover was Pounds 148.6m (Pounds 139.8m). The pre-tax figure was struck
after an exceptional charge of Pounds 1.7m relating to costs of adjusting
the cost base.
