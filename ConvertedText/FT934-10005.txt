FT  08 NOV 93 / Construction Contracts: US bridge expands


TIDEWATER CONSTRUCTION CORPORATION, an operating subsidiary of Hanson's
Spectrum Construction Group, has been awarded a contract by the Virginia
Department of Transportation for Dollars 72.7m (Pounds 48.1m).
Tidewater is to replace the George P. Coleman Bridge, an existing 3800ft
double leaf swing span bridge over the York River in Yorktown, Virginia.
The effect of the replacement will be to widen the roadway from 40ft to
80ft.
