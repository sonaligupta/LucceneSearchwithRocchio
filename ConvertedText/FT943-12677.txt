FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: W Canning


W Canning, the speciality chemicals and electronic components group, has
sold its electronic component distribution division to its management.
The division, to be called Innovative Electronic Components Group, has a
network of distribution companies in Italy, France and Germany, and will
acquire Future Components, a UK distributor.
Finance of Pounds 19.5m is being provided for the deal, which is being
backed by CINVen, the venture capital company, with NatWest Acquisition
Finance providing senior debt and working capital.
