FT  16 MAR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


A flurry of activity before the expiry of futures and options lifted
equities in late trading. The overall index advanced 98 to 5,254,
industrials 105 to 6,099 and golds 3 to 1,967.
Anglos climbed R4.50 to R229.50 and SAB R2 to R92.
