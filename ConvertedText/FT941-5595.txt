FT  05 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Smaller Companies IT asset value ahead


Net asset value per share of Smaller Companies Investment Trust rose by 74
per cent from 84.1p to 146.4p over the year to December 31. Fully diluted,
the value rose by 67 per cent to 140.7p.
Earnings per share fell to 3.04p (3.19p) and again no final dividend is
proposed. Interims totalling 2.6p (same) have been paid.
