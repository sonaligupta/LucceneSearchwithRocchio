FT  22 OCT 92 / World News In Brief: US reserves


US reserves of recoverable crude oil may be far larger than previously
believed, according to an Energy Department study. It estimates them at from
99bn barrels if oil is sold at Dollars 20 a barrel to 204bn barrels if the
price is Dollars 27. The estimates assume the use of existing technology.
