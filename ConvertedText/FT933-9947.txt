FT  06 AUG 93 / UK Company News: National Home Loans sells healthcare arm


National Home Loans Holdings has disposed of its controlling interest in
Community Health Services, its healthcare subsidiary, which has been
acquired by a new holding company.
NHL has exchanged its CHS shares for 35.3 per cent of the new holding
company and the remaining 64.7 per cent has been issued to venture capital
fund managers Nash, Sells and Partners for Pounds 1.5m which will be paid to
NHL in settlement of monies lent to CHS. NHL's holding in the new company is
worth Pounds 984,000.
