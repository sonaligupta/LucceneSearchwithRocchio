FT  06 JAN 94 / People: Johnson & Higgins Holdings


John Gussenhoven is appointed chairman of JOHNSON & HIGGINS HOLDINGS; he
replaces Charles Carter and Nuno de Brito e Cunha who have become co-chief
executives of London Market Investors, which is sponsored by Johnson &
Higgins and Salomon Brothers.
