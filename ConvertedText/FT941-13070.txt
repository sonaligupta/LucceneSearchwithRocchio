FT  27 JAN 94 / Technology: Buzzwords - Software at Work


EXPERT SYSTEM takes the principles of programming (If this situation then
take this action . . . ) into the realms of rule-based decision making.
Rules determining a course of action or diagnosis are built in to the
software in an interactive, question-and-answer form.
SIGNATURE refers to each 'bundle' of printed, paginated text bound into a
book. Sir David Frost's autobiography, for example, is a big hardback with
18 signatures, 17 of 32 pages text, and one of 16 pages of illustrations.
