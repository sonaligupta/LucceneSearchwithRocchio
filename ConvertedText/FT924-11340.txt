FT  27 OCT 92 / Investment plea


   By AP-DJ


   SEOUL


The Asian Bankers' Association said at its annual meeting yesterday attended
by some 200 delegates from 23 Asian and Pacific nations that the private
sector should take on a greater burden of investment to enhance Asia's
infrastructure and to sustain growth, AP-DJ reports from Seoul.
