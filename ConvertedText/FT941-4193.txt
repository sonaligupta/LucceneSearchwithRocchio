FT  12 MAR 94 / Shanghai dissidents arrested


   By REUTER


   SHANGHAI


Two prominent Shanghai dissidents, Yang Zhou and Wang Fuchen, were arrested
yesterday, as the US secretary of state, Mr Warren Christopher, arrived in
China to confront its leaders on human rights issues. He has to assess
whether the US should continue to grant Chinese exports most favoured nation
status, entailing low tariffs.
