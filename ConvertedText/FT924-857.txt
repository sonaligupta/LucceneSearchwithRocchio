FT  22 DEC 92 / E Timor crackdown continues


   By WILLIAM KEELING


   JAKARTA


The Indonesian army has arrested at least 60 people in the disputed
territory of East Timor since mid-November in a continuing military
crackdown, the Sydney office of Amnesty International, the human rights
group, said yesterday, writes William Keeling in Jakarta.
