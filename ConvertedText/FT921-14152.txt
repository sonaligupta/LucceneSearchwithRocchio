FT  17 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value rises at Investment Co


Net asset value for the Investment Company improved from 32.8p to 36.65p
over the six months to September 30. A year earlier the figure had been
32.09p.
Total income for the half year was Pounds 635,000 (Pounds 668,000) for
pre-tax profits of Pounds 592,000 (Pounds 589,000) including profit on
changes of investments of Pounds 28,000 (Pounds 22,000).
Earnings per share were 1.47p (1.49p) and directors declared an unchanged
interim dividend of 0.375p.
