FT  05 JUN 92 / Observer: Forked tongue


The warning 'Beware of Greeks bearing gifts' has been given an equivalent
for England by the latest ABC Guide to International Travel. Beware of
English saying 'With the greatest respect . . .', it cautions. What they
mean is: 'I am about to humiliate you publicly.'
