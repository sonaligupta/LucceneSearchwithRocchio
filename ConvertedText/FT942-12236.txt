FT  30 APR 94 / International Company News: Navan Resources achieves IPounds
667,933


Navan Resources, the Dublin-based natural resources producer, achieved
pre-tax profits of IPounds 667,933 (Pounds 649,361) on turnover of IPounds
8.33m in the 21 months to December 31.
The figures were mainly the result of its Hungarian mines which achieved net
profits of IPounds 931,163. The company also has interests in Bulgaria,
Spain and the Irish Republic.
Earnings per share came out at 0.59p.
