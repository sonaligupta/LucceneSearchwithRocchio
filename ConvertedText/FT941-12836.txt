FT  28 JAN 94 / Engineering sales show increase


There was a 1.1 per cent rise in the engineering industry's sales for the
three months to November compared with the previous three months, figures
from the Central Statistical Office showed yesterday.
The rise is based on constant 1990 prices. Export sales rose 3.2 per cent
while home sales were unchanged.
