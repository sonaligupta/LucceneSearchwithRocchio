FT  02 JUL 92 / Ukrainian threatens to quit


   By REUTER


   KIEV


Ukraine's teething problems worsened yesterday when its top economic
reformer threatened to resign and new difficulties surfaced in the division
of the Black Sea fleet with rival Russia, Reuter reports from Kiev. Mr
Vladimir Lanovoy said he planned to quit in protest at plans to appoint
another man above him.
