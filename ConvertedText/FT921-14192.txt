FT  17 JAN 92 / UK seizure of forged guiders


Forged 100 guilder (Pounds 31) banknotes were displayed at a London police
station yesterday after the UK's biggest seizure of counterfeit money.
Scotland Yard said later that Mr Andrew Thomas Bourke from County Limerick
in the Irish Republic had been charged with possessing forged Dutch currency
with a face value of nearly Pounds 20m.
