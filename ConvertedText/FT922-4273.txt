FT  06 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Ivory & Sime buys back Ensign stake


Ivory & Sime, the investment trust manager, yesterday confirmed that it was
buying back Ensign Trust's 7.2 per cent stake in the Ivory & Sime. The total
cash paid will be Pounds 2.49m and the deal is scheduled to go through on
Monday.
The purchase was arranged last month as part of a complex restructuring deal
following the purchase of Ensign Trust by the Merchant Navy Officers'
Pension Fund.
