FT  06 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Mile mark lowered


Algerian Noureddine Morceli took almost two seconds off the eight-year-old
world mile record held by Steve Cram of the UK with a time of three minutes
44.39 seconds at a meeting in Rieti, Italy.
