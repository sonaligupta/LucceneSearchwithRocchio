FT  08 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Everest team fined


A 14-strong British team which set out to climb Mount Everest was fined
Dollars 100,000 (Pounds 66,000) by Nepal for breaking regulations limiting
foreign members of expeditions to seven. Six members of the team, which
included actor Brian Blessed, reached the summit.
