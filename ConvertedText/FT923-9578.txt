FT  04 AUG 92 / At least 26 die in Tamil ambush


   By REUTER


   COLUMBO


Fourteen government soldiers and at least 12 Tamil rebels were killed when
separatists ambushed troops in east Sri Lankan jungles yesterday a military
spokesman said, Reuter reports from Columbo. The rebels ambushed two army
groups, totalling 25 soldiers.
