FT  01 DEC 94 / Whitehall asked to fill homes


Government departments have been asked to fill 5,166 of their empty homes
next year, Mr Robert Jones, environment minister, said yesterday.
The Department of Transport has the worst record on empty property, with 23
per cent of its 3,845 homes vacant. It has been asked to fill 484 properties
next year.
The North British Housing Association has raised a Pounds 25m revolving loan
from Kleinwort Benson, the UK merchant bank, in what is believed to be the
first unsecured lending to a housing association.
