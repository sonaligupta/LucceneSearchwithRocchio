FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Record orders help lift VHE


VHE Holdings, which came to the market in September, reported pre-tax
profits ahead by 30 per cent in the year to March 31.
Record orders were secured in land reclamation and regeneration activities,
and civil engineering made progress.
On turnover of Pounds 26.1m (Pounds 19.2m) profits were Pounds 3.76m (Pounds
2.89m). There was lower non-recurring directors' remuneration of Pounds
422,000 (Pounds 947,000) relating to before the flotation.
After the end of the period the company acquired TW Ward (Industrial
Dismantling), involved in specialist demolition work.
Earnings were 8p (6.5p) and a proposed final dividend of 2.7p makes a total
of 3.8p.
