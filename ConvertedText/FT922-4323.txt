FT  05 JUN 92 / International Capital Markets: UBF to spin off investment
side


   By AP-DJ


   STOCKHOLM


UNION Bank of Finland will spin off its investment banking operations as
part of a plan to concentrate on commercial banking operations, AP-DJ
reports from Stockholm.
Operations spanning mergers and acquisitions, equity finance, corporate
finance and issue syndication will be transferred to a new company,
Mandatum.
