FT  19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Forminster maintains position


FORMINSTER, the clothing manufacturer for mail order and chain stores, has
virtually maintained turnover and profit in the six months ended October 31
1992 despite a poor start to the year.
Pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 967,000 compared with Pounds 997,000, on
turnover of Pounds 11.09m (Pounds 11.57m).
Mr Ronald Gulliver, chairman, said that the second half was seasonally
stronger.
Earnings per share worked through at 22.48p (22.11p) and the interim
dividend is 4.04p (3.78p).
