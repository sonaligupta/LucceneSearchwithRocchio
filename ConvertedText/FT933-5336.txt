FT  03 SEP 93 / International Company News: NatWest sells assets


   By RICHARD WATERS


   NEW YORK


NATIONAL Westminster Bancorp sold a portfolio of troubled US property assets
to Daiwa Securities, further reducing its non-performing real estate
exposure, writes Richard Waters in New York.
The loans and properties, all in New Jersey, were part of a portfolio of
troubled assets.
