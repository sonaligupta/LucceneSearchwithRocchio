FT  12 MAR 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


JOHANNESBURG rose on positive sentiment ahead of the referendum next week
and a weak financial rand. The all-gold index rose 20 to 1,209 while the
industrial index advanced 8 to 4,407. The all-share index added 18 to 3,578.
