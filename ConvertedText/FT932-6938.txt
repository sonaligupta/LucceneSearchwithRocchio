FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: London Finance & Investment Group


LONDON FINANCE & Investment Group advised shareholders to take no action in
response to a document from New Burlington Street Investments until that had
been clarified. The board said that the Takeover Panel had requested New
Burlington to withdraw its document and to return any share certificates it
may have been sent.
