FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Harris (Philip)


HARRIS (PHILIP) has acquired the Birmingham-based occupational health
supplies business of Surgicon Reside Group and Surgicon for Pounds 632,000
cash, plus stock and debtors at valuation estimated at Pounds 350,000. The
business makes operating profits of about Pounds 100,000 on sales of Pounds
1.3m.
