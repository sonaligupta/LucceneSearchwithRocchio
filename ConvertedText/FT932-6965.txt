FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Fleming European improvement


At March 31 1993 net asset value of the Fleming European Fledgeling
Investment Trust rose to 77.7p, compared with 74.4p a year earlier.
The trust invests in continental European smaller companies. While the
outlook there remained poor until next year, the directors said there were
some signs of improving business within companies in the portfolio.
In the 1992-93 year gross revenue came to Pounds 874,000 (Pounds 622,000)
and earnings per share were 0.44p (0.09p). There is a return to the dividend
list with a payment of 0.15p.
