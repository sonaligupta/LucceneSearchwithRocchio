FT  15 JUN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


JOHANNESBURG saw a decline in gold shares on worries that the bullion price
was due for a correction. The index lost 89, or 5.4 per cent to 1,562, as
industrials shed 6 to 4,590 and the overall 45 to 3,904. Vaal Reefs fell R15
to R275.
