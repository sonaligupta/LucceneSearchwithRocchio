FT  22 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Holliday Chemical


Holliday Chemical Holdings, the speciality chemicals manufacturer, has
acquired Mallinckrodt's photo-chemical plant in Dieburg, Germany, for about
Dollars 8m (Pounds 5m) cash.
Some Dollars 5m is payable on completion with the balance a year later.
