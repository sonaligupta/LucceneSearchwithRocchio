FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: CRH Acquisition


The Irish building materials group, has acquired Schuster's Block and Bosse
Concrete Products, of the US, for Dollars 11.8m (Pounds 7.1m) cash,
including debt. Schuster's is the leading concrete masonry producer in
Indianapolis, Indiana, while Bosse makes patio products and precast concrete
at its plant near Atlanta, Georgia. CRH said the two companies' combined
trading profits amounted to Dollars 1.9m on turnover of Dollars 16m.
