FT  12 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: Israel quits talks


Israel on walked out of an international peace conference on Middle East
refugees in Ottawa because it objected to the participation of the head of
the Palestinian delegation to the refugee talks, Mohammad Hallaj, because he
was a member of the Palestine National Council, the Palestinian parliament
in exile.
