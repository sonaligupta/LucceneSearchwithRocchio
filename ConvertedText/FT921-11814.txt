FT  30 JAN 92 / Algerian police clash with fundamentalists


   By REUTER


   ALGIERS


ONE person was killed and seven wounded in clashes between security forces
and young Islamic militants, the official Algerian APS news agency reported
yesterday, Reuter reports from Algiers.
Police fired shots to disperse demonstrators angry at an attempt to arrest a
Moslem preacher in eastern Algiers.
