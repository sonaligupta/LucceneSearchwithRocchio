FT  26 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Aberdeen Steak cuts losses


ABERDEEN Steak Houses Group, the restaurants operator, reported reduced
pre-tax losses of Pounds 422,000 in the 1991 year, compared with Pounds
3.27m. However the trading profit on continuing activities fell from Pounds
2.37m to Pounds 934,000.
Mr A Salih, chairman, said that against the background of the recession, the
second-half had increased business, but not enough to make up for the heavy
losses at the beginning of the year.
Sales for continuing activities were currently ahead of last year.
Total turnover fell 17 per cent to Pounds 13.3m (Pounds 16m). The figure of
Pounds 14m (Pounds 12.2m) for continuing activities was 12 per cent down.
Losses per share were 2.6p (23.5p).
