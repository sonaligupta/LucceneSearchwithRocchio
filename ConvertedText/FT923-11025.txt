FT  27 JUL 92 / Observer: For love alone


The sing has been put back into Singapore with the lifting of its official
ban on buskers, but they had better refrain from numbers like 'Buddy can you
spare a dime?'
All they can earn from street entertainment is 'psychic income', says
National Arts Council chairman Tommy Koh. Any cash coming their way must be
given to charity.
