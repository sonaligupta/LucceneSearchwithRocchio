FT  27 JAN 94 / Brick production down in 1993


Brick production last year was 12 per cent below its level in 1992, but
actual deliveries were 8 per cent higher.
During the fourth quarter of last year, brick production and deliveries in
Great Britain totalled 680m and 666m respectively. At the end of December
stocks were 1.03bn, about 17 weeks' deliveries at current levels.
Seasonally adjusted and after allowing for working day variations, output in
the fourth quarter of last year was approximately the same as in the third
quarter, but 1 per cent higher than in the corresponding period a year
earlier. Deliveries were 4 per cent lower than in the third quarter but 13
per cent higher than in the fourth quarter of 1992.
