FT  04 MAR 94 / International Company News: Bankers Trust subsidiary
improves


   By NIKKI TAIT


Bankers Trust Australia, an offshoot of the US-based investment bank and now
the second largest fund management operation in Australia, said yesterday
that its operating profits rose to ADollars 363.5m (USDollars 259.9m) last
year from ADollars 345.8m, writes Nikki Tait.
After-tax profits in 1993 were ADollars 273.5m, up from ADollars 203.6m.
