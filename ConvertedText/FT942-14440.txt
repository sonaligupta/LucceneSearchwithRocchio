FT  20 APR 94 / UK Company News: Inchcape makes Pounds 1.21m disposal


Inchcape has sold Whetstone Garage to Europe Energy Group for Pounds 1.21m,
to be satisfied by Pounds 609,000 cash and the placing of 4.71m new Europe
Energy ordinary shares with institutions.
There will be a further payment of Pounds 500,000 for Whetstone's inventory.
Whetstone will be renamed Millenium Motor Holdings.
It is Europe Energy's first acquisition since Mr Gerald Davison, former
chairman of Keep Trust and managing director of Honda UK, was appointed
chairman and chief executive.
