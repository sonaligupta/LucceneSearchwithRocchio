FT  12 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Bullers reduces losses to Pounds 2.56m


Bullers, the giftware, decoration and fine arts products manufacturer,
reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.56m from turnover of Pounds
9.82m in the year to end-December.
The result compares with losses of Pounds 4.77m from sales of Pounds 12.8m
in 1990. At the operating level losses were cut from Pounds 2.12m to Pounds
1.52m.
Losses per share were reduced to 6.48p (12.72p) and again there is no
dividend.
