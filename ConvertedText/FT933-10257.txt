FT  05 AUG 93 / 999 emergency scheme announced


BRITISH Telecommunications and Mercury will provide the emergency '999'
service for all UK telecom operators under a scheme announced by Oftel, the
industry regulator.
Mr Don Cruickshank, Oftel director-general, said it was a pragmatic response
to problems arising from a proliferation of operators.
