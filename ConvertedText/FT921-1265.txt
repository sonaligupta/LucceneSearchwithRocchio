FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Mexico Fund reveals rights price


Mexico Fund, a mutual fund listed in New York and London which primarily
invests in Mexican equity, has revealed terms of a previously-announced
rights offering.
Holders of every three shares on the record date of March 20 will be
entitled to buy one additional share at Dollars 20, a one-third discount to
the Dollars 30 closing price on the New York Stock Exchange on Friday.
