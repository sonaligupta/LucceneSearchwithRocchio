FT  14 APR 94 / People: Bradstock Group


Edmund McGrath, chairman of Bradstock Blunt & Thompson, is also appointed
chairman of BRADSTOCK GROUP, on the resignation of David Plunkett. Plunkett
retires from Bradstock Blunt & Crawley, where he is succeeded as chairman by
Robin Gibson.
