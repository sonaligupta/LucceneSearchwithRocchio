FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Clayton, Son


Clayton, Son & Co, the Leeds-based engineer, announced pre-tax losses
narrowed to Pounds 1.26m, against Pounds 1.39m, for the six months to June
30.
Turnover amounted to Pounds 5.33m against Pounds 6.29m, including Pounds
599,197 from Baxter Crushers, sold in June 1992.
Losses were 47.78p (49.24p). The shares dipped 5p to 68p.
