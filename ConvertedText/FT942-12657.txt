FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News: El Oro Mining


El Oro Mining and Exploration, the investment and dealing company, reported
pre-tax profits down from Pounds 1.94m to Pounds 1.55m in 1993.
Earnings per share declined to 23.27p (30.53p) while a first and final
dividend of 20p compares with a 24p interim payment for the whole of 1992.
El Oro has a 49.85 per cent stake in the Exploration Company, which in turn
has a 46.75 per cent holding in El Oro.
Exploration Company suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.48m to
Pounds 1.72m in 1993, and earnings dropped from 14.61p to 9.78p. A final
dividend of 10p is recommended (12p interim for 1992).
