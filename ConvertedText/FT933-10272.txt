FT  05 AUG 93 / Fall in housing starts reported


HOUSING starts fell to a seasonally adjusted 44,300 in the second quarter of
this year from 47,200 in the first quarter, the Department of the
Environment said yesterday. On an unadjusted basis, 48,000 dwellings were
started in the second quarter, up from 47,100 in the first quarter and
43,500 in the second quarter of last year.
