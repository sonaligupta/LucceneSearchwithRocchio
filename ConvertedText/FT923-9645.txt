FT  04 AUG 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Lead plant restarts


   By REUTER


   NEW YORK


DOE RUN Company's lead smelter in Herculaneum, Missouri, is back in
operation, run by salaried staff, a company official said yesterday, reports
Reuter from New York.
It was shut July 30 when unionised workers went on strike over a contract
dispute.
