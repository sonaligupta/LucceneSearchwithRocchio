FT  12 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Smaller assets advance 19%


Kleinwort Smaller Companies Investment Trust increased its net asset value
per share by more than 19 per cent to 113.7p over the year to end-December.
Gross revenue for the year was down from Pounds 1.36m to Pounds 1.24m with
investment income lower at Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 1.03m) and other income at
Pounds 227,733 (Pounds 325,876).
Earnings per share were 5.07p (5.34p) and the total dividend stays at 4.6p
with a proposed final of 2.6p.
