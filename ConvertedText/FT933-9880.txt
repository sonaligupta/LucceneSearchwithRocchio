FT  06 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Explosion kills up to 70 in China


Huge explosions ignited a string of warehouses in Shenzhen, south China.
Reports said up to 70 had died, including many firemen, and 200 were
injured. Hong Kong's fire units and hospitals were on standby after an
unprecedented plea from China.
