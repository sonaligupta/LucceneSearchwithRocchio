FT  19 APR 94 / International Company News: Polaroid back in the black


   By MARTIN DICKSON


Polaroid, the instant camera manufacturer, has reported first-quarter net
earnings of Dollars 1.4m, or 3 cents a share, compared with a net loss of
Dollars 143.2m, or Dollars 3.07 a share, a year earlier.
Excluding various special accounting charges, it would have earned Dollars
2.4m, or 5 cents a share, in the 1993 quarter.
The company said worldwide shipments of instant cameras increased
substantially compared with the same period last year, while instant film
increased slightly.
