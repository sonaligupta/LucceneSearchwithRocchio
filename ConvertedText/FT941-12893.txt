FT  28 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Delecour wins Monte Carlo rally


Francois Delecour of France, driving a Ford Escort, won the Monte Carlo
rally, finishing 1 min 5 secs ahead of world champion Juha Kankkunen of
Finland in a Toyota Celica. It was Ford's first Monte Carlo win for 41
years.
