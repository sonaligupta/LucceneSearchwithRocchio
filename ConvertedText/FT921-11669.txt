FT  30 JAN 92 / Fisons peat deal expected


FISONS, the chemicals company, is expected to announce today that it has
reached an agreement with English Nature to extend peat cutting from the
Thorne Moors in Yorkshire, an area designated as a site of special
scientific interest.
The environment department said it expected that the long-term nature
conservation interests of the area would be preserved.
