FT  02 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Graseby offshoot sold to management


Graseby, formerly known as Cambridge Electronics, yesterday sold Graseby
Ajax, its machine tools subsidiary, to management. Graseby is receiving
Pounds 500,000 cash and will be repaid Pounds 552,000 of intra-company
loans. It will subscribe to Pounds 500,000 of non-voting redeemable
preference.
