FT  03 SEP 93 / International Company News: Michael Page rises to Pounds
1.57m


Michael Page Group, the recruitment agency, increased interim pre-tax
profits to Pounds 1.57m from Pounds 1.29m a year earlier. Turnover rose 9
per cent to Pounds 23.4m (Pounds 21.4m). The interim dividend is unchanged
at 0.6p paid out of earnings per share of 1.65p (1.36p).
