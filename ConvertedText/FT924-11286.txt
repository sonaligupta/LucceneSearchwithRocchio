FT  27 OCT 92 / World Trade News: China taste test


   By GARY MEAD


Consumer research in China receives a boost with the setting-up of a new
joint-venture company, thought to be the first of its kind, to be called
Survey Research Group (Guangzhou), Gary Mead writes. It will focus on
developing consumer research in Chinese cities to help companies and
advertising agencies understand Chinese taste.
