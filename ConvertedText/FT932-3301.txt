FT  15 JUN 93 / People: Lloyd replaces Lloyd


Peter Lloyd, one of the founders of the insurance and reinsurance broker,
Lloyd Thompson, is to retire at the end of the year. He will be replaced by
his namesake and co-founder, John Lloyd.
Peter Lloyd is 55, and will advise the group on its Norwegian marine
business, while John Lloyd, who has held several senior positions within the
group, will maintain his responsibilities for the group's energy accounts.
