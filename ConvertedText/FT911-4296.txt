FT  19 APR 91 / International Company News: Selling costs hurt Tandem
Computers


   By LOUISE KEHOE


TANDEM Computers, the manufacturer of fault-tolerant computers, reported
lower second-quarter earnings on increased revenues.
Revenues for the quarter rose 8 per cent to Dollars 489.4m, from Dollars
451.3m last time. Net income was Dollars 18.0m, or 17 cents a share, down
from Dollars 26.8m, or 25 cents.
Seagate Technology, the leading supplier of personal computer disk drives,
reported disappointing third-quarter results, sending its share price
sliding 23 per cent from a Wednesday close of Dollars 18 1/8 to trade at
Dollars 13 7/8 late yesterday.
Sales for the quarter were Dollars 676.6m, compared with Dollars 676m in the
same period last year. Net income fell to Dollars 25.3m, or 38 cents, from
Dollars 28.3m, or 44 cents.
