FT  07 JAN 94 / International Company News: Valeo sees 3% reduction in sales


   By AGENCIES


Valeo, the French car parts company, expects consolidated 1993 sales to dip
3 per cent to FFr20bn (Dollars 3.4bn) from FFr20.6bn in 1992, agencies
report.
European sales fell 10 per cent but rose 39 per cent in North and South
America and 21 per cent in Asia. Those markets account for 22 per cent of
Valeo's 1993 business, against 16 per cent in 1992.
