FT  14 APR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves


   By JOEL KIBAZO, PETER JOHN, CHRISTOPHER PRICE and STEVE THOMPSON


A profit warning from Sims Food, the meat and poultry supplier, sent the
shares sharply lower, closing 21 down at 82p. The company said gross margins
had come under unexpected pressure in the first quarter but that it expected
to make a small profit for the year as a whole.
