FT  05 JUN 92 / Food for Britain celebrates government aid


Rich pickings: government aid to expand fruit production, particularly
apples, would pay handsome dividends, Food from Britain, which promotes
British produce, said yesterday. The fruit trade gap last year accounted for
Pounds 1.7bn of the Pounds 5.5bn food and drink trade deficit.
