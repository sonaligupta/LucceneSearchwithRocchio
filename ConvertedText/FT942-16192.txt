FT  12 APR 94 / Funerals cost most in south-east


The south-east of England is the most expensive place in the UK to die, with
funerals costing twice as much as in East Anglia, the cheapest region, says
a report published today by Mintel, the market research organisation.
The average cost of a funeral in the south-east is Pounds 1,165, while in
East Anglia it is Pounds 877.
