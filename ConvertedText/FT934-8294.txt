FT  16 NOV 93 / International Company News: Power Corp up


   By ROBERT GIBBENS


   MONTREAL


POWER Corp of Canada, the senior holding company of financier Mr Paul
Desmarais, posted third-quarter net profit of CDollars 32.6m (USDollars
24.8m), or 25 cents a share, against CDollars 32.3m, or 24 cents, a year
ago, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Nine-month profit was CDollars
101.9m, or 79 cents, against CDollars 135m, or CDollars 1.03 a share.
