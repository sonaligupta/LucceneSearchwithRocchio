FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Seeboard expands with Pounds 4m acquisition


Seeboard, the electricity supplier for south-east England, has agreed the
purchase of Nene Electrical Installations from the Nene Group for a total
not exceeding Pounds 4m cash.
Nene Electrical, a medium-sized electrical installation company based in
Daventry, Northamptonshire, had turnover of Pounds 12.4m in the year to
end-March 1991. Profits after tax were Pounds 560,000.
