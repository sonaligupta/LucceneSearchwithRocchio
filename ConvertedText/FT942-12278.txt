FT  30 APR 94 / B&C writ issued


Barclays Bank yesterday said the administrators of British & Commonwealth
Holdings, the collapsed financial services group, had issued proceedings
against BZW, Barclays securities arm, for an unspecified amount. The action
is in connection with B&C's acquisition of Atlantic Computers.
