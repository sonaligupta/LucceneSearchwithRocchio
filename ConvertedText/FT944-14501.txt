FT  22 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Govett Strategic


Net assets of Govett Strategic Investment Trust dropped from 298.07p to
271.14p per share in the year ended September 30.
Net available revenue was Pounds 6.69m (Pounds 7.03m) and earnings per share
came to 6.78p (7.16p). The final dividend was 4.1p (same), for an unchanged
total of 6.75p.
