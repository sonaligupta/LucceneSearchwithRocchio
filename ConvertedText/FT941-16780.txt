FT  07 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: 40 die in Togo fighting


At least 40 people were killed in renewed fighting in Togo's capital, Lome.
The government imposed a curfew and closed the west African country's land
borders, later announcing a postponement of parliamentary elections due on
January 23.
