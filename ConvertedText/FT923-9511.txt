FT  05 AUG 92 / Reserves fall by underlying Dollars 86m


THE UK's gold and currency reserves fell an underlying Dollars 86m in July,
indicating small-scale intervention by the Bank of England to support
sterling in the exchange rate mechanism of the European Monetary System last
month.
The Treasury said that stripping out proceeds from the privatisation of
British Telecommunications, the official reserves fell a net Dollars 296m.
The overall level of official reserves, which includes borrowings in foreign
currencies and the Ecu and official repayments, rose by Dollars 50m to
Pounds 45.7bn at the end of last month.
