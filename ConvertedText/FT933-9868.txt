FT  07 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Troops move into townships


Convoys of armoured cars rolled into troubled black townships east of
Johannesburg as the South African government attempted to restore order
after a week in which 143 people died in fighting. In the latest violence,
five commuters were killed on a train.
