FT  21 DEC 92 / Scandic Crown hotel a blaze


Nearly 200 guests were evacuated before dawn yesterday in sub-zero
temperatures as firefighters tackled a blaze at the Scandic Crown hotel in
Edinburgh's Royal Mile. Police said no one had been hurt. The hotel, opened
just two years ago, was designed to blend with other historic buildings in
the street and last week housed the Danish delegation to the EC summit in
the Scottish capital
