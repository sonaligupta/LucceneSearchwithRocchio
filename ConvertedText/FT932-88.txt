FT  30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Feedback shares advance 17p


Shares in Feedback, the USM-quoted high technology equipment manufacturer,
rose 17p to 65p after the company reported a continuing recovery and a
return to the dividend list.
On turnover of Pounds 9.84m (Pounds 9.13m) pre-tax profits were Pounds 1.06m
for the year to March 31, against losses of Pounds 366,200. The company
returned to profits at the interim stage.
Earnings per share came out at 7.66p (loses 3.37p) and a final dividend of
1.5p is recommended.
The company said the turnround was achieved by cost reductions and firm
financial control.
