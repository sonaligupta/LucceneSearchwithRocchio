FT  12 MAR 94 / Finance and the Family: New investment trust - At a glance


Scottish Value Management is launching the Undervalued Assets investment
trust, which aims to achieve long-term capital growth by investing in
undervalued UK commercial and industrial companies. The value management
technique involves detailed analysis of accounting data to identify
companies with better prospects than the stock market sees.
