FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Holders Technology 27% ahead


HOLDERS Technology, a supplier of tools, materials and equipment to the
printed circuit board industry, achieved a 27 per cent improvement in
pre-tax profits to Pounds 251,000 for the half year ended May 31.
Turnover of the USM-quoted company rose from Pounds 1.65m to Pounds 1.99m.
Earnings increased to 5.39p (4.23p) and the interim dividend is 2p (same).
The shares advanced 4p to 132p.
