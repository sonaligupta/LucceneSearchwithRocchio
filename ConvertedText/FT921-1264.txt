FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: EBC down 42%


A 42 per cent fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.51m to Pounds 2.04m was
announced by EBC Group for the year to December 31.
A recommended unchanged final dividend of 4.5p maintains the total at 8p.
Earnings per share dropped from 18.99p to 12.17p.
Turnover was Pounds 60m (Pounds 65.2m).
