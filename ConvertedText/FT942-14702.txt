FT  19 APR 94 / UK Company News: HCG Lloyd's net asset value at 101.4p


HCG Lloyd's Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 101.4p per share
as at December 31.
Net revenue for the period from incorporation on May 27 to the year-end
amounted to Pounds 199,000 for earnings of 1.19p per share.
A final of 0.3p is recommended.
