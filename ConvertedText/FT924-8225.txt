FT  12 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value slips at Fleming High


Net asset value per share at Fleming High Income Investment Trust was 85.4p
at October 31, against 88.7p at the April year end and 86.6p 12 months
earlier.
Pre-tax revenue for the six months fell from Pounds 1.24m to Pounds 1.18m.
Earnings worked through at 2.82p (2.98p) and the second interim is
maintained at 1.45p making a total for the six months of 2.9p (same).
