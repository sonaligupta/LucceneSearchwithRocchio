FT  28 APR 94 / Sharp decline in Japanese output


   By PAUL ABRAHAMS


   TOKYO


Japan's industrial production fell 3.1 per cent last month compared with
March last year, the Ministry of International Trade and Industry said
yesterday, writes Paul Abrahams in Tokyo.
The ministry also published figures showing sales by Japan's large retailers
fell 3 per cent in March compared with the same month last year. Industrial
production, compared with the previous month, was 4 per cent up, but the
ministry warned it would show declines during April and May.
