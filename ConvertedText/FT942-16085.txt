FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wilshaw purchase


Wilshaw, the specialist metals and building products group, is expanding its
distribution activities with the acquisition of Agripieces Sud-Ouest
Distribution for a maximum of FFr15m (Pounds 1.75m) in shares.
The cost is made up of FFr900,000, the consolidated net assets at March 31,
1994, and a profit-related sum.
Agripieces reported 1993 profits of FFr2.84m and net assets at the end of
the period of FFr4.8m.
As part of the deal Wilshaw is to place 1.55m shares at 43p. Shares closed
up 1/2 p at 45p.
