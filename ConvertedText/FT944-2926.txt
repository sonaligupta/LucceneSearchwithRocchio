FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Bogod setback


Bogod Group, which sells sewing machines and accessories, reported the
anticipated decline in interim profits but said that trading had improved in
the third quarter.
On turnover of Pounds 3.11m (Pounds 3.04m) pre-tax profits for the half year
to September 30 amounted to Pounds 59,000 (Pounds 74,000).
Directors said the company was unlikely to match the outcome of last year's
second half.
Earnings fell to 0.7p (1.26p); interim dividends were held at 0.1p per
ordinary and 0.2p per A restricted voting share.
