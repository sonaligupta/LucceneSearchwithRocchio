FT  13 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


Shares lost ground on a combination of financial rand strength and gold
price weakness. The overall index slipped 73 to 4,937, industrials 56 to
5,701 and gold shares 94 to 1,916. Among individual stocks, De Beers fell
R3.50 to R105 and Anglos R4 to R204. Gold Fields firmed R1 to R99 following
a strong first-quarter performance from its four gold mining companies.
