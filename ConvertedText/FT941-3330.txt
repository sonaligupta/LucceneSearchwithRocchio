FT  16 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Castle Comms rises 22%


Castle Communications, which is involved in marketing audio and video film
rights, achieved a 22 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits from Pounds
927,000 to Pounds 1.13m, for the six months to December 1993.
Mr Terry Shand, chairman, said the company had been helped by substantial
increases in licensing income and video product sales, and good growth in
the German subsidiary.
Turnover advanced by 10.5 per cent to Pounds 18.8m (Pounds 17m).
After taking into account losses at Castle Australia, since sold, operating
profits fell to Pounds 1.39m (Pounds 1.47m).
The interim dividend is lifted to 4.5p (4p), payable from earnings of 10.2p
(8.2p) per share.
The company's shares trade on the USM.
