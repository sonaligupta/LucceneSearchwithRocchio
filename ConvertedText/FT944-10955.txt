FT  08 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: Venezeula


Caracas lost 4 per cent as local investors sold Electricidad de Caracas
ahead of its forthcoming subscription of new shares. There was also
continued negative reaction to the government's policy on repatriation of
profits, announced at the end of last week. The Merin-vest composite index
fell 6.06 to 132.78, its lowest level since August 11.
Electricidad rallied just before the close to finish a net 9 bolivars down
at 265 bolivars.
