FT  22 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Elbief


Elbief, the Birmingham-based manufacturer of photograph and handbag frames,
clocks and mirrors, yesterday announced a sharp reduction in annual losses
after the improvement shown in the first half was sustained after the
Christmas period.
On turnover ahead from Pounds 3.74m to Pounds 4.19m, the pre-tax deficit for
the year to April 30 was cut from Pounds 592,000 to Pounds 257,000.
Losses per share were 2p (4.3p).
