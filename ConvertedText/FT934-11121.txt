FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Vinten Group


VINTEN GROUP has completed the sale of the UK electro-optics business of its
subsidiary, Vinten Electro-Optics Ltd, to Coherent Optics (Europe), an
offshoot of Coherent, the US laser and optics company. Consideration is an
estimated Pounds 996,000, payable in cash.
