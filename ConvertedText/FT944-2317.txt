FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Moorgate Smaller


Moorgate Smaller Companies Income Trust reported net revenue unchanged at
Pounds 1.58m for the six months to October 31 giving earnings per share of
2.31p. The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.8p.
Net asset value per share was 127.7p at October 31, down from 143.3p at the
April 30 year-end and 132.5p a year earlier.
