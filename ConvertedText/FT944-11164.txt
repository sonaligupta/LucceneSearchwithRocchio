FT  08 NOV 94 / India opens directorships


   By SHIRAZ SIDHVA


   NEW DELHI


The Indian finance ministry will allow foreigners, including Indians
residing outside the country, to become directors of joint venture
stockbroking companies, it said yesterday. The ministry said clauses of the
Securities Contracts Rules, 1957, would be amended 'to allow joint-venture
stock broking companies to have non-Indian citizens on their boards of
directors'.
