FT  24 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Fiegel has proxies


Mr Gideon Fiegel, founder of the Amstrad Shareholders Club opposed to Mr
Alan Sugar's buyback, has proxies for about 2m of Amstrad's 581m shares. The
Takeover Panel asked him to clarify this after he said investors with 25m
Amstrad shares backed him.
