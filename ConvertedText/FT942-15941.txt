FT  13 APR 94 / Properties sold for Pounds 27m


IM Properties, a subsidiary of the Midlands-based IM Group motor business,
has sold part of a portfolio of industrial properties to Standard Life
Assurance for Pounds 27m.
IM Properties also announced the acquisition of a former GEC factory site in
Witton, Birmingham, from Ascot Holdings. It plans to develop a 500,000 sq ft
industrial park alongside the M6 at Spaghetti Junction.
