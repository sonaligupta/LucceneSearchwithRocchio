FT  24 MAR 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: SA Maize Board first officical
estimate


   By REUTER


   JOHANNESBURG


The South African Maize Board's first official estimate of the 1991-92 maize
crop is 2.1m tonnes, reports Reuter from Johannesburg. The board expects to
have to import 5m tonnes of maize as a result of the drought-reduced crop.
