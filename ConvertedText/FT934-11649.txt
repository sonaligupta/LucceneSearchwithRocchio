FT  30 OCT 93 / Rise in visitors to tourist attractions


THE NUMBER of visitors to tourist attractions rose 1 per cent to 357m last
year, with revenues up 6 per cent to Pounds 930m, the English Tourist Board
says.
Numbers were boosted by nearly 100 new attractions. The most popular new
attraction was Liverpool's Pleasure Island.
