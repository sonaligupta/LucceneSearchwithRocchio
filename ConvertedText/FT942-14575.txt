FT  20 APR 94 / Iran bomb blast


   By AP


   NICOSIA


A bomb exploded in a busy Tehran street yesterday, wounding 13 people, AP
reports from Nicosia. Iran's official Islamic Republic News Agency said the
bomb was probably planted near a pedestrian overpass at a down-town
crossroads. State-run radio blamed 'Iraqi infiltrators'.
