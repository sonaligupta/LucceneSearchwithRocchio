FT  19 APR 94 / UK Company News: Warner Estate property deals


Warner Estate Holdings has bought 19 properties from Sydney and London
Properties for about Pounds 39m cash.
It is selling on 10 of the properties for more than Pounds 23m.
The retained portfolio comprises a mixture of office and industrial premises
with one retail outlet in locations throughout England and Wales. Their
current net rental income totals more than Pounds 1.4m.
