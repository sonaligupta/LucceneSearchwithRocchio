FT  14 JUN 93 / World News In Brief: Lamont in bomb scare


Former chancellor Norman Lamont took a package to police in Notting Hill,
west London, believing it could be a bomb, Scotland Yard said. The area
round the police station was sealed for 30 minutes before the package was
found to contain a compact disc.
