FT  06 JUN 92 / Finance and the Family: At a glance - Smaller companies
slide


Smaller companies had a bad week. The County NatWest Index fell 0.5 per cent
from 1038.23 to 1032.95 in the seven days to June 4; the Hoare Govett Small
Companies Index (capital gains version) fell 0.8 per cent from 1309.3 to
1298.58 over the same period.
