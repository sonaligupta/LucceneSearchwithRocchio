FT  19 APR 94 / Ferry service to be relaunched


Ferry services between Folkestone and Boulogne are to resume after more than
two years.
Folkestone Boulogne Ferries has been formed to provide a three-times-a-day
service for passengers and cars from early June using a chartered 4,500
tonne German-owned vessel.
