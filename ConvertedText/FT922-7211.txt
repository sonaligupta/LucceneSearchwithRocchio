FT  20 MAY 92 / Swedish tax cuts


   By ROBERT TAYLOR


   STOCKHOLM


The Swedish government is to lower corporate tax on dividends, interest
payments and capital gains from 30 per cent to 25, writes Robert Taylor in
Stockholm. Companies will also be able to set off for tax purposes current
losses against profits made in earlier years.
