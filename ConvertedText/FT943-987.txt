FT  27 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Asahi to import US beer cans


   By REUTER


   TOKYO


Asahi Breweries of Japan is to import beer cans produced by Ball Corporation
of Indiana in the US on a trial basis, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Asahi,
Japan's second-largest beer brewer, will import 500,000 350ml beer cans
produced by Ball by the end of September.
