FT  06 AUG 93 / Gas deal signed


BRITISH GAS yesterday signed a IPounds 6m (Pounds 5.71m) one-year contract
to supply the Irish Republic in its first deal to sell gas outside the UK.
The gas will be supplied through a pipeline being built between Moffat,
Dumfries and Galloway, and Loughshinny, north of Dublin, by the Republic's
Bord Gais.
