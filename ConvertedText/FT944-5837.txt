FT  01 DEC 94 / International Capital Markets: Roadshows for Ammeberg offer
start this week


   By MARTIN BRICE


Roadshows started this week for the international offering of shares in
Ammeberg, the Swedish mining group, writes Martin Brice.
The offering is led by Morgan Stanley. Co-leads are BZW, Goldman Sachs and
Enskilda, which is also the lead for the 30 per cent of the offering for
domestic investors.
The roadshow started in Stockholm on Monday, in Frankfurt and Zurich on
Tuesday, and London yesterday and today. It moves on to Geneva and Rotterdam
on Friday, and Scotland on Monday, before going to the US.
The offering values the whole company at around SKr1.26bn.
