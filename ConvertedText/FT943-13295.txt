FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Batleys shows decline to Pounds 5.74m


Batleys, the private Huddersfield-based cash and carry wholesale chain,
reported a decline in pre-tax profits from Pounds 6.53m to Pounds 5.74m in
the year to April 30.
Turnover fell to Pounds 467m (Pounds 478m) generating operating profits of
Pounds 6.36m (Pounds 7.05m).
Earnings per share slipped to 25.04p (28.06p).
