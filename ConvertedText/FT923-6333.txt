FT  25 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Eng & Caledonian


The net asset value of English & Caledonian Investment was 181.8p per share
at June 30  - a decline of 6 per cent over the year.
Net revenue amounted to Pounds 245,000, up from Pounds 134,000, for earnings
of 5.46p (2.99p) per share. As already announced, a final dividend of 2.5p
brings the total for the year to 3.75p (3.15p).
