FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: British Assets


Net asset value at the Ivory & Sime-managed British Assets Trust rose from
89.85p to 109.11p over the year to September 30.
Net revenue amounted to Pounds 16.2m (Pounds 15.6m). A proposed final
dividend of 1.07p lifts the total to 4.28p (4.19p), again uncovered by
earnings of 4.16p (4.02p) per share.
