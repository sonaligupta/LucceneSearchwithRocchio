FT  27 JAN 94 / Australian's arrest sought


   By NIKKI TAIT


   SYDNEY


A Brisbane district court judge has issued a warrant for the arrest of Mr
Christopher Skase, the failed Australian businessman, at present living in
Spain, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney.
Mr Skase faces 32 charges brought by the Australian Securities Commission
and related to the management of his now-defunct Qintex media group.
