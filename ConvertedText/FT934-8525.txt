FT  15 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Emerging asset value 103.9p


Kleinwort Emerging Markets Trust, which came to the market in July, reported
a net asset value per share of 103.9p fully diluted at September 30.
For the period from incorporation on June 21 to September 30 net revenue
amounted to Pounds 19,000 for earnings of 0.07p.
The directors reiterated that the objective of achieving long-term capital
growth meant it was unlikely dividends would be significant but that any
dividend would be in the form of a single payment in August each year.
