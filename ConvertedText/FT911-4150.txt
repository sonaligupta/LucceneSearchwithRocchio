FT  20 APR 91 / Norway pipeline


   By DEBORAH HARGREAVES


Norway's cabinet yesterday approved the construction of a third gas pipeline
to Europe. The Europipe, which will cost NKr10.2bn (Pounds 880m), will have
an initial capacity for transporting 13bn cubic metres of gas a year to
Emden in Germany, reports Deborah Hargreaves. The project now has to be
approved by the country's parliament.
