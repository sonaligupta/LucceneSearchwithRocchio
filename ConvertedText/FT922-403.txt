FT  27 JUN 92 / Dismissed dockers win compensation


ONE HUNDRED Port of London dockers dismissed during the 1989 national dock
strike have won a total of Pounds 1.03m compensation after recent
settlements, the TGWU general union said yesterday.
The figure does not include amounts won by 19 shop stewards found to have
been unfairly dismissed.
