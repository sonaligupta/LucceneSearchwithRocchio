FT  01 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Finsbury issues Lloyd's prospectus


Finsbury Underwriting Investment Trust, one of the funds set up to invest in
the Lloyd's insurance market, has issued its full prospectus.
Up to 30m shares are on offer at 100p each.
The issue will raise Pounds 28.2m after expenses. Net assets per share after
the issue are estimated at 94p.
Rea Brothers and UBS are co-sponsors to the issue, which is being undertaken
as a placing and intermediaries offer. The insurance adviser is Wren
Underwriting Agencies; the investment manager is Finsbury Asset Management.
