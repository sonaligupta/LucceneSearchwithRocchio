FT  25 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: Malaria drug


Australian scientists said they had developed a process to boost production
from a plant known to alleviate malaria. Chinese production of the plant
Qing Hao cannot meet demand. Rhone Poulenc of France holds the patents on
the Australian process.
