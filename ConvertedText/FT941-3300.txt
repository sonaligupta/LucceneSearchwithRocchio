FT  16 MAR 94 / Survey of Information and Communications Technology (31):
Outsourcing contract with Ford of Europe


   By MICHAEL WILTSHIRE


Ford of Europe's parts and services operations division has signed a
five-year outsourcing contract with Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC), one
of the world's leading suppliers of IT services.
CSC will provide Ford with IT services throughout Europe and take
responsibility for applications development, sales analysis and accounting.
CSC won almost Pounds 1bn of outsoucing business in Europe last year.
