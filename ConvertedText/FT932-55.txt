FT  30 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton


Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 84 tonnes for the week ended
June 25 against 38 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not
bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles,
notably in the Middle Eastern range.
