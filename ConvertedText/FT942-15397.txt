FT  15 APR 94 / People: VSEL Consortium


Noel Davies, chief executive of VSEL Consortium, has been elected president
of the Engineering Employers' Federation for a two-year term. He succeeds
Graham Mackenzie, who will continue as acting director-general until a
replacement is appointed. The previous director-general, Neil Johnson, left
last autumn.
