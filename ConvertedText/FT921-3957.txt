FT  12 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: 'Peace boat' ejected


Australia welcomed the retreat of a Portuguese 'peace boat' which returned
to Darwin after being ordered out of Indonesian waters by a naval patrol.
The 120 activists on board were protesting against Indonesia's treatment of
East Timor.
