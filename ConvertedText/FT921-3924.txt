FT  12 MAR 92 / Natwest plans Princes Street refurbishment


Fiscal facelift: NatWest plans a Pounds 20m refurbishment of 1 Princes
Street, a listed 72,000 sq ft office building in the City of London next to
the Bank of England. NatWest moved out nine months ago but has not yet
decided whether to return when work has finished.
