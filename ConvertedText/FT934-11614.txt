FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Broadgate Inv Trust


Broadgate Investment Trust, which came to the market in August 1992 to
manage a limited portfolio of the largest capitalised UK companies,
yesterday announced results from April 14 1992 - the date of incorporation -
to end-September 1993.
Since August last year, net asset value had risen more than 29 per cent to
119.68p.
A dividend of 1.8p is payable from earnings of 2.16p.
