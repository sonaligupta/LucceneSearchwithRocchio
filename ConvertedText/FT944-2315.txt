FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Total Systems down


Total Systems, the USM-traded computer services group, reported a 39 per
cent drop in pre-tax profits for the half-year to September 30.
The outcome of Pounds 7,460 (Pounds 12,330) was struck on turnover slightly
ahead at Pounds 1.1m (Pounds 1.08m). Mr Terry Bourne, chairman, said the
core business had continued to be profitable in a 'difficult' market, but
investment in package systems had impacted on results.
Earnings per share halved to 0.039p.
