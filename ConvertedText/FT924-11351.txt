FT  27 OCT 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Indian aluminium - Correction


IT WAS stated on this page on October 15 that National Aluminium Company was
the only Indian aluminium producer to have its brand recognised by the
London Metal Exchange. In fact, Bahrat Aluminium Company, Indian Aluminium
Company, Hindalco and Aluminium Corporation of India all have brands
registered as 'good delivery' against LME contracts.
