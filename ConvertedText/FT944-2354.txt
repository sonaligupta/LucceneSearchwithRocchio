FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Optometrics


The sudden demise of one of its larger components customers hit interim
results of Optometrics Corp, the USM-quoted optical systems group. On sales
down from Dollars 1.88m to Dollars 1.72m (Pounds 1.04m), pre-tax profits in
the period to September 30 declined by Dollars 58,000 to Dollars 40,000.
Earnings per share came to 30 cents (70 cents).
