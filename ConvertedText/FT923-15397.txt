FT  02 JUL 92 / Fresh clashes break out in Kabul


   By REUTER


   KABUL


Fighting broke out between rival factions in the Afghan capital Kabul
yesterday, shattering six days of relative calm, Reuter reports from Kabul.
The military hospital confirmed one dead and one wounded in the Sara Chowk
bazaar district.
