FT  27 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Children afraid of parents' driving


British children believe nine out of 10 adults drive badly. According to a
survey of 10-11 year olds, adults speed, swear and shout. They don't wear
seat belts, 'cut up' other drivers, and drink and drive.
