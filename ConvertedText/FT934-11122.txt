FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Southern Water


SOUTHERN WATER has acquired the ERG Environmental Resource Group for Pounds
2.25m. The consideration, in part via the issue of 311,858 new ordinary
shares, is subject to adjustment. In 1992 ERG achieved a turnover of Pounds
7m, of which a third was derived from outside the UK.
