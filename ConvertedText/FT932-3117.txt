FT  16 JUN 93 / Cabinet filled in Venezuela


   By JOSEPH MANN


   CARACAS


MR Ramon Velasquez, interim president of Venezuela, has appointed two
prominent business figures to his cabinet, reports Joseph Mann from Caracas.
Mr Hernan Anzola, central bank president in previous administrations, is
planning minister; Mr Gustavo Perez Mijares is economic development
minister. Mr Velasquez has now completed his 19-member cabinet. It retains
five who worked under Mr Carlos Andres Perez, who is suspended from the
presidency to face corruption charges.
