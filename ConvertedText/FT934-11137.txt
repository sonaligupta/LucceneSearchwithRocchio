FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Loss at Westminster Scaffolding rises


Losses at the USM-quoted Westminster Scaffolding Group rose from Pounds
524,000 to Pounds 1.66m pre-tax for the half year to April 30. The deficit
per share rose by 1.9p to 3.2p.
In consideration for the purchase from the Rigblast Group of scaffolding
worth Pounds 150,000 Westminster is issuing 1.5m new ordinary 10p shares at
par. The issue represents 2.8 per cent of the ordinary equity.
