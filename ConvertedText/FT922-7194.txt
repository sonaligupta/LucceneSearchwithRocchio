FT  20 MAY 92 / Barclays IT staff to receive pay rise


MORE than 2,500 information technology staff at Barclays Bank will receive
pay rises of between 3 per cent and 4 per cent. Managers will get a maximum
of 3.1 per cent, while junior staff will receive between 3 per cent and 4
per cent.
