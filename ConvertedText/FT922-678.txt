FT  26 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Gartmore Value asset value falls


Gartmore Value Investments had a net asset value of 27.4p at April 30
against 35.5p a year earlier. At June 24 the value was 21.4p.
Pre-tax profits for the year were unchanged at Pounds 2.9m.
Earnings per share emerged at 3.81p (4.18p) and the fourth interim dividend,
already declared, is held at 1.5p for a total of 4.275p (4.2p).
