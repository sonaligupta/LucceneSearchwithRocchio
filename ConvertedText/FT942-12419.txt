FT  29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Sykes meeting is adjourned


The egm called to consider the rebel shareholders' latest bid to oust the
board of Andrews Sykes was adjourned yesterday after a number of proxy cards
received were discovered to be invalid.
A proposal to adjourn the meeting until May 12, was carried.
