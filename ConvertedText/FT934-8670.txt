FT  13 NOV 93 / Dutch counterfeit haul


   By REUTER


   AMSTERDAM


Dutch police said on Friday they had seized Dollars 10m in high quality
forged dollar bills from a warehouse near Edam in the northern Netherlands,
Reuters reports from Amsterdam.
Police said they had arrested six people after acting on a tip-off and
finding hundreds of fake Dollars 100 bills packed inside sports bags.
Paper and ink of exceptional quality had been used to make the bills, they
said. Printing equipment and a large amount of special Ink were also found
in the warehouse.
