FT  21 NOV 92 / Chile in Dollars 450m iron ore deal


   By LESLIE CRAWFORD


   SANTIAGO


Compania Minera del Pacifico, part of Chile's privatised iron and steel
group CAP, has clinched a Dollars 450m (Pounds 298m) contract to supply 20m
tonnes of iron ore products to Japanese steel mills over the next five
years, Leslie Crawford writes from Santiago.
