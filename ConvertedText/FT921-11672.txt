FT  30 JAN 92 / Redundancies at Easington colliery


BRITISH COAL yesterday announced that 128 jobs are to go at Easington
colliery, Co Durham, following talks with the mining unions.
The pit, one of only five left in north-east England, has made a loss of
more than Pounds 4m since April. British Coal said the job losses would
reduce manpower at the colliery to 1,350.
