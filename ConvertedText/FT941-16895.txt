FT  07 JAN 94 / People: QS Holdings


Marc Walters has relinquished his role as group md of of QS HOLDINGS because
of continuing ill health, but remains chairman. Neil Wanstall, formerly
retail director, becomes group md and David Heywood, already a non-executive
director and chairman of Remploy, is appointed deputy chairman.
