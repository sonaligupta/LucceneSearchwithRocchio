FT  13 APR 94 / UK Company News: Lopex Pounds 502,000 in the black


urnround in the UK helped Lopex, the advertising and marketing services
company, achieve pre-tax profits of Pounds 502,000 for the 1993 year,
against losses of Pounds 372,000 in the previous 12 months, restated for FRS

The result was struck after a Pounds 327,000 deficit on the disposal of
Alliance, the lossmaking London advertising agency.
Turnover increased by 2 per cent to Pounds 142.4m.
The company is returning to the dividend list for the first time since 1990
with a proposed final of 0.25p, payable from earnings of 0.3p (losses of
2.53p) per share.
