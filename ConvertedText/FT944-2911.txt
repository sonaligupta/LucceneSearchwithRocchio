FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Memory placing


Memory Corporation, which has developed a technology which cuts the cost of
producing computer memory chips, has placed 13.5 per cent of its equity at
45p per share. Market capitalisation will be Pounds 26.6m when trading
begins under Rule 4.2 on December 21.
