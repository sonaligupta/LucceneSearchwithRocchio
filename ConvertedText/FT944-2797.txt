FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Polar


Pre-tax profit of this USM-quoted electronic components company increased 43
per cent from Pounds 1.31m to Pounds 1.87m in the year to September 30.
Sales rose 30 per cent to Pounds 27.6m (Pounds 21.2m) and operating margins
grew from 6.2 to 6.8 per cent. The increased final dividend of 3.1p makes a
total of 5.4p (4.85p). The shares yesterday added 10p to 263p. Polar aims to
move its shares to the main market in the new year.
