FT  02 JUL 92 / HFC production


IMPERIAL Chemical Industries is to begin the world's first commercial
production of a non-ozone-depleting hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) to replace
ozone-damaging hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs), at a Pounds 4m plant in
Cheshire later this month.
