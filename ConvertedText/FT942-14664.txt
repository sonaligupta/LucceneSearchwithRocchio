FT  19 APR 94 / International Company News: Two bidders for State Bank NSW


   By REUTER


   SYDNEY


Bidders for the State Bank of New South Wales have contracted to two from
six interested parties, the NSW state government said, Reuter reports from
Sydney.
The government put the sale of the bank up for tender in December. The two
remaining bidders - not yet named - have entered the due diligence phase.
