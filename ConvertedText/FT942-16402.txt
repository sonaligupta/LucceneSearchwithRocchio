FT  11 APR 94 / National day nets record Pounds 75m bets


William Hill, the bookmaker, estimated that a record Pounds 75m was gambled
on Saturday, Grand National day.
Its biggest single winner on the race was a customer at one of its betting
shops in Bayswater, central London, who staked Pounds 1,000 cash on 16-1
National winner Miinnehoma.
