FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Mansfield Brewery to buy Courage pubs


Mansfield Brewery has confirmed that it expects to complete the purchase of
86 pubs from Courage by early March, increasing its estate by more than a
quarter.
The pubs are within Mansfield's established trading area in the east
Midlands, Humberside, and South Yorkshire.
Mansfield, which owns 335 pubs, last year paid Pounds 12.6m for 29 houses
which it had operated on lease from Courage.
