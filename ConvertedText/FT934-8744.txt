FT  13 NOV 93 / Markets: Midland launches gold card - At a glance


MIDLAND BANK is launching a gold Visa credit card from Monday with a minimum
credit limit of Pounds 3,000. The APR for purchases will be 19.9 per cent
and the annual fee will be Pounds 35, or Pounds 45 to cover an additional
cardholder. Cardholders can withdraw up to Pounds 500 a day worldwide and
goods or travel paid for with the card will have 90 days purchase protection
and travel accident insurance. There will also be a 24-hour helpline for
travel assistance in an emergency. Applicants must be over 21 years old and
earning Pounds 20,000 a year.
