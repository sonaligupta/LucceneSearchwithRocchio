FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Aran Energy shows decline to IPounds 5,000


Aran Energy, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration company, turned in
sharply reduced pre-tax profits for 1991, down from IPounds 121,000 (Pounds
113,000) to IPounds 5,000.
Turnover was slightly ahead at IPounds 29.9m (IPounds 29.1m).
The company has now arranged a Dollars 197.5m credit facility to finance its
activities until production is on stream.
Losses per share were 0.14p (earnings of 0.09p).
