FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Daks Simpson


DAKS SIMPSON, a subsidiary of Sankyo Seiko, reported pre-tax profits of
Pounds 1.4m on turnover of Pounds 65.5m for the year to January 31 1993. The
year end has been changed and the figures compare with losses of Pounds
525,000 on sales of Pounds 33.3m in the six months to end-January 1992.
