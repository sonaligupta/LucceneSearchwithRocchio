FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Geared Income


At June 30 net asset value per share of Geared Income Investment Trust stood
at 86.1p, a 8 per cent increase on the 79.49p at which it stood a year
earlier.
Net revenue for the three months to end-June amounted to Pounds 358,732
(Pounds 326,367). Earnings per share came out at 1.63p (1.48p) and the first
interim dividend is again 1.5p.
