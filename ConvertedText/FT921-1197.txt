FT  25 MAR 92 / Airlines warn Brussels on fares


   By PAUL BETTS, Aerospace Correspondent


The European Commission's third package of airline deregulation could
backfire and lead to a more restrictive fares regime in Europe, the
Association of European Airlines (AEA) warned yesterday, writes Paul Betts,
Aerospace Correspondent.
