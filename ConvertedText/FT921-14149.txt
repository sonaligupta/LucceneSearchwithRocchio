FT  17 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Northern Industrial makes Pounds 215,000


In the half year to September 30 1991 Northern Industrial Improvement Trust
virtually maintained pre-tax profits at Pounds 215,000, compared with Pounds
220,000.
Earnings per share were 12.41p (12.62p). Directors anticipate that the
interim dividend to be paid in July would be similar to the previous 7p.
Investment income totalled Pounds 190,000 (Pounds 182,000) and net rents
came to Pounds 5,000 (Pounds 8,000). Realised and unrealised surpluses on
sales and revaluations of properties and investment were credited direct to
general reserve.
