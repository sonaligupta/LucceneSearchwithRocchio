FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Ashtead Group


Ashtead Group has acquired the hire activities of Steve Vick International
for Pounds 200,000 cash. Annualised turnover of the assets is about Pounds
480,000. Ashtead will also become sole distributor for Steve Vick's rental
equipment.
