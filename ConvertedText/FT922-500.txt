FT  27 JUN 92 / International Company News: Pricing set for AFC's initial
public offering


AUTHENTIC Fitness Corporation (AFC), Pentland's US affiliate, is pricing its
initial public offering at Dollars 14 a share. This is at the bottom of
expectations.
AFC, which holds the licence for Speedo swimwear and accessories in the US,
will use the Dollars 37.7m proceeds from the issue to reduce debt.
Pentland's stake in AFC will fall to 26.2 per cent, worth about Dollars 34m
at the issue price.
