FT  06 JAN 94 / People: Rolls-Royce


Richard Maudslay, managing director of Rolls-Royce Industrial Power Group,
has been appointed to the main board of Rolls-Royce.
Maudslay, 47, has risen swiftly since the takeover in 1989 of Northern
Engineering Industries by Rolls-Royce. Before becoming md in 1992 of the
Industrial Power Group, an amalgamation of Rolls-Royce's non-aerospace
operations and NEI, he was md of turbine generator manufacturer NEI Parsons.
He began his career with Bruce Peebles, part of NEI, in 1968 and has
subsequently held a wide range of UK and overseas appointments within NEI.
