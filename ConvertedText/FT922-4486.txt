FT  05 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Brit Syphon lower at Pounds 5.91m


BRITISH SYPHON Industries, the engineering and polymer processing group,
reported profits of Pounds 5.91m pre-tax for the 1991 year.
The outcome, achieved on turnover of Pounds 46.1m, compared with Pounds
8.26m for the previous 16 month period when turnover totalled Pounds 64.9m.
Earnings per share worked through at 11.9p (21.2p). A final dividend of 3p
is proposed.
