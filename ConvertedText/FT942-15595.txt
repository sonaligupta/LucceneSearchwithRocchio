FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: PizzaExpress chief sells 2m shares


Mr Peter Boizot, founder and non-executive chairman of PizzaExpress, the
pizza restaurant chain floated in February 1993, sold 2m of its shares on
Tuesday at 120p each, representing 3.8 per cent of the issued capital.
The sale reduces his stake to 5.72m ordinary shares, or 10.8 per cent of the
issued capital. Mr Boizot has undertaken not to dispose of any further
shares for at least a year.
The shares were placed with institutional clients of Greig Middleton.
