FT  26 JUN 92 / Bank notes low


BANK OF England figures yesterday disclosed a sharp slowdown in the growth
of bank notes and coins in circulation during June. Notes in circulation
increased by only 0.5 per cent in the week to June 24 compared with the same
week of 1991 after a 0.8 per cent annual increase in the previous week.
