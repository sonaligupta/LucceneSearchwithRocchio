FT  05 MAR 94 / Germans want to ban British meat


   By REUTER


   BONN


German health minister Mr Horst Seehofer wants European Union agriculture
ministers to ban British livestock and beef imports because of the dangers
of 'Mad Cow disease', Reuter reports from Bonn. If a special meeting of the
EU ministers fails to agree an immediate ban, Germany will impose
restrictions.
