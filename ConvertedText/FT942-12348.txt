FT  29 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Former CIA officer is jailed for life


Former US Central Intelligence Agency officer Aldrich Ames (left) was
sentenced to life in prison after he pleaded guilty in Virginia to spying
for Moscow. Ames revealed he was paid Dollars 2m (Pounds 1.3m) for supplying
the former Soviet Union with the names of every Soviet citizen working for
the United States as a spy. He said he regretted his actions. Ames's wife,
Rosario, also pleaded guilty and will be sentenced in August.
