FT  13 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Daks Simpson surges to Pounds 1.21m


Daks Simpson Group, the clothing maker which was acquired by Sankyo Seiko in
1991, announced a surge in pre-tax profits from Pounds 88,000 to Pounds
1.21m for the six months to July 31. Turnover amounted to Pounds 30.3m
against Pounds 29.2m.
Earnings per share worked through at 9.82p (3.19p).
