FT  13 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore offer fully subscribed


THE PUBLIC offer of 15.76m shares in Gartmore, the UK's fourth largest
pension fund management company, has been fully subscribed. Banque Indosuez,
the French bank, is raising Pounds 75.7m via the sale of a 25 per cent stake
in Gartmore.
Valid applications for a total of 18.12m shares were received which
represented a total value of Pounds 30.4m at the offer price of 168p each.
After preferential arrangements for eligible employees in respect of 524,450
shares, allocations were: 200 to 2,000 shares applied for - 100 per cent;
2,500 to 10,000 - 90 per cent; 12,000 to 20,000 - 80 per cent; 25,000 and
over - 70 per cent. A total of 29.27m shares, representing a total value of
Pounds 49.2m at the 168p price, were placed on November 4.
