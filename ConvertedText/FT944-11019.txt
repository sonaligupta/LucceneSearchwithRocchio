FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: REA Pounds 1.3m sale


REA Holdings is to end its involvement in west Africa with the Pounds 1.3m
sale of its 26 per cent interest in Plantation J Eglin, which grows bananas
and pineapples on the Ivory Coast.
The assets being sold are valued at Pounds 1.1m, including loans. They
contributed a loss of Pounds 71,000 in the last audited accounts, but have
since been profitable.
Two thirds of the price is receivable in three months and the balance in
December 1995.
