FT  23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Grahams Rintoul net assets ahead


Grahams Rintoul Investment Trust, which invests mainly in smaller companies
and is ultimately controlled by American Express Bank, had a net asset value
per share of 157p at June 30.
That compared with 113.9p at the December year end and with 133.4p a year
ago.
Net revenue for the half year was Pounds 586,000 (Pounds 427,000) and
earnings per share came out at 0.25p (0.96p losses).
