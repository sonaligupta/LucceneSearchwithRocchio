FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Five Oaks Pounds 2.28m in black


Five Oaks Investments, the property group, swung from an Pounds 851,000 loss
to a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.28m during the year to June 30.
As a result, earnings per share were 2.54p (1.83p losses) and the
recommended final dividend of 0.3p makes a total of 0.5p (nil) for the year.
Rental income grew 20 per cent to Pounds 4.2m, with the current annualised
figure running at Pounds 5.2m.
The balance sheet was strengthened through a share placing and open offer,
which increased the capital base by Pounds 12.2m. Also, a large part of the
short-term debt was converted into a Pounds 15m 25-year secured debenture.
Gearing fell from 106 per cent to 44 per cent at the year end.
