FT  27 SEP 94 / Repubblica reports loss at Independent


Newspaper Publishing, publisher of The Independent, lost Pounds 12.6m in the
first half of this year as a result of restructuring and a fierce price war,
its Italian shareholder La Repubblica said.
La Repubblica, the Italian daily that has a 17.6 per cent stake in the UK
company, released the figure as part of its own first-half results.
