FT  15 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Waverley Mining


WAVERLEY MINING intends to make an offer for the shares in Westralian
Resource Projects it does not already own. Offer will be made on the basis
of three Waverley for every 10 Westralian. The maximum number of new
Waverley shares which could be issued is 7.83m representing 38.5 per cent of
the enlarged Waverley capital.
