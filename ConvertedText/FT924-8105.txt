FT  12 NOV 92 / Cancer fund cuts


THE Imperial Cancer Research Fund is to cut 43 jobs and make savings of
Pounds 7.5m over the next two years, it was announced yesterday. The fund
said the action was necessary to safeguard vital research from the
continuing effects of the recession.
The 43 will retire early or take redundancy.
