FT  02 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Refocused New London advances


New London, the oilfield services group, lifted pre-tax profits by 31 per
cent to Dollars 5.04m (Pounds 2.72m) in the year to March 31, compared with
Dollars 3.84m previously.
Turnover jumped to Dollars 113m (Dollars 44.7m), helped by the acquisition
of International Drilling Fluids last July. Drilling fluids and well
servicing revenues now account for more than 90 per cent of total revenues.
After tax and minorities net profits advanced by 76 per cent to Dollars
3.55m (Dollars 2.01m). Earnings per share were 3.5 cents (3.2 cents).
An extraordinary Dollars 4.81m provision was made against loss on the sale
of its US oil and gas exploration and production operations.
