FT  06 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


GOLD shares moved higher as the bullion price remained above Dollars 370 an
ounce. However, industrial stocks declined in line with falls elsewhere in
the world. Gold rose 92 to 1,890, industrials fell 14 to 4,548 and the
overall gained 26 to 4,004.
