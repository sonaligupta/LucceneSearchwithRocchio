FT  25 MAY 93 / UK Company News: N American Gas Trust assets rise


North American Gas Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 101.68p
per share at April 30, compared with a figure of 58.32p a year earlier.
Net revenue for the nine months to the end of April advanced slightly from
Pounds 457,000 to Pounds 461,000. Earnings per share came out at 1.31p,
compared with 1.3p at the same stage of the 1991-92 year.
