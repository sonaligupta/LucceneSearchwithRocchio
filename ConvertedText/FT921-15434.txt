FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Refuge Group shows improvement


Refuge Group, the Cheshire-based life assurance company, has bucked the
trend for the insurance industry by announcing improved new business
figures.
Sales of single premium pensions increased by 60 per cent to Pounds 52.3m.
Annual premium pensions also increased, from Pounds 2.61m to Pounds 3.32m.
Sales of unit trusts were down heavily, from Pounds 10.75m to Pounds 4.05m,
reflecting an awkward year for the unit trust industry as a whole.
