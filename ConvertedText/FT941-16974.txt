FT  07 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Kidnap father held


Businessman Peter Malkin was arrested at Heathrow Airport as he arrived back
in Britain from Egypt with his 12-year-old son, Oliver, two months after the
kidnap of the boy, a ward of court, from his ex-wife.
