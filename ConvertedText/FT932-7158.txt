FT  25 MAY 93 / International Company News in Brief: IBM


   By Our New York staff


INTERNATIONAL Business Machines intends to issue up to Dollars 3bn of
long-term debt and preferred stock in the next nine to 12 months. It will
also issue depositary shares representing Dollars 750m of perpetual
preferred stock, writes our New York staff.
IBM is to name Ms Abby Kohnstamm 39, to the newly-created post of
vice-president, corporate marketing. She moves from American Express, where
she was vice-president, Cardmember marketing, agencies add.
Mr Lou Gerstner, who became chief executive of IBM in March, is a former
president of American Express.
