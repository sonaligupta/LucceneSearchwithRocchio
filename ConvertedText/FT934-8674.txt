FT  13 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Delors targets interest rates


European Commission president Jacques Delors identified lower interest rates
as the key to starting to solve the EU's jobs crisis. 'The driving force can
only come, in the short term, from a lowering of interest rates,' he told a
German Social Democratic Party conference in Frankfurt.
