FT  16 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Paramount Communications


Paramount Communications, US entertainment group, is recommending rejection
of TV company QVC Network's increased offer of Dollars 10.8bn (Pounds
7.1bn), equal to Dollars 90 a share. The bid tops an Dollars 85-a-share
friendly offer from cable group Viacom.
