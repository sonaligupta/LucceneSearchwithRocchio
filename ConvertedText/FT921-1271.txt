FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Exceptionals leave Bredero in the red


Exceptional write-downs of Pounds 6.52m in its property portfolio left
Bredero Properties with pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.5m for 1991 compared with
profits of Pounds 1.58m. Losses at the half year were Pounds 1.27m.
Turnover fell from Pounds 75.6m to Pounds 53.3m, for operating profits of
Pounds 2.02m (Pounds 3.21m). Losses per share were 13.1p (4.3p earnings) and
the final dividend is passed (1.2p) resulting in a nil distribution (2.4p)
for the year.
Net asset value per share stood at 1.96p at December 31 against 2.19p a year
earlier.
