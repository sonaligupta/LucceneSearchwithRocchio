FT  30 NOV 94 / Dole meets Claes before Bosnia talks


Robert Dole, the US Senate Republican leader, meets Nato secretary general
Willy Claes yesterday before talks in Brussels. Dole later called for an end
to the arms embargo against Bosnia's Moslems as a way of putting pressure on
the Serbs to agree a peace plan.
