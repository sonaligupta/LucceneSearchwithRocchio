FT  22 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Ecu Trust


Net asset value per share of the Ecu Trust fell 6 per cent from 71.3p to 67p
during the 12 months to June 30.
Net revenue for the year was Pounds 224,304 (Pounds 229,476) after an
increased tax charge of Pounds 160,484 (Pounds 73,715). Earnings per share
were 0.75p (0.76p) and a single unchanged final dividend of 0.5p is
proposed.
