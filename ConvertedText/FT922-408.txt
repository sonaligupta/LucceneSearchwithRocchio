FT  27 JUN 92 / Sales boost for unit trusts


UNIT TRUSTS this year enjoyed their best net sales in May since 1987 while
total funds under management reached an all-time high of Pounds 60.3m,
according to the Unit Trust Association.
The association said that May's net sales of Pounds 235m were greater than
the net inflow of Pounds 179m recorded by building societies in the same
month.
