FT  11 NOV 92 / International Company News: Malaysian Airline System's
one-for-one rights issue oversubscribed


   By REUTER


   KUALA LUMPUR


MALAYSIAN Airline System's one-for-one rights issue of 350m MDollars 1
shares at MDollars 5 each has been oversubscribed by 3.9 times, Reuter
reports from Kuala Lumpur.
Aseambankers Malaysia, the adviser and managing underwriter of the issue,
said that at the close of the rights issue on November 2, applications for a
total of 363.66m new MAS shares were received.
The issue, which will double MAS's share capital to MDollars 700m, will
raise MDollars 1.75bn (USDollars 689m) to part-finance its fleet expansion
programme.
