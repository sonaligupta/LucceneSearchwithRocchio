FT  29 JUL 92 / Jobs lost in paint factory closure


AKZO, the Dutch chemicals group, is to shut the Macpherson paint factory in
Bury, Greater Manchester, with the loss of 390 jobs.
Another 110 jobs will also go in a reorganisation of Akzo Coatings
operations in Britain. Production will be transferred to Akzo's factory in
Hull, starting next year.
