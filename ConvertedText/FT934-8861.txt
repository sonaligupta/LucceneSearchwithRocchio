FT  13 NOV 93 / Kazakhstan dumps rouble


   By REUTER


   ALMA ATA


The former Soviet republic of Kazakhstan will dump the rouble on Monday,
bringing in a new currency with full support from the International Monetary
Fund, President Nursultan Nazarbayev said yesterday, Reuter reports from
Alma Ata.
