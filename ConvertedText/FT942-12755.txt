FT  28 APR 94 / Foreign Office to cut 500 posts


The Foreign Office is to cut 500 jobs and is considering sharing some
embassies with Germany because of budget cuts, Sir David Gillmore, a senior
official, said yesterday.
He told the Commons foreign affairs committee inquiry into diplomatic
service finances that the Foreign Office, which has 6,400 staff, planned to
reduce larger posts and cut 500 support staff jobs at home and overseas.
Plans were also being considered to share embassies with Germany in
Azerbaijan, Ecuador and Iceland.
