FT  21 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Birkby buying two more mills for Pounds 2m


Birkby, the Huddersfield-based rental company, has bought two further mills
for conversion. Cost of the acquisitions was Pounds 2.1m.
The purchases are former textile mills in Dewsbury and Rochdale.
