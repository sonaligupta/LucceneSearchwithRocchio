FT  16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: New London Capital


Net asset value per share of New London Capital, the Lloyd's investment
trust which came to the market in November 1993, slipped from 91.2p to 88.7p
during the six months to September 30.
Net revenue amounted to Pounds 780,564 for earnings per share of 1.3p. For
the six months from October 8 1993 to March 31 1994 net revenue was Pounds
539,592, for earnings of 0.9p per share.
A dividend of 0.5p is declared, making 1p so far. The current accounting
period is for the 18 months to end-March 1995.
