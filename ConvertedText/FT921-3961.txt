FT  12 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Kurds killed


Turkish troops killed at least 28 Kurdish separatist guerrillas near the
eastern town of Genc. More than 3,300 people have been killed since 1984
when the Marxist Kurdish Workers party began its campaign for an independent
Kurdish state.
