FT  23 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: High salary for CSO director


MR BILL McLENNAN, director of the government's Central Statistical Office,
earned Pounds 116,610 in his first year in the job, making him one of the
highest paid UK civil servants.
Mr McLennan's salary for the year ending in March was disclosed in the CSO's
annual report released yesterday. It included an allowance of Pounds 33,685
to help with the cost of his move from Australia in February last year.
