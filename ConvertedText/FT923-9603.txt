FT  04 AUG 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


JOHANNESBURG fell in thin trading as the two-day general strike, led by the
ANC, began. The gold index dropped 28 or 2.6 per cent to 1,041, the
industrial index lost 27 to 4,215 and the overall index shed 21 to 3,409.
