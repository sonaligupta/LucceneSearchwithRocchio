FT  25 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Sunleigh reports Pounds 2.04m deficit


Sunleigh, the leisure group rescued last year through a Pounds 3.8m placing,
announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.04m for 1991, a capital restructuring
and the Pounds 1.6m acquisition of a controlling interest in the leading
manufacturer of Laser sailing dinghies.
The purchase of a 63 per cent stake in the ordinary share capital of Gavel
Securities, and a further 72 per cent of the preference shares, will be
funded by a Pounds 1.8m placing and open offer to shareholders at 8p.
The deficit compared with losses last time of Pounds l81m.
Sunleigh also announced a capital restructuring to reduce the deficit on the
profit and loss account.
