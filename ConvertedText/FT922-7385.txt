FT  19 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Impshire reduces losses to IPounds 255,000


Impshire Thoroughbreds, the Irish breeding and racing concern, reported a
reduced loss before tax of IPounds 255,000 (Pounds 232,000 sterling) in the
year to end-December.
This compared with a deficit of IPounds 1.56m last time. Losses per share
were cut from 21.5p to 4.5p.
