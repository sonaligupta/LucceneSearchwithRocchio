FT  13 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Stewart scores second century


West Indies were 47-2 at the close of the fourth day of the fourth cricket
Test at Bridgetown, Barbados, after England declared their second innings at
394-7, setting the home side 446 for victory. Alec Stewart became the first
England player to hit centuries in both innings against West Indies, scoring
143 to follow his 118.
