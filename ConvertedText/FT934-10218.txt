FT  06 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Capital Gearing Trust improves


Capital Gearing Trust raised net asset value per share to 505.4p on October
5, against 443.5p six months earlier.
Losses after tax for the six months to October 5 were cut from Pounds 21,000
to Pounds 15,000, on income up from Pounds 54,000 to Pounds 64,000. The loss
was struck after management costs of Pounds 26,000 (Pounds 24,000) and
interest charges of Pounds 52,000 (Pounds 51,000).
There was a loss per share of 1.34p (1.48p).
The company is anticipating that the single dividend for the year to April 5
will not be less than the 0.43p paid last time.
