FT  26 OCT 92 / International Company News: Losses cut at Conrad Continental


Losses at Conrad Continental, which has interests in leather clothing,
fashion accessories and sports coaching, were cut from Pounds 398,000 to
Pounds 297,000 pre-tax for the half year ended June 30.
The directors said the second half traditionally provided better trading
conditions and added that they were confident of being able to report a
profit for the period.
First half turnover totalled Pounds 2.82m (Pounds 4.57m). Losses per share
emerged at 0.89p (1.43p).
