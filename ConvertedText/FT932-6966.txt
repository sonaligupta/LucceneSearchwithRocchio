FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News: East West Oil raises holding in Aminex


East West Oil, a private company controlled by Russian shareholders, has
increased its stake in Aminex, the Dublin-based oil explorer, from 22.5 per
cent to 25.2 per cent.
The announcement came as Aminex revealed that the deal with Svenska
Petroleum, the Swedish company, to assign a 13 per cent interest in a
Tunisian field in exchange for a 4.5 per cent per cent stake, had been
approved by the Tunis government.
Aminex shares closed yesterday 2 3/4 p higher at 17 3/4 p.
