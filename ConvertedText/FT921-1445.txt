FT  24 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Friedrich von Hayek dies


The economist known as the 'father of monetarism', and a guru of former
prime minister Margaret Thatcher, Professor Friedrick von Hayek, died at his
home in Freiburg in Breisgau, Germany, aged 92.
