FT  05 MAR 94 / Moslems guilty of New York bombing


   By REUTER


   NEW YORK


A New York jury yesterday found four Moslem fundamentalists guilty of
bombing the World Trade Center last year, in which six people died, Reuter
reports from New York. Mohammad Salameh, Nidal Ayyad, Mahmud Abouhalima and
Ahmad Ajaj faced conspiracy and 10 other charges.
