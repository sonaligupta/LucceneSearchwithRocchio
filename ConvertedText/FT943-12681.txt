FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Baring Tribune


Baring Tribune Investment Trust had a net asset value of 367.8p per share at
June 30 - a year-on-year advance of 8 per cent, but a decline of 12 per cent
since the trust's December year end.
The trust, which seeks long-term growth through an international portfolio,
reported net revenue of Pounds 1.16m for the six month period, down from
Pounds 1.79m last time. Earnings per share dipped to 2.26p (3.5p) but the
interim dividend is maintained at 1.7p.
