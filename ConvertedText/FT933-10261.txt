FT  05 AUG 93 / Turning the tide

resorts, according to a report published yesterday by the English Tourist
Board. 'Turning the Tide' urges resorts to make the most of historic
buildings and monuments. The main attraction at Scarborough, appears to be
the sands but on the cliffs above stands an impressive medieval castle
