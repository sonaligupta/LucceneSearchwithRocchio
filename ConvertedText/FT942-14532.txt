FT  20 APR 94 / 'Instant' plants up by 40 per cent


Gardeners are no longer prepared to sow seeds and wait for them to sprout.
Ready-grown flowering plants and shrubs form the fastest-moving sector in
the gardening market, says a study published today by market researcher
Mintel. Spending on 'instant' plants has shot up 40 per cent between 1988
and 1993 to Pounds 1.09bn out of a total gardening market of Pounds 2.6bn
