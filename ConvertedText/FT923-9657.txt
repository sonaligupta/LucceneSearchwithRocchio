FT  04 AUG 92 / International Company News: Synthelabo sales increase to
FFr3.17bn


   By ALICE RAWSTHORN


SYNTHELABO, the French pharmaceuticals company which is part of the L'Oreal
cosmetics group, saw its sales increase by 9.7 per cent to FFr3.17bn
(Dollars 634m) in the first half of this year from FFr2.88bn in the same
period of 1991, writes Alice Rawsthorn.
The group said yesterday its pharmaceutical sales had risen by 9 per cent to
FFr1.6bn during the interim period, with bio-medical sales increasing by
12.17 per cent to FFr313m.
Synthelabo, is France's fourth-largest pharmaceuticals group.
