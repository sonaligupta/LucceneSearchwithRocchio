FT  22 JUL 94 / UK Company News: 3i raises Ecu330m


3i, the recently floated venture capital group, said yesterday it had raised
Ecu330m (Pounds 260m) for its first fund to be invested in small and medium
sized private companies in continental Europe. At the first closing in
February, 3i had raised Ecu300m for the fund.
