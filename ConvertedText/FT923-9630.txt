FT  04 AUG 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Graf assured of a medal


Defending tennis champion Steffi Graf of Germany clinched at least a bronze
by routing Sabine Appelmans of Belgium 6-1, 6-0 in the quarter-finals.
Meanwhile Wimbledon finalist Goran Ivanisevic guaranteed Croatia its first
Olympic medal with a five-set victory over Frenchman Fabrice Santoro.
