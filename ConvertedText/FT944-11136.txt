FT  08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Ansaldo - Contracts and Ventures


   By ANDREW HILL


   MILAN


Ansaldo, part of Italy's state-controlled Finmeccanica engineering group,
has won a L115bn (Dollars 74.7m) order for rehabilitation, modernisation and
maintenance of the Al Ain power plant in the United Arab Emirates, following
an international tender.
