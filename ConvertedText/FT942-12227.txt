FT  30 APR 94 / UK Company News: Hunters Armley launches Pounds 9.4m rights
issue


Hunters Armley Group, the Leeds-based printer, is seeking about Pounds 9.4m
in an underwritten 1-for-4 rights issue. The company is issuing 5.09m shares
at 192p.
The shares fell 9p to close at 225p.
Hunters said the proceeds would be used to fund capital spending of Pounds
5.6m with the balance being held on deposit for suitable acquisitions,
although none was being considered at the moment.
