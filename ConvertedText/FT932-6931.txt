FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Shires assets up 13%


Shires Investment reported a 13 per cent increase to 250.35p in fully
diluted net assets per share at end-March 1993, compared with 221.4p a year
earlier.
After-tax revenue dropped from a restated Pounds 4.52m to Pounds 4.4m in the
year and earnings per share were 17.48p (19.54p). The final dividend is an
unchanged 5.9p for a total of 18.4p (same) - a total not less than 16.8p is
forecast for the current year.
