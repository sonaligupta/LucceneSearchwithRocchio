FT  11 APR 94 / John Smith hurt while climbing


Mr John Smith, the Labour party leader, has severely damaged the ligaments
in his right ankle and has cancelled his engagements for the coming week,
his office announced yesterday.
He injured himself when he slipped in snow while climbing in the Scottish
Highlands on Wednesday. He will be unable to attend the Commons this week.
