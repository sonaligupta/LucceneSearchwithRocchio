FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore Micro raises Pounds 34.5m


Gartmore Micro Index Trust, which was launched in November to track the
MicroCap index covering the smallest 1 per cent of UK equities by market
capitalisation, has raised about Pounds 34.5m net of expenses through a
placing and open offer.
The trust said yesterday that a total of 35.5m ordinary shares (with
warrants attached) at 100p each had been subscribed for.
