FT  04 AUG 92 / Mozambique peace talks


   By REUTER


   ROME


President Joaquim Chissano of Mozambique and Afonso Dhlakama, head of the
Mozambique National Resistance (Renamo), are due to meet in Rome tomorrow to
try to hammer out a peace agreement, Reuter reports from Rome.
It is the first meeting between the two sides in 16 years of conflict.
