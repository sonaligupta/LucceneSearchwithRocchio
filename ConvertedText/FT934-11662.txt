FT  30 OCT 93 / Air France strikers go back


   By REUTER


   PARIS


Striking Air France freight workers at Paris' main Roissy-Charles de Gaulle
airport yesterday voted to suspend a 16-day stoppage, signalling an end to a
crippling protest at the French national flag-carrier, Reuter reports from
Paris.
