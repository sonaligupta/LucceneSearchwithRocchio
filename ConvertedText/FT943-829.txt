FT  27 SEP 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea


There was fair general demand this week reports the Tea Brokers'
Association. Quality Assams were again strongly supported in the 180 to 250
pence range, while coloury mediums were fully firm. Selected brightest East
Africans were strong, but below best sorts lost 4 to 6 pence by the close.
Coloury mediums were fully firm, with plainer sorts steady. Fair demand at
firm to sometimes dearer rates. Quotations: best available: 210p/kg, good:
140p/kg, good medium: 130p, medium: 120p/kg, low medium: 85p/kg, nom the
highest price realised this week was 250p/kg for an assam pd.
