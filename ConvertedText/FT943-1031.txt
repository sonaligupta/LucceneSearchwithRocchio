FT  27 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: First for British chef


John Burton-Race, 36, who runs L'Ortolan, in Shinfield near Reading, is to
be awarded the Grand Prix de l'Art de la Cuisine by the International
Academy of Gastronomy - the only British chef to be given the prize.
