FT  01 JUL 92 / Buyer sought for small factories


ENGLISH ESTATES, the government's commercial property developer, is to seek
a buyer for 100 industrial estates worth a total of between Pounds 40m and
Pounds 50m.
The agency believes it can find one purchaser for the portfolio. It
comprises 1,750 small factories and workshops in northern England.
