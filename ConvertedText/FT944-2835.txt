FT  15 DEC 94 / Probes into bond losses


   By RICHARD WATERS


   NEW YORK


A number of US agencies, including the Justice Department, are investigating
how Orange County lost Dollars 2bn (Pounds 1.28bn) in the bond markets this
year, county officials confirmed yesterday, Richard Waters reports from New
York. The agencies include the Securities and Exchange Commission and the
Commodities Futures Trading Commission.
