FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Equitable Life


Equitable Life Assurance has purchased Direct Line's regional headquarters
building in Leeds for Pounds 27m. The seller was the Leeds Permanent
Building Society. The building is currently producing rental income of about
Pounds 1.6m, with a passing rent of Pounds 13.75 per sq ft.
The office block, Direct Line House, comprises 90,000 sq ft of offices on
nine upper floors, and an additional four shop units at ground level.
