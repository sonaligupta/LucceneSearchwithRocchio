FT  30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Boscombe Property ahead to Pounds 520,000


In the year to March 31 pre-tax profit at Boscombe Property rose from Pounds
351,000 to Pounds 520,000, leading to earnings ahead from 262.25p to
367.73p.
Rental income increased 10 per cent to Pounds 897,000 and expenses relating
to repairs and maintenance at San Remo Towers fell.
Contracts have been exchanged for the sale of San Remo at a price of Pounds
5.45m.
Dividends totalling 355p (180p) have already been paid.
