FT  22 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Holders Tech


Shares in Holders Technology fell by 32p to 147p after the USM-quoted
distributor of high precision tools and specialist materials reported
pre-tax profits for the six months to May 31 down 39 per cent at Pounds
152,000, against Pounds 251,000.
The result was despite an 11 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 2.21m
(Pounds 1.99m).
Earnings per share were 3.3p (5.39p). The interim dividend is maintained at
2p.
