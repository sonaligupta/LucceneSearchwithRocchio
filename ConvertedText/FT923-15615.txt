FT  01 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Czechoslovak pact goes ahead


   By FRANCES WILLIAMS


   GENEVA


A free trade pact between Czechoslovakia and the European Free Trade
Association (Efta) comes into effect today, despite uncertainty about
whether the country will split into two separate states, writes Frances
Williams in Geneva.
