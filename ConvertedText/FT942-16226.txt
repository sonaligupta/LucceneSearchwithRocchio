FT  12 APR 94 / US to sell 25 jets to Israel


   By GEORGE GRAHAM


The US administration has notified Congress that it plans to sell up to 25
F-15I long-range fighter bombers to Israel in a sale worth an estimated
Dollars 2.4bn (Pounds 1.64bn), George Graham reports.
Congress could block the deal, but no opposition is expected to the sale of
the McDonnell Douglas jets - a modified version of the sophisticated F-15E
Eagle.
