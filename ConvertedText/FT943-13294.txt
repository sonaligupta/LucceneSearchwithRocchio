FT  20 JUL 94 / International Company News: Irish venture capital fund
launched


A IPounds 47.5m (Pounds 46.8m) venture capital fund was launched yesterday
to invest in expanding businesses in the Irish Republic.
Participants in the ACT 1994 Development Capital Limited Partnership include
Irish and UK pension funds and investment institutions.
ACT 1994 is a 10-year, closed-end fund; most of its investments are expected
to be made during the first five years. It will provide capital ranging
between IPounds 300,000 and IPounds 5m for investment in medium-sized,
expanding companies and to finance management buy-outs and buy-ins.
ACT Venture Capital, a new company, will manage the fund.
