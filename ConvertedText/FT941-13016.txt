FT  27 JAN 94 / UK Company News In Brief: Elliott


ELLIOTT (B), the electrical and mechanical engineer, has acquired the
business and assets, including freehold property, from the liquidator of
John Hall (Oldham) for Pounds 367,250 cash. Stock to the value of Pounds
15,750 was also acquired.
