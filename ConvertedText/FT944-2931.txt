FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Close Brothers


Close Brothers Group, the merchant banking and financial services concern,
has acquired a further 15.2 per cent of Winterflood Securities, its
marketmaking subsidiary, taking its stake to 98.7 per cent.
The consideration of Pounds 8.74m cash was satisfied by the issue of 3.97m
new ordinary shares, which are being placed by Warburg Securities at 220p
each.
