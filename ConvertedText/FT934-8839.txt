FT  13 NOV 93 / Oil site to close


ACATOS & Hutcheson yesterday announced the closure of its edible oils
refinery at Bootle in Merseyside by mid-1995, with the loss of 250 jobs. It
said the 1950s factory could not be brought up to modern standards at a
reasonable cost.
