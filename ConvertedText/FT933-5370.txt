FT  03 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Motor World acquires eight more outlets


Motor World Group, the car parts and accessories retailer, has made its
first acquisition since coming to the market in February with a Pounds 27.7m
tag.
At the time Mr Darrell Kershaw, managing director, expressed the hope that
the company could expand from its existing 180 outlets to 300 within the
next two years.
It is buying Mr Motor, an eight branch chain of accessory shops in
Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire, for an undisclosed sum.
The company had sales of Pounds 1.5m last year.
