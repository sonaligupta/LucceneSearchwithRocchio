FT  21 APR 94 / Independent commission to investigate Mexican presidential
candidate's assassination


   By REUTER


   MEXICO CITY


President Carlos Salinas de Gortari named an independent commission
yesterday to investigate the assassination of ruling party presidential
candidate Luis Donaldo Colosio, in a move to quell growing suspicions,
Reuter reports from Mexico City.
