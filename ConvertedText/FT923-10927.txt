FT  28 JUL 92 / UK Company News: EFM Java asset value up 17%


Net asset value per ordinary share of EFM Java Trust stood at 30.03p at June
30, an improvement of 17 per cent over the 25.91p standing 12 months
earlier.
For the half year to end-June the trust incurred an attributable loss of
Pounds 27,000 (earnings Pounds 78,000). Losses per share were 0.0908p
(earnings 0.261p).
