FT  19 APR 91 / They're smiling all the way to the bank


   By STEPHEN FIDLER


   LONDON


THE BOARD of the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development met for
the first time yesterday in London. The meeting will continue today. writes
Stephen Fidler in London. The board - comprising 23 executive directors and
Mr Jacques Attali, the bank's president (in foreground) - is meant to play a
strong role in guiding the operations of the bank, owned by 39 governments
and two European institutions.
The meeting will map out the lending criteria of the European bank - the
first multilateral institution to attach political as well as economic
conditions to its loans.
