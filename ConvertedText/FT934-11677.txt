FT  30 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Face of fortune


Turkish businessmen pioneering in the former Soviet republic of Turkmenistan
gave the country's president, Saparmurat Niyazov, a gold mask cast in his
likeness. The president has become the subject of orchestrated adoration
since the country's independence two years ago.
