FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Courtaulds expands in Indonesia


Courtaulds, the chemicals company, is continuing its development in the Far
East with the opening of a Pounds 7m coatings factory in Jakarta, Indonesia.
The factory is owned by PT Courtaulds Coatings Indonesia, a joint venture
between Courtaulds, with an 80 per cent stake, and Dharmala, an Indonesian
group, with 20 per cent.
