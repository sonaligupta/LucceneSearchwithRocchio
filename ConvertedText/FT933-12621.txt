FT  23 JUL 93 / Wimpey director jailed for year


MR ALAN WORTHINGTON, former north-west divisional director of Wimpey
Construction, was jailed for a year yesterday after admitting fraudulently
authorising Pounds 31,432 worth of work - including a bathroom with
gold-plated taps - at the home of his daughter.
Appearing at Liverpool Crown Court he admitted conspiring to defraud between
August 1988 and January 1990.
