FT  27 MAY 93 / People: Public posts - ABTA


Mike Grindrod, general manager of Co-op Travelcare, will become chairman of
the ABTA National Training Board when his ABTA presidency ends in June. He
replaces Norman Richardson who is to become chairman of the governors of New
College, Durham.
