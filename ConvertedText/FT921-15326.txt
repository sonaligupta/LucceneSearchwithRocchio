FT  10 JAN 92 / Observer: Daddy?


Is the curse of the bogus Piltdown skull about to fall on scientists
claiming Bronze Age antiquity for the mummified corpse of a man found in the
Italian Alps in September?
A Swiss woman has written to the foreign ministry saying she has seen
photographs of the body, now in a university freezer in Austria, and
identifies it as her father who disappeared in the 1970s on the same alpine
glacier.
