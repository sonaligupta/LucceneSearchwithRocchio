FT  17 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Volvo finance arm in BFr2bn
offering


   By REUTER


VOLVO Group Finance Europe is launching a BFr2bn multi-currency commercial
paper programme in Belgium, Reuter reports.
Generale Bank, manager of the issue, said the paper would be issued in the
form of promissory notes with a maximum maturity of one year.
