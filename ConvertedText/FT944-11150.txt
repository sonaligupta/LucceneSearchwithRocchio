FT  08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Conrail - Contracts and ventures


   By REUTER


   PHILADELPHIA


Conrail of the US will build 45 General Motors Electro-Motive Division SD601
locomotives at its Juniata Locomotive Shop, the first diesel-electric
locomotives to be built at the Altoona facility. The new units are part of a
90-unit order placed earlier this year, Conrail said.
