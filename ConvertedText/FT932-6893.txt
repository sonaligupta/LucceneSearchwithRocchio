FT  26 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and jute


COTTON
Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool amounted to 196 tonnes for the week
ended 21 May, against 248 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did
not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist
styles notably in the American, Israeli and Senegal range.
JUTE
June/July: C and F Dundee:- BTC USD 325, BWC USD N/A, BTD USD300, BWD USD
300. C and F Antwerp:- BTC USD 305, BWC USD 305,BTD USD 280, BWD USD 280.
