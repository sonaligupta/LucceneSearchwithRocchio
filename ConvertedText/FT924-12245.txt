FT  21 OCT 92 / Suharto nominated


   By REUTER


   JAKARTA


Indonesian President Suharto, in power since crushing a coup attempt 27
years ago, yesterday accepted the nomination of his country's ruling party
as candidate for another five-year term in elections next March, virtually
ensuring his re-election, Reuter reports from Jakarta.
