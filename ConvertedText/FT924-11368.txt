FT  27 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Sea Containers


Sea Containers' Seacold Technologies subsidiary, has sold its marine
container refrigeration machinery manufacturing facility in Singapore to
Carrier Corporation.
The plant will be integrated into Carrier's Transicold division, based in
Syracuse, New York.
Purchase price for the assets was Dollars 19m (Pounds 11.6m), which includes
a lease of the Seacold factory and offices. Sea Containers said it would
continue to purchase substantial quantities of Seacold machines.
