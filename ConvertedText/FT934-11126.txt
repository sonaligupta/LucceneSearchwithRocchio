FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Enterprise Oil


ENTERPRISE OIL has received elections for its enhanced scrip dividend
alternative in respect of 455.8m existing ordinary shares, representing 95.3
per cent of its issued ordinary share capital. The cash offer has been
accepted in respect of 176m existing ordinary, representing 38.6 per cent of
elections. The cost of the 1993 special interim will be Pounds 2.1m against
Pounds 45.4m if all shareholders had received the full 9.5p cash dividend.
Also, some Pounds 12.6m has been saved in ACT.
