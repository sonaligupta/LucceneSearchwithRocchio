FT  11 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wagon's Pounds 6.7m expansion move


Wagon Industrial Holdings, the acquisitive materials handling, engineering
and automotive products group, is buying two vehicle components companies
for a total of Pounds 6.7m.
Avenell Engineering, of Oxford, and Ernest Derricott, of Birmingham, are
being acquired for Pounds 4.5m cash and Pounds 500,000 in unsecured loan
notes, with the balance in ordinary shares.
This strengthens Wagon's position as a supplier to the automotive industry.
