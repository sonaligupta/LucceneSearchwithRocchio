FT  01 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Tina Brown to edit New Yorker magazine


Tina Brown, aged 38, the Englishwoman who has edited New York-based Vanity
Fair since 1984, raising its circulation from 279,000 to nearly 1m a month,
is to edit The New Yorker. Both magazines are owned by Advance Publications
group. Observer, Page 21
