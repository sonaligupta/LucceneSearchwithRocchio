FT  22 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Actor Burt Lancaster dies in Los
Angeles


Actor Burt Lancaster died at his home in Los Angeles, aged 80. He appeared
in more than 70 films including From Here to Eternity, Elmer Gantry, for
which he received an Academy Award, and The Birdman of Alcatraz. He made his
last film, Voyage of Terror, in 1990.
Obituary, Weekend XIII
