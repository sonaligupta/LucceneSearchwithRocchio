FT  30 OCT 93 / German blood scandal probe


   By QUENTIN PEEL


   BONN


The German parliament yesterday set up an inquiry into a scandal over
HIV-infected blood products, Quentin Peel reports from Bonn. Two directors
of a blood plasma laboratory, were charged with criminal negligence
yesterday after infected blood reached German and Austrian hospitals.
