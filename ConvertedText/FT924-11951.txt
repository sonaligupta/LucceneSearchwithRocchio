FT  23 OCT 92 / International Company News: Occidental slips to Dollars 61m
on flat sales


   By KAREN ZAGOR


OCCIDENTAL Petroleum, the energy group, saw its third-quarter net income
tumble to Dollars 61m, or 20 cents, from Dollars 171m, or 56 cents, a year
earlier, writes Karen Zagor. Sales were flat at Dollars 2.3bn.
The latest quarter included a one-time loss of Dollars 8m, or 3 cents a
share, while last year's results included one-time gains of Dollars 75m, or
25 cents a share.
