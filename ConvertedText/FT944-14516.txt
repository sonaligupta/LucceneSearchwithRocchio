FT  22 OCT 94 / UK Company News: London American


London American Growth Trust ended the six months to September 30 with net
asset value per share at 56.6p. This compared with 64.5p at the end of the
year to March 31 1994 and 53.3p at the end of the previous first half.
Total income declined to Pounds 63,000 (Pounds 804,000). Net losses were
Pounds 344,000 (Pounds 392,000 profits), for losses per share of 0.38p
(0.36p earnings).
