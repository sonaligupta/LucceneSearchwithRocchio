FT  15 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Widney recovers with Pounds 1.2m


Recovery continued in the second half at Widney and the electrical and
mechanical engineer is paying its first dividend for five years.
On turnover of Pounds 31.2m (Pounds 22m) including Pounds 282,000 from
acquisitions, pre-tax profits for the year to October 1 were Pounds 1.19m,
compared with losses of Pounds 150,000. Earnings were 0.73p (losses 0.49p)
and there is a proposed final dividend of 0.2p.
