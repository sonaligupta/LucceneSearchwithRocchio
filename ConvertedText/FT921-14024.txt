FT  17 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Witan net asset value rises 17%


The net asset value per ordinary share of Witan Investment amounted to
178.8p at December 31 1991, a 17 per cent improvement on the 153.3p figure
of a year earlier.
The net asset value of the warrants improved from 76.8p to 102.3p over the
same period.
Revenue before tax for the year grew by Pounds 1m to Pounds 26.3m. A final
dividend of 2.8p (2.5p) is proposed making a total for the year of 5.3p
(4.75p). Earnings per share came out at 5.67p (5.4p).
Lord Faringdon, chairman, attributed the rise in net asset value to a
cautious investment approach under difficult stock market conditions.
