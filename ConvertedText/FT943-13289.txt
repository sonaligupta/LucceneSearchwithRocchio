FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Brasway advances 17% to Pounds 910,000


A 17 per cent rise in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 776,000 to Pounds
910,000, was announced by Brasway, the hydraulic hose and couplings and
lubricants group, for the year to April 30.
Mr Mark Swaby, chairman, pointed out that there had been a turnround of
Pounds 850,000 at the trading level, excluding profit on the sale of Brasway
Tube in April 1993.
Turnover advanced from Pounds 30.4m to Pounds 32.1m with acquisitions
contributing Pounds 2.26m in sales and Pounds 55,000 in operating profits.
Mr Swaby said that significant progress had been made in integrating Winster
Hose and the British Benzol lubricants business.
Earnings per share increased to 1.04p (0.69p) and a final dividend of 0.29p
is proposed for a 0.53p (0.51p) total.
