FT  16 NOV 93 / UK Company News: North West Water Pounds 15m contract


North West Water offshoot, Water Engineering, has won a further contract
from the Macau government for work on the peninsula. The Pounds 15m design
and build contract is for a sludge treatment plant. Water Engineering is
partnered by Portuguese civil engineering groups Teixeira Duarte and SC
Soares Da Costa.
