FT  25 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: Collor 'must have known' of corruption


Brazilian President Fernando Collor de Mello (left) must have known of a
vast network of corruption operating in and around the government, a
congressional committee's draft report into the affair said. Senator Amir
Lando, reading his findings to the Senate, said the committee had evidence
that a corruption operation was set up by Paulo Cesar Farias, Collor's
election campaign treasurer. Corruption crisis, Page 3
