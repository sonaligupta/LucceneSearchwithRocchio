FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Pillsbury buys Martha White


Pillsbury, the US subsidiary of Grand Metropolitan, the food and drinks
group, is acquiring Martha White, the Tennessee-based baking mix business,
for an undisclosed sum.
Mr David Nash, chairman and chief executive of GrandMet Food Sector, said
the purchase would further strengthen the market position of Pillsbury.
The company already boasts a product portfolio including Haagen-Dazs, Green
Giant and Jeno's.
