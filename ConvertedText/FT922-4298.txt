FT  06 JUN 92 / Largest ever debt-for-nature swap for Bolivia


   By CHRISTINA LAMB


The largest ever debt-for-nature swap in terms of face value was announced
yesterday for projects in Bolivia, writes Christina Lamb. JP Morgan, the
western bank, will donate the Dollars 11.5m (Pounds 6.3m) balance of its
Bolivian debt to the Nature Conservancy and the World Wildlife Fund for
environmental projects.
