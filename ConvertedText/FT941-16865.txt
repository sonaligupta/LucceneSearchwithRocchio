FT  07 JAN 94 / Observer: Pearly king


Coopers & Lybrand has been more than usually munificent with its 1994
calendar, entitled 'Solutions for Business'.
It has sent one to Simon Noble, chairman of Loch Fyne Oysters, in Argyll,
even though it addressed him as 'Mr S Noble Decd', or deceased. Alive and
kicking, Noble wonders how many other clients have been killed off by their
accountants.
Far too numerous to mention, perhaps . . .
