FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: Severn Valley Railway share offer


Severn Valley Railway (Holdings) is offering up to 750,000 new ordinary
shares at Pounds 1 each, payable in full on application, to fund the
purchase of its Kidderminster railway station and provide additional working
capital.
The company's shares trade under Rule 535(2).
