FT  11 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wimpey sale


George Wimpey, the construction group, has agreed the sale of Wimpol, its
satellite survey and positioning company, to Fugro McClelland, the Dutch
engineering consultant.
The sale for Pounds 5.4m includes the repayment of inter-company debt,
thought to be Pounds 6m.
Wimpol, based in Swindon, Wiltshire, works mainly for the offshore oil and
gas industry, mainly in the North Sea, east Asia and the Gulf of Mexico.
