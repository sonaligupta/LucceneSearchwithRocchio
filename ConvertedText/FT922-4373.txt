FT  05 JUN 92 / Glazing rescue


CIS Windows, a wholly owned subsidiary of Pilkington, has bought part of the
customer list of Surestyle, the Bury double-glazing company that closed this
week. CIS said it hoped to honour orders at Surestyle's prices and absorb
deposits paid.
