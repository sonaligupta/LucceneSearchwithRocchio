FT  07 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Beckenham share trading restored


Trading in the shares of Beckenham Group, the USM-quoted heating and
ventilation engineer, was restored yesterday after the announcement of
refinancing proposals on Wednesday. The shares closed down  1/2 p at 2p.
The proposals, including a placing, rights issue and share consolidation,
will be put to shareholders on January 27.
