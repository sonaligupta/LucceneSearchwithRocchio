FT  24 MAR 92 / International Company News: HK trader seeks exchange listing


   By AP-DJ


   HONG KONG


EASTERN Century, a Hong Kong company which trades in minerals and metals,
yesterday announced it was seeking a listing on the local stock exchange. It
is offering 95m, or 25 per cent, of its enlarged share capital, to raise
HKDollars 98m (USDollars 13m), AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong.
The group said it had also formed a joint-venture with two mainland and two
Hong Kong partners in Xinyu, Jiangxi province, China, for the production and
trading of manganese ferro-alloys.
