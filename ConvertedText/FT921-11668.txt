FT  30 JAN 92 / Approval for Bristol plan


BRISTOL Development Corporation has been allowed to issue a compulsory
purchase order on the first phase of its Pounds 500m plan to redevelop the
city's Temple Meads station. The project is expected eventually to create
8,500 jobs.
The corporation intends to develop Temple Meads as one of the main points of
entry into the city. The project is to be undertaken by British Land.
