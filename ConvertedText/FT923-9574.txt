FT  04 AUG 92 / League quits Chad cabinet


   By REUTER


   N'DJAMENA


The Chadian Human Rights League (LTDH) has withdrawn from Chad's troubled
unity government after paramilitary gendarmes killed six civilians and
wounded 14 while trying to arrest a Moslem preacher, Reuter reports from
N'Djamena.
