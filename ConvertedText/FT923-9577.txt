FT  04 AUG 92 / Ramos threat to communist rebels


   By REUTER


   MANILA


Philippine President Fidel Ramos said yesterday he would stamp out communist
rebels if they refused his offers of peace, Reuter reports from Manila.
Mr Ramos, has offered to legalise the banned Communist Party of the
Philippines and grant amnesty to New People's Army (NPA) guerrillas if they
renounce armed struggle.
