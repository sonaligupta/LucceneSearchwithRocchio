FT  06 JAN 94 / Council sells public works unit


Barrow Council in Cumbria has sold its loss-making former public works
operations to Sita, the UK subsidiary of the French-owned Societe
Industrielle de Transports Automobiles, for Pounds 800,000 plus payments for
stock and equipment.
Sita also gets a five-year contract to run Barrow's building maintenance,
street lighting, cemeteries, refuse collection and cleansing services.
The Surrey-based company provides similar services to Blackpool, Kettering,
Leicester and the London boroughs of Enfield, Kingston and Bromley.
