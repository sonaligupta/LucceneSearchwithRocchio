FT  11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Hewitt in Dutch joint venture


Hewitt Group, the industrial ceramics and refractory group, has entered into
a joint venture with the technical ceramics division of NV Koninklijke
Sphinx, the Dutch-based sanitaryware and ceramic tile group.
Hewitt will acquire 50 per cent of BV Keramische Industrie, the company in
which the business of Sphinx's technical ceramics division is carried out,
for Fl 5m cash, on the basis of warranted net assets of Fl 10m (Pounds
3.8m).
