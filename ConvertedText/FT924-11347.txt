FT  27 OCT 92 / World News In Brief: Germans unhappy


Thirty-five per cent of Germans surveyed in the west and 52 per cent in the
formerly Communist east are dissatisfied with their political system, a
survey by the Mannheim opinion research institute shows. In terms of trust,
the federal government lags behind trade unions and the news media.
