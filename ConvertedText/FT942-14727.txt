FT  19 APR 94 / UK Company News: Eastern Elect completes 5% share buy-back


   By MICHAEL SMITH


Eastern Electricity, the regional electricity distributor based in Ipswich,
has completed the purchase of 13.5m of its own shares, representing 5 per
cent of the total equity.
It bought 4.2m shares yesterday at an average price of 675p. The shares
closed 8 1/2 p up at 656p.
The average price of the 13.5m shares bought was 663p.
Eastern received permission for a 5 per cent share buy-back at its last
annual meeting.
Other regional power companies are likely to seek similar authorisations at
their AGMs.
