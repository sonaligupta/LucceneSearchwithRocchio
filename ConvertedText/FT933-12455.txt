FT  23 JUL 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa

fell 51 to 1,810 and the industrials index weakened 23 to 4,545. The overall
index shed 39 to 3,989. Kloof receded R1.25 to R51 and Anglos R1.50 to
R140.50.
