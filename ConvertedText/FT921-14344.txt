FT  16 JAN 92 / Remands over Revenue claims


THREE MEN who allegedly cheated the Inland Revenue of Pounds 14.5m in
relation to Nissan UK, the car importer and distributor, were yesterday
further remanded on bail by Worthing magistrates until February 26.
Mr Frank Shannon, the company's former finance director, and Mr Brian
Laventure and Mr Gerald Compton, both financial advisers, are charged with
making false representations to the Inland Revenue in 1980 and 1981
regarding stock relief claims for Nissan UK, based in Worthing, West Sussex.
