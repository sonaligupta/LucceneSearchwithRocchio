FT  27 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Dawn looks at new capital


Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust is holding discussions with existing and
potential shareholders about a possible increase in capital.
Directors are considering an issue of C shares with new warrants on
conversion. Any issue would be designed so that fully diluted net asset
value per share would not be reduced on conversion.
