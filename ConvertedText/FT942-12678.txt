FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News: Spinning recovery helps Shiloh to Pounds 1m


The expansion of its healthcare activities, together with a recovery in
spinning in the second half, resulted in a 49 per cent profits rise at
Shiloh in the year to March 26.
On sales up 10 per cent from Pounds 25.6m to Pounds 28.2m the pre-tax figure
jumped to Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 756,560).
The final dividend is raised to 2.5p (2.025p) making a total of 3.5p (2.9p)
on earnings per share of 15.15p (7.96p).
