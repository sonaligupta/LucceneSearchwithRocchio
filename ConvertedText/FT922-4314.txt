FT  05 JUN 92 / People: Lygo goes


Sir Raymond Lygo, a former chief executive of British Aerospace, has been
appointed chairman of TNT EXPRESS(UK), Britain's leading express parcels
carrier. He succeeds Peter Allsebrook who died last year.
Sir Raymond, a 68-year-old retired admiral, has been associated with the
Australian-headquartered transport group for many years. His former
employer, BAe, supplied aircraft to TNT Express Worldwide and to Ansett
Airlines, a TNT associate. He retired from BAe in 1989 and is currently
chairman of Rutland Trust and continues to work as a business consultant.
