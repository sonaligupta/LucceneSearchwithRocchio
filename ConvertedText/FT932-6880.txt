FT  26 MAY 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


GOLD shares weakened in spite of a late rise in the bullion price, and the
index finished down 29 at 1,856. Industrials slipped 8 to 4,518 and the
overall index 23 to 4,009. De Beers receded R1.50 to R82 and Anglos R1 to
R143.50.
