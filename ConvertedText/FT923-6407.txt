FT  25 AUG 92 / Businessman killed


MR ROBERT WATTS, chairman of the Blackburn Partnership, which organised the
Lancashire town's bid for City Challenge government funding, was one of four
men killed in a Scottish air crash at the weekend, police said yesterday. Mr
Watts founded Pressport Manufacturing in Blackburn.
