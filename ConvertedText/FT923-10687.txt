FT  29 JUL 92 / Rouble suffers further fall


   By REUTER


The rouble tumbled further against the dollar on Moscow's small-scale
currency exchange yesterday, Reuter reports. The rouble changed hands at
Rbs161.1 to the dollar on the Moscow Interbank Currency Exchange after 155.7
at the last trading session on Thursday and 130.2 earlier this month.
