FT  30 APR 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Continental net assets rise


Net asset value per share of the Fleming Continental European Trust rose
from 270.1p to 359.8p over the 12 months to March 31 1994.
Earnings available, however, dipped to Pounds 1.51m, compared with Pounds
1.81m, giving a per share value of 2.26p (2.71p). The final, single dividend
is also lower at 2.2p, against 2.7p.
