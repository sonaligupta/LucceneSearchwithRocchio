FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Seafoods buy for Perkins Foods


Perkins Foods has acquired the Cromer Crab Company, a Norfolk processor and
supplier of crabs and shellfish for a consideration to be satisfied by the
issue to the vendors of 130,952 new ordinary 10p shares.
