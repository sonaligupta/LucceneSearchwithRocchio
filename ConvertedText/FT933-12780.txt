FT  22 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Aminex


AMINEX: Applications for recent open offer received in respect of 10.2m
shares, representing 71 per cent of offer and raising IPounds 2.29m of new
money. East West Oil to take up full entitlement of 4.28m shares, bringing
its stake to 35 per cent of enlarged capital. East West does not intend to
bid for outstanding shares.
