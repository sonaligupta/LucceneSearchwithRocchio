FT  23 NOV 92 / Gas platform heads for North Sea


A 9,000-tonne deck for a gas production platform leaving Glasgow for the
North Sea, where it will be hooked up to the Lomond field operated by Amoco,
the oil company. The Pounds 60m deck left the yard on Clydebank where the
Queen Mary, Queen Elizabeth and QE2 were built
