FT  12 MAR 94 / UK Company News: BZW Endowment net asset value 44.7p


BZW Endowment Fund, which came to the market last October, had a net asset
value of 44.7p per share at January 31.
For the period from September 17 to January 31 there was a net loss of
Pounds 1.17m equivalent to 5.85p per share. The trust invests in
second-hand, with-profit endowment policies.
