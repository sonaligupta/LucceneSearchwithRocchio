FT  29 OCT 93 / International Company News: Seagram raises Time Warner stake
to 6.8%


   By ROBERT GIBBENS


   MONTREAL


SEAGRAM, one of the world's top four drinks groups, has raised its stake in
Time Warner, the entertainment group, to 6.8 per cent from 5.7 per cent.
The 4.6m additional Time Warner shares acquired cost Seagram about USDollars
195m, analysts said, bringing the total cost of its investment to nearly
USDollars 900m.
Time Warner stock has risen from USDollars 38 in June to about USDollars 44
a share this week.
Mr Edgar Bronfman, senior chairman, said Seagram would invest about
USDollars 2bn for 15 per cent in Time Warner.
