FT  05 MAR 94 / Hungarian purge


   By NICHOLAS DENTON


   BUDAPEST


Hungary's government yesterday reinforced its grip on broadcasting in
advance of parliamentary elections on May 8 which polls say the conservative
coalition will lose, writes Nicholas Denton in Budapest. State radio gave
notice to leading broadcasters, capping a state media purge begun last year.
