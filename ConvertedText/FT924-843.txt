FT  22 DEC 92 / Holiday groups cut prices


   THE BIG three holiday tour operators


Thomson, Owners Abroad and Airtours - are cutting the costs of summer
holidays next year in an attempt to stimulate sales in the recession-hit
market.
Thomson says bookings for summer 1993 are 10 per cent down and there is no
sign of the recession ending.
