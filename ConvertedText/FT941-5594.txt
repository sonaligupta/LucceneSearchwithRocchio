FT  05 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Albert Fisher buys Campbell arm


Albert Fisher, the food processing and distribution group, has acquired
Campbell Chilled Foods, a subsidiary of Campbell's UK, for a maximum of
Pounds 6.5m.
The consideration comprises Pounds 5.6m cash together with the assumption of
Pounds 900,000 of debt - subject to final adjustment based on net assets at
completion.
Campbell Chilled Foods trades under the Wrights of York name and had
operating profits of Pounds 886,000 on turnover of Pounds 12m in the year to
August 1 1993.
