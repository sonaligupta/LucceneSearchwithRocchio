FT  23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: TDG buys French distribution company


Transport Development Group, the UK's second largest haulier, has acquired
Cotradip, a French specialist wines and spirits distribution company, for
FFr70m (Pounds 8.35m) from Remy Cointreau Group.
