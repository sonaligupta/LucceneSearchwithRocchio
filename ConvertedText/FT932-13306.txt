FT  23 APR 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


THERE WAS a late recovery in gold shares after the price of bullion
strengthened: the index was up 2 at 1,218 after a low of 1,199. Vaal Reefs
added 50 cents at R220. The overall index slipped 5 to 3,566 and industrials
lost 17 to 4,352.
