FT  08 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: UK rejects Lockerbie trial in
Switzerland


Britain rejected an offer from two Libyans accused of blowing up a Pan Am
jumbo jet over Lockerbie, Dumfries, in 1988, killing 270 people, to stand
trial in Switzerland, insisting they should be tried in either Scotland or
the US.
