FT  14 APR 94 / Hamleys to issue Pounds 20m of shares


As well as games and puzzles, Hamleys, the London toyshop, is offering a
Pounds 20m share issue, valuing the company at Pounds 40m. Its pathfinder
prospectus was published yesterday.
Mr Stephen Woodbridge, managing director, and Mr Howard Dyer, chairman, plan
to increase selling space and capitalise on the Hamleys name, through
branded products, concessions in other stores and small outlets such the one
at Covent Garden.
