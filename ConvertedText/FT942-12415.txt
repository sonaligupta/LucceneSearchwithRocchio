FT  29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Anglian Group Pounds 0.4m purchase


Anglian Group, the Norwich-based double glazing company, is to pay Pounds
400,000 in cash and shares for 77.5 per cent of Living Design (Home
Improvements).
Living Design, based in Scotland, is a supplier of double glazing and
kitchens with an annual turnover of Pounds 13m.
Consideration will be met via Pounds 200,000 in cash and the issue of 76,924
new ordinary shares.
