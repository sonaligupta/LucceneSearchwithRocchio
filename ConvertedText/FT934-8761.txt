FT  13 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico


MEXICAN STOCKS ended at a second consecutive record high as optimism grew
about prospects for the North American Free Trade Agreement.
The IPC index of 36 leading shares soared 52.09 points to end at 2,108.44, a
gain of 2.53 per cent. Shortly before the close the IPC index hit a record
intra-day high of 2,109.82.
