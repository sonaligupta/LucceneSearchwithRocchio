FT  26 OCT 92 / Exports up 9% in Singapore


   By KIERAN COOKE


Singapore's exports rose 9 per cent in September over the August figure, the
Trade Development Board said, writes Kieran Cooke. Analysts say that if
export growth is maintained Singapore's GDP could grow by about 6 per cent
this year.
