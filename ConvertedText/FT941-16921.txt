FT  07 JAN 94 / Downing Street Christmas tree to be recycled


The Christmas tree which stood outside Number 10 Downing Street was removed
yesterday to be recycled as compost for a children's nature garden in south
London. More than 60,000 such trees will be recycled this year, according to
community service charity CSV
