FT  12 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: Writer buried


Henri Alain-Fournier, author of romantic novel Le Grand Meaulnes, has been
buried 78 years after his death in the first world war. Fournier, whose body
was discovered a year ago, went missing in action when he was 28.
