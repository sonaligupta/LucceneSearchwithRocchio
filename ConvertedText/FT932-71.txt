FT  30 JUN 93 / International Company News: Setback at General Mills


   By NIKKI TAIT


   NEW YORK


GENERAL Mills, the US food manufacturer and restaurant operator, yesterday
reported a fall in fourth-quarter after-tax profits in the three months to
May 30 to Dollars 67.5m from Dollars 93.1m, on unchanged sales of Dollars
2bn.
Net income for the year was Dollars 506.1m, against Dollars 495.6m in the
previous 12 months.
