FT  16 MAR 94 / Death for fraud


   By REUTER


   BEIJING


The mastermind of one of China's largest fraud schemes has been sentenced to
death and a former vice-minister jailed for 20 years for corruption in the
case, it was revealed yesterday, Reuter reports from Beijing.
Shen Taifu was sentenced on March 4 for organising the Great Wall Machinery
and Electronics Group, a pyramid-style scam that sucked up Yuan 1bn (Dollars
114m) from unsuspecting small investors.
