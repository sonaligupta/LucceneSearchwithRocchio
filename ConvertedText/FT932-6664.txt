FT  27 MAY 93 / Observer: To boot


W H Smith would seem to be siding with Tottenham Hotspur chairman Alan Sugar
in his dispute with club chief executive Terry Venables. The retail chain's
Hammersmith branch is displaying a pile of board games fronted with a large
portrait of Venables and the title: How to Be a Manager.
They've been marked down from Pounds 14.99 to Pounds 7.49.
