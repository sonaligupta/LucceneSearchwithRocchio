FT  30 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Anglo Irish rights


Anglo Irish Bank Corporation's rights issue has attracted acceptances in
respect of 55.29m new ordinary shares (91.9 per cent). The remaining 4.89m
shares were sold in the market at 47p each, a 1p premium over the issue
price.
