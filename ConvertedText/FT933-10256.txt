FT  05 AUG 93 / Mercury changes domestic tariff


MERCURY yesterday announced changes to its residential tariff structure to
bring it into line with BT's. Designed mainly to make comparisons with BT
easier, it includes a 5 per cent discount for customers whose Mercury bill
exceeds Pounds 20 in any month.
