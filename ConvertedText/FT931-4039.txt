FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Castle Comms improves 17%


Castle Communications, the USM-quoted music and entertainment company,
achieved a 17 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits to Pounds 927,000 for
the six months to end-December.
Turnover increased to Pounds 17m (Pounds 15.6m) and earnings per share to
8.2p (6.7p). The interim dividend is maintained at 4p.
