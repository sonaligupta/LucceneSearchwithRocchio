FT  24 AUG 92 / Observer: Discounted


Arranging your own pension plan might be all the rage at the moment, in the
wake of Captain Bob Maxwell's pension fund raids, but the Which? guide,
Choose your own Pension, has not proved a best seller. Copies of the Pounds
7.95 book have been knocked down to just Pounds 1 at a Fleet Street
bookseller.
