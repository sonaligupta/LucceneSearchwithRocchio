FT  23 APR 93 / UK Company News: United Energy back in black with Pounds
0.06m


A turnround from net losses of Pounds 127,000 to profits of Pounds 58,000
was announced by United Energy, the USM-quoted oil and gas production
company, for 1992.
Mr John Billington, chairman, said that AmBrit International had been fully
integrated into the group and the savings in overheads envisaged at the time
of the takeover had been achieved.
Turnover improved from Pounds 377,000 to Pounds 2.79m with average oil
production at 820b/d. Earnings per share amounted to 0.2p (1p losses).
