FT  22 OCT 94 / Staff at Yorkshire TV agree terms


Employees of Yorkshire-Tyne Tees Television, who had resisted new
cost-cutting terms and conditions, agreed to them yesterday.
New terms for the 185 employees - almost all members of the Bectu
broadcasting union - include an end to premium overtime rates and flexible
rostering.
