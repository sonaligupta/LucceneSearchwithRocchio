Dan Quayle jokes may be a little passe seeing that he will soon be safely
more than a heartbeat away from the US presidency, but the following may be
worth recycling:
Question to Dan Quayle from the audience at a public meeting. 'What do you
make of Roe v. Wade?'
Quayle pauses for reflection.
'Gee, are those new ways to cross the Potomac?'
