FT  19 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Lebanon cuts ties with Iraq


Lebanon severed diplomatic ties with Iraq after Baghdad refused to lift the
immunity of two of its diplomats suspected of killing Iraqi dissident Taleb
as-Suheil in Beirut.  Lebanese officials said the two men, currently
detained, had confessed to the murder.
