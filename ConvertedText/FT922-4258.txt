FT  06 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices


Jamaica lifted its suspension of pimento sales and increased the official
price to Dollars 2,400 a tonne fob Kingston from Dollars 2,100, reports
Man-producten.  European resellers offering spot Jamaican pimento at Dollars
2,550., ex-warehouse, Mexican new crop available at discount.  Spot
Madagascar cloves Dollars 1,100.  Seychelles cassia/cinnamom Dollars 1,350
cif Europe.  Vietnam cassia, broken quality between Dollars 1,600 and
Dollars 1,900 cif. Madagascan cinnamom FF6.25 a kg cif. Ginger Indian Cochin
Dollars 1,000 and special quality Dollars 1,050.
