FT  25 MAY 93 / World News in Brief: Mandela backs vote for 14-year-olds


A suggestion by African National Congress head Nelson Mandela (left) that
14-year-olds be given the vote stunned South Africa's political
establishment - and surprised senior ANC members. Home affairs minister
Danie Schutte said the idea would not receive serious consideration.
