FT  02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: TR Far East net assets improve


TR Far East Income Trust had a net asset value of 160.9p at August 31
compared with 91.6p a year earlier. Fully diluted, the value was 151.6p and
93.8p respectively.
Net revenue for the year improved from Pounds 2.12m to Pounds 2.44m for
earnings of 5.25p (4.56p) per share. A fourth interim dividend of 1.3p
(1.2p) is declared making a total of 5p (4.5p). The directors have forecast
a dividend for the current year of not less than 5.2p.
