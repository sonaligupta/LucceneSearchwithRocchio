FT  29 JAN 92 / Parliament and Politics: Poll puts Tories 1 point ahead


AN opinion poll today puts the Conservatives just 1 percentage point ahead
on 41 per cent (up 2 points on two months ago) with Labour on 40 (-1) and
the Liberal Democrats on 13 (-3), according to an ICM survey for the Daily
Mail.
