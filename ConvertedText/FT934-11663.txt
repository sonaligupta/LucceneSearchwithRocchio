FT  30 OCT 93 / Georgian rebels repulsed


   By REUTER


   TBILISI


Georgia said yesterday its troops had recaptured territory from rebels in
the west, as the civil war looked headed towards a final showdown, Reuter
reports from Tbilisi. The rebels, fighting to restore ex-president Zviad
Gamsakhurdia, were reduced to a single western stronghold, Zugdidi.
