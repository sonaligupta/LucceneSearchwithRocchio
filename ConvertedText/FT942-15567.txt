FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Minmet


MINMET, through its wholly owned subsidiary, Groundsland, has bought the
landscaping and ground maintenance business of Salisbury Garden Services
(Northampton) for Pounds 200,000. There is a profit-related deferred
consideration of Pounds 230,000, payable on July 1 1995.
