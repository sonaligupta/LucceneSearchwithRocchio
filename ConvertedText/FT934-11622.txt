FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Dares Estates


Dares Estates, the property investment and development group, reported
pre-tax losses down from Pounds 2.87m to Pounds 1.8m for the half year to
June 30. Income rose from Pounds 2.9m to Pounds 3.4m.
Losses per share came out at 0.28p (2.08p).
