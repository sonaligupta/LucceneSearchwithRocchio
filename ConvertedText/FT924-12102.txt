FT  22 OCT 92 / City of Oxford asset value dips


Over the six months to September 30 net asset value per share at City of
Oxford Investment Trust fell from 19.6p to 17.6p.
For the zero dividend preference shares the value improved from 72.4p to
77p.
Net revenue for the half year came to Pounds 781,000 (Pounds 627,000) for
earnings of 2.6p (2.83p).
The second interim dividend is 1.2p on increased capital, making an
unchanged 2.4p to date.
