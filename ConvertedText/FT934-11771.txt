FT  29 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Lloyd's capital vehicle for the US


   By ANDREW JACK


THE FIRST corporate capital vehicle for the Lloyd's of London insurance
market to be quoted in the US was announced yesterday.
Lutine Capital Corporation has filed a registration statement with the US
Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of 12.5m
shares at Dollars 25 each.
The company will acquire Anton Members Agency, a members' agent, at the time
its initial public offer closes and use it as an underwriting adviser.
The sponsor for Lutine will be Phoenix Securities, a London-based investment
bank and a founder of Lutine. The offering will be managed by Donaldson,
Lufken & Jenerette Securities and Morgan Stanley.
Meanwhile, Hiscox Select Insurance Fund, another Lloyd's investment trust,
announced yesterday that broker Charterhouse Tilney had underwritten a
placing of 30m shares at 100p each.
