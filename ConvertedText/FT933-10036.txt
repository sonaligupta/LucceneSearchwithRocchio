FT  06 AUG 93 / Power company to cut bills by Pounds 9


SWEB, the electricity distributer in south-west England, is to give
customers a Pounds 9 reduction in their quarterly bills received between
October and December, at a total cost of Pounds 12m.
For a domestic customer the benefit is equivalent to 2.5 per cent of a total
annual bill. Other regional companies have implemented similar cuts.
