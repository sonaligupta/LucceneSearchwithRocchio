FT  20 APR 94 / People: Watmoughs (Holdings)


Michael Howell, managing director of DH Greaves, and Declan Salter, managing
director of Watmoughs Ltd, are to join the board of Watmoughs (Holdings),
the Bradford-based printer, on May 1. Russell Allan, chairman of DH Greaves,
and a director of Watmoughs (Holdings) retires at the end of the month.
