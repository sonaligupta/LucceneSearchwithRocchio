FT  29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Investors Capital net assets rise


Net asset value per ordinary share of Investors Capital Trust, ultimately
owned by British Assets Trust, edged ahead from 132p to 139.4p over the 12
months ended March 31.
For the six months to end-March available revenue amounted to Pounds 6.18m
(Pounds 5.75m). Group earnings per share emerged at 2.48p (2.31p). A second
interim dividend of 1.3p makes 2.6p (2.55p) to date.
