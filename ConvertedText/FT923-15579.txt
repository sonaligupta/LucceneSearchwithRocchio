FT  01 JUL 92 / BSkyB sells two original satellites to Norwegian Telecom


BRITISH Sky Broadcasting has sold the second of two original BSB Marco Polo
satellites to Norwegian Telecom in a deal believed to be worth around Pounds
30m.
The high-power satellite will be used by Tele-TV, a subsidiary of Norwegian
Telecom, to launch a five-channel television service in October or November.
