FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Trifast


Trifast, the industrial fastenings group which came to the market in
February, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1.16m to Pounds 1.94m in
the six months to September 30, despite exceptional costs of Pounds 242,000
on an aborted acquisition.
Earnings were 7.69p (5.6p) and the interim dividend 2.35p.
