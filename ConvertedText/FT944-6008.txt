FT  01 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Syrian state shipping line invites bids -
Contracts and ventures


   By REUTER


   DAMASCUS


Syria's state-run shipping line has invited bids for two new merchant ships
to carry heavy cargo containers and general cargo. It has called for
international tenders to build the vessels, with a closing date of December
26.
