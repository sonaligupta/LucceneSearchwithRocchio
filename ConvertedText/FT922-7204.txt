FT  20 MAY 92 / Israelis and Palestinians meet


   By REUTER


   TOKYO


Israeli and Palestinian delegates discussed their region's environmental
problems face-to-face for the first time at an international meeting in
Tokyo which ended yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Participants agreed
to convene the next meeting in the autumn in the Netherlands.
