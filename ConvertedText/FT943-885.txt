FT  27 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Go-Ahead above forecast at Pounds 2.86m


The Go-Ahead Group turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.86m for the year to
July 2, which was 25 per cent higher than its prospectus forecast. This
compared with a previous Pounds 315,000.
Turnover rose from Pounds 49.3m to Pounds 61.4m, because of the impact of
acquisitions in Brighton and Oxford. Operating profit rose from Pounds
521,000 to Pounds 3.47m, with Pounds 716,000 coming from the newly-acquired
businesses.
Earnings per share were much higher at 8.2p (1.5p).
