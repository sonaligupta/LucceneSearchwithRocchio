FT  23 APR 93 / North Sea oilfield contract awarded


AMERADA HESS, the US oil company, awarded a contract to Stena Offshore to
develop its latest North Sea oilfield.
The Pounds 20m contract is for 54km of undersea pipelines which will link
six sub-sea wells to Shell/Esso's Tern platform, east of the Hudson
oilfield, which has estimated recoverable reserves of more than 80m barrels
of oil.
