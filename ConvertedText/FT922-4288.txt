FT  06 JUN 92 / Shipyard pay deal


HOURLY PAID workers at Swan Hunter, the Tyneside shipbuilders, have accepted
a two-year pay deal in a ballot. It consists of three lump-sum payments this
year totalling Pounds 275 and an inflation-linked pay rise with a 4.5 per
cent ceiling in April next year.
