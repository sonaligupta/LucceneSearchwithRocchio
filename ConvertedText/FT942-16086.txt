FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Chepstow Racecourse ahead


From turnover of Pounds 1.95m, against Pounds 1.83m, Chepstow Racecourse,
which promotes and runs race meetings, lifted pre-tax profits to Pounds
388,817 for 1993, compared with a previous Pounds 247,069.
Earnings per share were 65.5p (41p) while the single dividend is boosted
from 1p to 5p. Directors also propose a special payment of 5p for the year.
