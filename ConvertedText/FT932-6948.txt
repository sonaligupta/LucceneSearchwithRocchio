FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Alida Holdings


ALIDA HOLDINGS, a wholly owned subsidiary of British Polythene Industries,
reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.17m (Pounds 1.23m) for 1992. Turnover
was Pounds 41.8m (Pounds 41.7m) and earnings per share 6.65p (5.55p).
