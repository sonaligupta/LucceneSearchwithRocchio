FT  29 APR 94 / International Company News: Sara Lee posts record sales


Sara Lee, the US consumer products group, announced record sales and flat
earnings for the third quarter ended April 2, in line with analysts'
expectations.
Net sales for the quarter were Dollars 3.7bn, up 11 per cent on last year's
Dollars 3.3bn. Net income was unchanged at Dollars 152m.
