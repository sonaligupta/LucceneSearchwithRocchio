FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Maid


MAID: the Daniel Wagner (Maid Trust), a trust associated with Maid director,
Mr Daniel Wagner, recently bought 100,000 shares in the company at a cost of
103p. Wagner now has an interest in 24.61m shares (30.37 per cent) in the
company.  These include 7.2m shares held by Curzon Securities and Trustees
on trust for beneficiaries who include Mr Wagner. Maid's placing price was
110p.
