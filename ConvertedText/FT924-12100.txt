FT  22 OCT 92 / Micklegate losses increase to Pounds 4.75m


Micklegate Group, the property developer, finished the year to April 30 with
a pre-tax loss of Pounds 4.75m, after exceptional provisions of Pounds 3m.
The deficit compared with Pounds 806,000 last time when exceptionals were
Pounds 869,000.
Losses per share were 21.84p (2.79p).
Exceptional provisions comprised two items: Pounds 2.28m (Pounds 869,000) in
respect of properties owned by the group, and Pounds 732,000 to write down
the debt due from an associate.
Certain of the group's bankers have agreed in principle to defer and
subordinate part of the bank borrowings and to put the balance of their
loans on a term loan basis.
A proposal to reduce the share premium account will be put to an
extraordinary meeting on November 18.
