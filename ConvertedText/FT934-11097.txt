FT  02 NOV 93 / International Company News: Blockbuster plans 12.6m share
sale


   By REUTER


BLOCKBUSTER Entertainment of the US plans an underwritten sale of 12.6m
shares, Reuter reports.
Blockbuster said it intended to use the proceeds to reduce debt.
It added that the shares being offered represent about 5.2 per cent of its
outstanding stock.
