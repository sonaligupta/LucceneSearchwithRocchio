FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Quiligotti acquisition


Quiligotti, the USM-traded tiles and flooring group, has acquired R
Cristofoli, a terrazzo tile maker, from the receiver for Pounds 1.61m cash.
The company has agreed to terminate finance leases in Cristofoli with a
value of Pounds 40,000.
For the year to March 31 Cristofoli had turnover of Pounds 4.49m (Pounds
5.67m) and profits of Pounds 292,000 (Pounds 169,000).
