FT  10 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Treatt shares rise on 14.5% improvement


A record year for its principle subsidiary helped Treatt, the USM-quoted
supplier of essential oils and aromatic chemicals, show a 14.5 per cent
increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 1.18m for the year
to end-September.
Shares in the Bury St Edmunds-based company increased 11p to close at 96p.
Mr Geoffrey Bovill, chairman, said that RC Treatt had profits of Pounds 1.4m
with increased net margins of 12.9 per cent, against 10.5 per cent, helped
by sales of its new Natural Speciality products.
There was a loss of Pounds 221,000 at Florida Treatt, the US offshoot, but
there had been a significant improvement in the second half.
Turnover was Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 11m) and earnings per share came out at
8.53p (7.17p). The proposed final dividend is raised to 2.4p for a total of
3.4p (3.25p).
