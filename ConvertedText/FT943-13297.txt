FT  20 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Airtours acquires Menorcan complex


Airtours, the holidays group, has acquired the Binimar Holiday complex in
Menorca from Promotora Catalunya Mediteranea for Pta1.32bn (Pounds 6.8m)
cash.
The purchase has been funded from Airtours' existing cash resources.
The Binimar complex consists of three swimming pools, one restaurant, two
snack bars and 404 self-catering apartments.
Completion is expected on October 31.
