FT  13 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Drayton Korea net asset value higher


Drayton Korea Trust reported net asset value of 113.26p at September 30,
against 108.14p six months earlier.
In the six months to the end of September net revenue was 85,000 compared
with Pounds 152,000 for the period from its starting operation on December 3
1991 to September 30 1992. Earnings per share were 0.36p (0.66p).
