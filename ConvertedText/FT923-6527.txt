FT  24 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: US highway


EVERED BARDON's mid-Atlantic division will be the primary supplier of
aggregates to three large highway contracts on Route 50, the major highway
between Washington DC and the Maryland state capital, Annapolis. The
contracts total in excess of 750,000 tons of aggregate and have an
approximate value of USDollars 7m (Pounds 3.6m).
