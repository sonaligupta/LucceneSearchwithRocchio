FT  08 NOV 94 / Heathrow bus service buy-out


London Transport has privatised another of its bus companies with the sale
of London United Busways - operator of the Heathrow Airbus service - to its
management and employees for more than Pounds 25m.
This takes the proceeds from the sale of London's red bus companies to more
than Pounds 210m. London United has more than 450 buses and 1,400 employees.
It operates mainly in west London, including Hammersmith, Fulham and
Kingston.
