FT  22 OCT 94 / Press Association


The Press Association, the news agency which sold its Fleet Street
headquarters to Reuters in February, is buying 292 Vauxhall Bridge Road in
Victoria, London, from Banque Paribas for an undisclosed price and plans to
move next summer. The agency has been in Fleet Street for 126 years.
