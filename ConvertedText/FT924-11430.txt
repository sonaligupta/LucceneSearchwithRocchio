FT  26 OCT 92 / International Company News: GR (Holdings) declines 66%


GR (Holdings), which operates Grayshott Hall health centre, deals in
property and processes and merchants sheepskins, reported pre-tax profits
down 66 per cent from Pounds 1.62m to Pounds 549,000 in the year to June 30.
After an extraordinary charge of Pounds 356,000 attributable losses were
Pounds 14,600, compared with profits of Pounds 413,000.
Earnings per share came out at 1.4p (6.5p). The proposed final dividend is
1.6p (1.75p) for a total of 27p (2.15p) which includes a special 25p
dividend paid at the interim stage.
