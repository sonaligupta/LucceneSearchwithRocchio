FT  15 JUN 93 / Referendum on one-party rule


Malawian tea estate workers queue yesterday to vote in a referendum on
one-party rule. The ballot has aroused great enthusiasm, and opposition
leaders claim the vote will bring an end to 30 years of rule by
President-for-life Kamuzu Banda.
