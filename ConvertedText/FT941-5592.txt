FT  05 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Murray Intl net assets at 382.6p


Murray International Trust reported net asset value per share of 382.6p at
December 31 up from 293.2p a year earlier.
Net revenue for 1993 came out at Pounds 13.2m (Pounds 13m) for earnings per
share of 11.1p (10.9p) or 10.95p (10.75p) assuming full conversion of the B
shares.
As forecast the final dividend will be 3.5p for a total of 11.6p. It is now
forecasting a final for 1994 of 3.5p for an unchanged total of 11.6p.
