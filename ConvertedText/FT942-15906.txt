FT  13 APR 94 / People: Equitable Life Assurance Society


Sir Roland Smith is retiring as president of EQUITABLE LIFE Assurance
Society and will be replaced by John Sclater, chairman of Hill Samuel Bank,
Foreign & Colonial Investment Trust and Berisford International. Also
retiring as non-executive directors are Barry Sherlock and Sir Christopher
Waites; coming onto the board are Jennifer Page, chief executive of English
Heritage, and David Wilson, chairman and chief executive of Wilson Bowden.
