FT  30 OCT 93 / British Gas wins Chile pipeline deal


   By DAVID PILLING


   SANTIAGO


BRITISH GAS and Tenneco Gas of the US were yesterday awarded a contract to
operate a Dollars 1.65bn project to pipe natural gas 1,200km from Argentina
to Chile, writes David Pilling in Santiago. British Gas will take
responsibility for the 8,000km distribution network.
