FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Gieves Pounds 0.8m in the black


GIEVES Group, the retailer, licensor and publisher, achieved pre-tax profits
of Pounds 837,000 in the six months to July 31, against losses of Pounds
941,000.
Turnover of continuing operations was Pounds 8.84m (Pounds 8.3m). Earnings
per share came through at 5.4p (6.6p losses).
Mr Tom Scruby, chairman, said he expected the second half to show an
improvement over the first.
The shares gained 11p to 56p.
