FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: BBB Design


BBB Design Group, the USM-traded company which takes in design, marketing
and computer related services, reported a deficit of Pounds 154,000 pre-tax
for the 12 months to April 30.
The outcome, on turnover of Pounds 1.89m (Pounds 1.65m), compared with
restated losses of Pounds 427,000.
Losses per share narrowed from 4.57p to 1.84p.
