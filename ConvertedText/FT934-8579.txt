FT  15 NOV 93 / People: Tullett & Tokyo


Nigel Coupland has been appointed a director of TULLETT & TOKYO Forex
(London); Peter Leccacorvi a director of Tullett & Tokyo (Foreign Exchange)
Co; and Nicholas Brown a director of Tullett & Tokyo (Money Markets) Co.
John Lawrence and John Nixon have been appointed directors of Tullett &
Tokyo Forex International.
