FT  17 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Tonya Harding fined Dollars 100,000


Figure skater Tonya Harding was fined Dollars 100,000 (Pounds 68,500) and
sentenced to three years probation after pleading guilty at Portland,
Oregon, to hindering the investigation into the attack on rival skater Nancy
Kerrigan.
