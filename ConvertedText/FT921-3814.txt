FT  12 MAR 92 / Two shot dead in Algiers


   By REUTER


   ALGIERS


A gunman has shot dead the son of an Algerian diplomat and a 16-year-old
messenger in a diplomatic district of Algiers, Reuter reports from Algiers.
The attack did not appear to be politically motivated and the gunman was
arrested. The messenger worked for a political party headed by Mr Kasdi
Merbah, a former prime minister.
