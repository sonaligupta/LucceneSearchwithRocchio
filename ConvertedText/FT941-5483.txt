FT  05 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Tonya Harding attacked


US figure skating champion Tonya Harding was attacked by a man in a park in
her home town of Portland, Oregon. Miss Harding, involved in a police
investigation into an attack on rival Nancy Kerrigan, suffered scrapes to
her knees and elbows.
