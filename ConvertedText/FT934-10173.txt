FT  06 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: Gloom for smaller companies - At a

Small company shares did not escape the general gloom in the equity markets
this week. The Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version)
fell 0.2 per cent from 1614.16 to 1611.71 over the seven days to November 4.
