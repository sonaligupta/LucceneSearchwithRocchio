FT  30 OCT 93 / Community care funds announced


LOCAL authorities are to get Pounds 1.27bn next year to spend on community
care, Pounds 20m more than originally promised, Dr Brian Mawhinney, health
minister, announced yesterday. He said the amount would rise to Pounds
1.83bn in 1995-96 and to Pounds 2.2bn in 1996-97.
Local authorities associations said the funding was at least Pounds 150m
short of what was needed.
