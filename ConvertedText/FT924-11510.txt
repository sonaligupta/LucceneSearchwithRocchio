FT  26 OCT 92 / Daewoo chief denies poll bid


   By JOHN BURTON


   SEOUL


MR Kim Woo-Choong, founder and chairman of Daewoo, South Korea's fourth
largest business group, last night denied he would be a candidate in
December's presidential election, writes John Burton in Seoul. It had been
reported the New Korea party (NKP), formed last Friday, would offer him its
nomination.
