FT  11 MAR 94 / International Company News: Cars unit plans more plants


   By REUTER


   GENEVA


Hyundai Motor, which plans to raise its annual output of cars to 2m by the
year 2000, said it also planned to open factories in North America, Europe,
south-east Asia and the Middle East, Reuter reports from Geneva.
'We intend to move closer to our customers by building more overseas
production sites,' said Mr Se Yung Chung, chairman of Hyundai Motor.
The company has already announced ambitious plans to become one of the
world's top 10 car producers by the end of the century. 'I can say that our
expansion projects are now under full swing,' said Mr Chung.
Hyundai Motor currently produces 1.25m cars a year, 1.15m in Korea and
100,000 overseas.
