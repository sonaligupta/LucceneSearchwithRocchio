FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Hand-knitting setback at Sirdar


Turnover at Sirdar, the textiles and hand-knitting yarns concern, was static
at Pounds 27.1m for the six months to December 31, but pre-tax profits fell
from Pounds 2.81m to Pounds 2.67m.
There was an increase in operating profits at the textiles sector from
Pounds 2.52m to Pounds 2.81m, but the contribution from hand-knitting yarns
fell from Pounds 698,000 to Pounds 86,000.
Earnings per share were 3.12p (3.25p). The interim dividend is maintained at
1.65p.
