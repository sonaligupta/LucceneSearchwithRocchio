FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Carnell declines


For the six months ended September 30 pre-tax profits of Carnell fell from
Pounds 167,000 to Pounds 130,000, despite turnover of Pounds 1.23m, against
Pounds 848,000.
Earnings of this USM-traded concern were 0.56p (0.86p) while the interim
dividend is 0.25p (nil). The directors expect to recommend a final dividend
of 0.326p.
