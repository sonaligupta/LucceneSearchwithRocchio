FT  12 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Reuters completes Dollars 125m purchase


Reuters Holdings, the financial information and news group, has completed
the Dollars 125.1m (Pounds 86m) cash purchase of California-based Teknekron
Software Systems.
The Federal Trade Commission of the US has allowed the acquisition to go
through without challenge.
