FT  30 OCT 93 / UK Company News: James Fisher


Profits at James Fisher fell from Pounds 1.81m to Pounds 304,000 pre-tax for
the half year to end-June and the interim dividend is omitted - 0.5p was
paid previously.
Turnover fell from Pounds 17.5m to Pounds 15.9m.
