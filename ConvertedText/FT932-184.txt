FT  30 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Disclosure rules to be unaffected


NEW requirements arising from an EC directive would not affect the UK's
fundamental rules on the disclosure of interests in the shares of public
companies, Mr Neil Hamilton, corporate affairs minister, said last night.
MPs approved the draft disclosure of interests in shares regulations 1993,
which change some provisions in part six of the 1985 companies act.
