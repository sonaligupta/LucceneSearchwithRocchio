FT  30 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: Halifax raises rates - At a glance


Halifax has raised interest rates on two fixed-rate investment accounts by
up to a percentage point. Gross rates on the minimum of Pounds 2,000 in
Guaranteed Reserve are now from 4.7 per cent on a six-month deposit, to 7
per cent for three years. The corresponding rates on Pounds 10,000 are 5.1
per cent and 7.15 per cent. Returns on Stepped Income Reserve, a five year
account which pays a return which increases each year, are 6 per cent, 6.5
per cent, 7.5 per cent, 8.5 per cent and 10 per cent gross in years one to
five.
