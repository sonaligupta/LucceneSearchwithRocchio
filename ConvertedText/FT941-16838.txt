FT  07 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Wm Morrison sales ahead


Wm Morrison Supermarkets reported takings up 12.9 per cent in the five weeks
to January 2.
Most of the increase came from new stores with a rise of 2.8 per cent from
stores opened before December 1992.
