FT  29 APR 94 / Observer: Tough guys


British Telecom's managers do not relish losing their performance-related
pay. Their union, the Society of Telecom Executives, reports outbreaks of
militancy across the UK, with meetings and pay-slip burnings. Future
'industrial action' not only includes pickets and the like, but boat trips,
a carnival and a visit to a brewery.
