FT  27 OCT 92 / International Company News: Continental loss grows to
Dollars 40.4m


   By KAREN ZAGOR


CONTINENTAL Airlines, the US carrier under bankruptcy protection and at the
centre of a bidding war, saw its third-quarter net loss grow to Dollars
40.4m from Dollars 37.3m a year ago on flat revenues of Dollars 1.4bn,
writes Karen Zagor. Operating loss was Dollars 16.3m, against operating
income of Dollars 3.4m last year. For the first nine months, Continental
made a net loss of Dollars 150m on revenues of Dollars 4.1bn against a
deficit of Dollars 351.1m on revenues of Dollars 4bn.
