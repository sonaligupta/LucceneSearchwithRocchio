FT  21 DEC 92 / Hunt for Christmas bargains


Shoppers approached Christmas with caution yesterday at Lakeside shopping
centre in Thurrock, Essex. They came in their thousands but were intent on
finding bargains. 'Any high-priced goods have been very slow,' said Lakeside
merchants' association chairman Bob Kurring. In Manchester and Sheffield
stores reported exceptionally busy Sunday trading.
