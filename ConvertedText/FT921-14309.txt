FT  16 JAN 92 / Observer: Own gaol


Sent to prison as a first offender, an English graduate was told by an old
lag there that if he made amorous advances to the governor's wife, she'd get
him released quickly.
'But I can't do that,' he protested. 'It's wrong to end a sentence with a
proposition.'
