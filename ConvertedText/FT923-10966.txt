FT  28 JUL 92 / People: SIB terms of office extended


Ralph Quartano, a director of Clerical Medical & General Life Assurance
Society, and Lord Runciman, chairman of Andrew Weir and Co and of Runciman
Investments, have been re-appointed deputy chairmen of the SECURITIES AND
INVESTMENTS BOARD, for a period of one and two years, respectively.
Other members of the board have also been re-appointed from July 31: John
Gardiner, for three years, Norman Lessels for two years, and Brian
Williamson for one year.
Meanwhile, Dame Rachel Waterhouse and Denis Child will retire on completion
of their terms of office on July 31 1992.
