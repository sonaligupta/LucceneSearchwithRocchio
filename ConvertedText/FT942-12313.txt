FT  29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Operating costs hit Berry Birch


Increased operating expenses at Berry Birch & Noble resulted in a reduction
in pre-tax profits in the year to January 31 from Pounds 1.06m to Pounds
670,000.
In January, the USM-quoted financial services group had warned of an
expected lower result and the market reacted yesterday by raising the share
price 16p to 134p.
Sales from continuing operations rose 11 per cent to Pounds 7.1m (Pounds
6.4m), but operating expenses increased by Pounds 1m to Pounds 6.51m.
As forecast, a maintained final dividend of 3.3p makes a 5.4p (5.3p) total.
Earnings per share declined to 7.6p (12.8p).
