FT  22 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Alcatel in China


   By AP-DJ


   CANBERRA


Alcatel Australia, a unit of Alcatel Alsthom of France, has signed contracts
valued at ADollars 56m (Pounds 26.6m) to provide telecommunications
equipment to China, AP-DJ reports from Canberra.
The company will supply and install digital switching exchanges to the
autonomous regions of Tibet and Ningxia and the province of Gansu.
