FT  23 JUL 93 / US, Belarus sign nuclear pact


   By REUTER


   WASHINGTON


The United States and Belarus yesterday signed agreements for Washington to
provide money and expertise to help the former Soviet republic fulfil its
promise to become a non-nuclear state, Reuter reports from Washington. The
US is to provide up to Dollars 59m (Pounds 39.3m) to help Belarus safely
ship some 80 SS-25 long-range strategic nuclear missiles to Russia.
