FT  22 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Aviva rights issue 60.65% subscribed


Aviva Petroleum, the Dallas-based oil and gas company quoted on the London
Stock Exchange, yesterday said its 2-for-1 rights issue at 27p per share,
which closed last Friday, had been 60.65 per cent subscribed.
A further 10.47 per cent was placed in the market on Tuesday at 28p per
share.
Before expenses, funds raised were Dollars 5.5m (Pounds 3.4m), of which
Dollars 2.8m will be used to reduce debt to Royal Bank of Scotland to
Dollars 12.2m.
The balance will go into working capital.
Aviva's shares firmed 1p to 31 1/2p.
