FT  05 AUG 92 / Senate backs supercollider


   By REUTER


   WASHINGTON


THE US SENATE has blocked a denial of nearly all funding for the
supercollider, a giant atom smasher being built in Texas, Reuter reports
from Washington.
It voted 62:32 late on Monday against joining the House of Representatives
in cutting Dollars 516m of the Dollars 550m allocated for the project.
