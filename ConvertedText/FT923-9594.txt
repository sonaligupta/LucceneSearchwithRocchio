FT  04 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: London stabbing


Anthony Travers, London representative of the rightwing Afrikaner Resistance
Movement (AWB) and an observer at the libel trial of Jani Allan, was stabbed
in a pub during the lunch break.  She is suing Channel 4 over allegations
that she had an affair with AWB leader Eugene Terre Blanche.
