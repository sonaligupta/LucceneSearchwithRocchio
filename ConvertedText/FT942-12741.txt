FT  28 APR 94 / People: Finance moves


Rodney Schwartz is joining PARIBAS capital markets to head its financial
institutions group in the investment banking department. He moves from
Lehman Brothers, Europe, where he was md and co-head of equities since 1991;
before that he was an analyst of European banks for Paine Webber.
