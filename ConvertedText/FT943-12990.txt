FT  22 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: S Africans back at Lord's


The South Africans, emerging from sporting isolation with the end of
apartheid, began their first cricket series against England for 29 years. At
the end of the first day at Lord's they were 244 for six, with captain
Kepler Wessels making 105.
