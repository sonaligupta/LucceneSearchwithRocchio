FT  08 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cobham US deal


Chelton, a subsidiary of Cobham, formerly FR Group, has acquired Comant, a
leading US aircraft antenna manufacturer, for Dollars 3.25m (Pounds 1.98m)
cash.
Comant, based in Los Angeles, is operating profitably with annual turnover
of more than Dollars 3m.
