FT  22 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Asprey takes 6.8% stake in Mallett


Asprey, the USM-quoted jeweller, yesterday announced that it had acquired a
stake of 950,000 shares in Mallett, an antique dealer. This represents a
holding of 6.8 per cent.
Mallett's shares jumped 9p to close at 45p while Asprey's were 3p higher at
285p.
Mr Naim Attallah, Asprey's chief executive, said Asprey was interested in
Mallett as an investment. 'Who knows what will happen in the future?' he
said. 'I cannot comment at this stage.'
