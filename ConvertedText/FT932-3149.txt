FT  16 JUN 93 / World News in Brief: Former world champion James Hunt dies


James Hunt, world formula one motor racing champion in 1976, died after a
heart attack at his home in Wimbledon, south London. He was 45. Hunt, whose
greatest successes were with the McLaren team, drove in 92 Formula one
grands prix, winning 10. He retired in 1979.
