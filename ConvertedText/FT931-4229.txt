FT  11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Grahams Rintoul revenue static


Net asset value per share of Grahams Rintoul Investment Trust fell from
123.8p to 113.9p over the year to end-December after deducting prior charges
at par.
However, in the first two months of 1993 the value improved by 25 per cent
to 142.6p.
Available revenue for 1992 edged ahead to Pounds 864,000 (Pounds 856,000).
After preference dividend payments losses per ordinary share emerged at 2.2p
(1.53p). Dividend is a same-again 0.25p.
