FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: GEI jumps to Pounds 1m


On turnover just lower at Pounds 37.4m, against Pounds 37.6m, GEI
International, the packaging and processing machinery concern, boosted
pre-tax profits from Pounds 131,000 to Pounds 1.04m for the six months to
September 30.
Earnings per share were well ahead at 2.12p (0.21p) and the group is
returning to the interim dividend list with a 1p net payment - last year's
single, final, distribution was 1.5p from pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.13m.
