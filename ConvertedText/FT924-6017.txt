FT  24 NOV 92 / Argentine defence clash


   By Our Correspondent


   BUENOS AIRES


ARGENTINA'S armed forces' joint chief of staff, Brigadier General Andres
Antonietti, has resigned his post and requested his retirement from the air
force, writes Our Correspondent in Buenos Aires.
A Defence Ministry source said the brigadier had clashed over military and
personal issues with Mr Antonio Erman Gonzalez, the defence minister.
