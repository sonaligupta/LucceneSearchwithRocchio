FT  20 APR 91 / UK Company News: British Empire Secs asset value improves


At March 31, net asset value of British Empire Securities and General Trust
had improved to 59.4p, from 52.53p six months' earlier.
The value at March 31 1990 was 60.9p.
In the six months ended March 31 gross income fell to Pounds 1.74m (Pounds
1.9m) and net revenue to Pounds 544,000 (Pounds 618,000). Earnings per share
were 0.41p (0.46p) and the interim dividend is again 0.25p.
