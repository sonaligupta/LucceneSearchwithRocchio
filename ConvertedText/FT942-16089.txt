FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Rathbone Bros advances 29%


Rathbone Brothers, the asset management and private banking group, reported
profits before tax of Pounds 5.25m for the 1993 year.
The outcome, a rise of 29 per cent on the previous year's total of Pounds
4.08m, was achieved on turnover of Pounds 17.7m (Pounds 15m).
A recommended final dividend of 5.5p brings the total to 7.5p (6p). Earnings
emerged at 18.5p (16.07p).
