FT  27 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Danka doubled at Pounds 22m for nine months


Acquisitions helped Danka Business Systems, the office equipment supplier,
which operates mainly in the US, virtually double pre-tax profits from
Pounds 11.6m to Pounds 22.1m for the nine months to December 31.
Sales were Pounds 233.6m (Pounds 127.5m).
Mr Dan Doyle, chief executive, said that 'the company will continue to
search for strategic acquisitions to serve as regional centres in new
geographic areas'.
So far this year, Danka has spent more than Pounds 45m on acquisitions.
Earnings in the third quarter were held back by higher tax and remained
steady at 2.9p.
