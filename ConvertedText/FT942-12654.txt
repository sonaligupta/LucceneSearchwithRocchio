FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News: Venturi net asset value increases


Diluted net asset value per ordinary geared share of Venturi Investment
Trust jumped 86 per cent, from 13.92p to 25.84p, over the 12 months to March
31. The value per capital indexed share increased from 287.87p to 308.64p.
Earnings available came through at Pounds 263,482 (Pounds 241,520) giving a
per share value of 4.39p, compared with 4.03p.
A final distribution of 2.19p lifts the total to 3.75p (3.45p).
