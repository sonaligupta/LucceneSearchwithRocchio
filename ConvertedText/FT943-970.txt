FT  27 SEP 94 / Inner Birmingham development deal


Birmingham Heartlands Development Corporation has selected St Modwen
Developments, a local property company, as its partner in the construction
of a warehouse and industrial park on 40 acres of land once owned by Leyland
Daf Vans.
Work on the Pounds 4.5m site will start next year.
