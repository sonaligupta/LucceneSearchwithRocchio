FT  16 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Burmine


BURMINE has received valid acceptances in respect of 19.3m Europa Minerals
Group ordinary shares (78.5 per cent) and has extended its offer to March
29. Acceptances of the linked Austmin offer had been received in respect of
45.1m fully paid ordinary shares, (63.9 per cent).
