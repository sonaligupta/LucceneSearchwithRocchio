FT  13 NOV 92 / Nato, WEU to liaise efforts


   By REUTER


   BONN


MR Manfred Worner, Nato secretary-general, yesterday said there was no
serious rivalry between the Atlantic alliance and the Western European Union
(WEU), Reuter reports from Bonn.
He agreed with WEU secretary-general Willem van Eekelen that both
organisations had to co-ordinate their work to avoid duplicating efforts.
