FT  29 JAN 92 / Observer: Professional view


A reader telephoned the Chartered Institute of Bankers to ask for his exam
results, and was told he would have to pay for a detailed breakdown. He
offered to pay by credit card, but was refused. The institute says the rates
charged by credit card companies (all owned by banks) are too high.
