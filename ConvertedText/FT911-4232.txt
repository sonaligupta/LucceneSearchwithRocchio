FT  20 APR 91 / International Company News: Colgate-Palmolive posts 12%
advance


   By KAREN ZAGOR


COLGATE-Palmolive, the second-biggest US household products manufacturer,
yesterday posted a 12 per cent improvement in first-quarter net income to
Dollars 91.3m, or Dollars 1.30 a share, on sales which rose 12 per cent to
Dollars 1.5bn.
A year earlier, Colgate recorded first quarter net income of Dollars 81.2m,
or Dollars 1.16 a share, on sales of Dollars 1.3bn.
Mr Reuben Mark, chairman and chief executive, said Colgate US and Hill's Pet
Products, Colgate's two big US businesses, posted double digit earning
increases.
Adjusting earnings per share for a two-for-one stock split with a record
date of April 25, 1991, profits in the latest quarter were 65 cents, against
58 cents in 1990. The company's earnings before interest and taxes grew 17
per cent to Dollars 160.7m in the 1991 quarter.
