FT  05 AUG 93 / Balfour Beatty fined over death


BALFOUR Beatty was fined Pounds 17,500 yesterday after pleading guilty to
breaching safety rules at its Derbyshire foundry where a worker was crushed
to death. It was ordered to pay the Health and Safety Executive's costs of
Pounds 1,246.
