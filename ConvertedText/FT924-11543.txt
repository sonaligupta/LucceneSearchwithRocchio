FT  24 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Overseas Investment net asset value lower


Overseas Investment Trust reported net asset value of 268.8p at September 30
down from 279.3p a year earlier.
For the year to the end of September net revenue was Pounds 1.34m (Pounds
1.25m) for earnings per share of 3.53p (3.3p). An increased final dividend
of 2p has been proposed for a total payment of 2.8p (2.5p).
