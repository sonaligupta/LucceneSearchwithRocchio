FT  25 AUG 92 / UK Company News: TR High Income


TR High Income Trust had a net asset value of 101.1p per share at June 30,
up from 96.3p 12 months earlier. At the December year-end the figure was
94.7p.
Net revenue for the six months to end-June rose to Pounds 683,000 (Pounds
447,000), equivalent to earnings of 2.66p (2.4p) per share.
As already announced, a second interim dividend of 1.4p brings the total so
far this year to 2p (2.8p).
