FT  17 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Selective Assets value falls


At the end of 1991 net asset value of Selective Assets Trust, which is aimed
is long-term capital growth, stood at 126.5p per share.
That compared with 138.8p six months earlier, and with 107.4p at the end of
1990.
Nearly 33 per cent of the portfolio was in the UK, 28 per cent in the US,
and 9.3 per cent in the Far East.
Total income for the year fell from Pounds 2.93m to Pounds 2.69m. Earnings
dropped to 1.4p (1.76p) but the dividend is lifted to 0.825p (0.75p).
