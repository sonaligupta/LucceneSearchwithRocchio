FT  01 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Coca-Cola - Contracts and ventures


   By EXTEL


   HONG KONG


Coca-Cola said it would begin building a bottling plant in Zhengzhou in
Henan Province, central China. The Dollars 18m facility is the fourth
joint-venture Coca-Cola plant in China.
