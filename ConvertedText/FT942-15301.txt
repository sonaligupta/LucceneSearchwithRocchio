FT  15 APR 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Financial Times Gold Mines
Index


The FT Gold Mines Index Committee has announced that Ashanti Goldfields
appears likely to meet the eligibility requirements of the FT Gold Mines
Index after its scheduled public listing on April 26. The stock is expected
to be added to the Index with effect from April 27.
