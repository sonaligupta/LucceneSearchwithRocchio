FT  12 MAR 92 / World Trade News: Rolls in China deal


   By PAUL BETTS


Rolls-Royce has made a breakthrough in the Chinese market for its Tay
engine, with an agreement to supply Pounds 20m of engines to China Eastern
Airlines, Paul Betts writes.
The Shanghai-based airline is buying seven Fokker 100 twin-engine regional
jets, to be powered by Rolls-Royce Tay 650 engines.
