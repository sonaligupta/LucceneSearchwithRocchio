FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: EFT advances to Pounds 1.45m


EFT Group, a provider of asset finance, reported pre-tax profits up from
Pounds 591,000 to Pounds 1.45m for 1992.
The results were prepared on the FRS 3 accounting basis with comparisons
restated. The published pre-tax profit figure for 1991 was Pounds 1.07m.
Earnings per share were 3.01p (0.58p). The total dividend is increased from
1.1p to 1.3p, with a proposed final of 0.9p.
