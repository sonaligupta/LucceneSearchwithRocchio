FT  16 JAN 92 / World News in Brief Forged banknotes seized


Police in London seized forged Dutch banknotes with a face value of Fl 60m
(Pounds 18.7m) in their biggest haul of counterfeit cash. Scotland Yard said
the money, in Fl 100 notes, was discovered in a raid on a house in Willesden
Green. A man was arrested.
