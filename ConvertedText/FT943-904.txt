FT  27 SEP 94 / Observer: Pond life


Not so much life imitating art, more a case of life mocking artefacts.
Michael Jack will be the first British government minister to use the
Channel tunnel for business purposes when he travels to Brussels today for a
European council meeting. The Chunnel is now leaking, thanks to drainage
problems. Jack, of course, is minister for fisheries.
