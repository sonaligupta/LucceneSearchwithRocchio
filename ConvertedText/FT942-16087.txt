FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Magnolia shows second half deficit


Magnolia Group, the picture frame and reproductions company, showed a small
loss in the second half of 1993.
Profit before tax for the full year came through at Pounds 72,000, compared
with Pounds 76,000 in the first six months. The result represented a
reduction compared with Pounds 84,000 in 1992 and Pounds 401,000 the year
before.
Earnings per share were lower at 1.08p (1.89p) and the dividend is held at
0.1p.
