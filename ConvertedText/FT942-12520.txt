FT  29 APR 94 / Jury still out


The jury in the trial of Mr Nazmu Virani had still not reached verdicts
yesterday. Mr Virani, who is accused of helping artificially to boost the
profits of the Bank of Credit and Commerce International, denies charges of
theft, false accounting and furnishing false information.
