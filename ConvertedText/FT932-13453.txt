FT  23 APR 93 / Cable TV move by French group


GENERALE des Eaux, the French water and energy group, said yesterday it was
increasing its British cable interests.
General Cable, a subsidiary of the French group, has purchased or agreed to
purchase cable television franchises covering Barnsley, Halifax and
Brighouse and Wakefield in the Yorkshire area. The company is also in
discussions to include Huddersfield and Dewsbury in a new regional franchise
covering 1m homes in the area.
Mr Marc Noblet, managing director of General Cable, said yesterday the total
project would involve investment of more than Pounds 500m over 10 years.
