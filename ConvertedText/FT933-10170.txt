FT  05 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Malvern UK Index assets edge higher


Malvern UK Index Trust, managed by Edinburgh Fund Managers, had a net asset
value per share, excluding retained earnings, of 128.07p at June 30, up from
123.37p at the trust's December year-end.
Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 974,000 (Pounds 983,000),
equivalent to earnings of 2.01p (2.03p) per share.
The interim dividend is maintained at 1.7p. The directors said that earnings
for the full year would be fully distributed; the final is expected to be
not less than 2p.
