FT  22 DEC 92 / Oilfield go-ahead


KERR-McGEE, the UK arm of the US resources company, received approval
yesterday to develop the Gryphon oilfield in the North Sea. The company
estimates that the field contains 96m barrels of oil. Production is due to
begin next October.
