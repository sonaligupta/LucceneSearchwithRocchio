FT  25 MAR 92 / International Company News: Van Heusen shows gain


   By BLOOMBERG


PHILLIPS-Van Heusen saw net income for the fourth quarter rise to Dollars
8.5m, or 32 cents a share, from Dollars 5.2m, or 17 cents, in the
year-earlier period, Bloomberg reports.
The US clothing manufacturer said sales in the three months ended February 2
had risen to Dollars 232.1m from Dollars 218.4m in the year-earlier period.
For the year to February, net income advanced to Dollars 31.1m, or Dollars
1.15, up from Dollars 26.4m, or 85 cents, a year earlier. Sales rose to
Dollars 904.1m from Dollars 806.3m.
