FT  08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: GE Power Systems - Contracts and Ventures


   By ANDREW BAXTER


   LONDON


GE Power Systems, part of General Electric of the US, has signed a contract
worth nearly Dollars 500m for the 2,000MW expansion of a Korea Electric
Power Corporation combined-cycle power plant. GE will be the prime
contractor for blocks three and four of the Seoinchon plant south-west of
Seoul.
