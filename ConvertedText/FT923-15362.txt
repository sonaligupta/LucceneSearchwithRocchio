FT  02 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Care transfer to go ahead


THE PLANNED transfer to councils of responsibility for people in nursing and
residential homes will go ahead on schedule in April next year, the
government insisted last night.
A clash over community care between government departments has led to
reports that consideration was being given to postponing the move.
