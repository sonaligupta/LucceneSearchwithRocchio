FT  11 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): Smiths Industries - Other

The medical systems and aerospace components group, is expected to report
increased interim pre-tax profits of Pounds 46.5m, against Pounds 40.3m, on
Wednesday. The improvement is thought to have been underpinned by strong
sales by the medical division and contributions from recent acquisitions.
