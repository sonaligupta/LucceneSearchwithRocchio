FT  16 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 8.3m placing at John Wood


JOHN WOOD Group, the Aberdeen-based oilfield services company, is broadening
its shareholder base by placing Pounds 8.3m-worth of convertible preference
shares with 13 institutions.
No cash is being raised for the company but the new shares will, on
conversion, account for 8.7 per cent of John Wood's equity.
