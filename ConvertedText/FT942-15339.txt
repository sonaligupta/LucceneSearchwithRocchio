FT  15 APR 94 / UK Company News: One-off items hit Estates & Agency


A number of one-off items left pre-tax profits at Estates & Agency Holdings,
the property investment company, lower at Pounds 283,000 for the six months
to December 31, against Pounds 416,000.
The company said that costs relating to the sale of a site in Plymouth,
offset by non-recurring income, cut operating profits by Pounds 340,000 to
Pounds 249,000 (Pounds 416,000).
The sale resulted in an exceptional credit of Pounds 34,000 being the net
surplus over the latest valuation.
Gross income was Pounds 2.65m (Pounds 2.48m). Earnings per share came out at
3.94p (6.89p). The interim dividend is raised to 3.5p (0.5p).
