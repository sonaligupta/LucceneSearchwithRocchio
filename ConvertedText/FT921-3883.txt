FT  12 MAR 92 / UK Company News: EW Fact advances 17% to Pounds 1.01m


EW Fact, the USM-quoted tuition services group, lifted pre-tax profits by 17
per cent from Pounds 867,000 to Pounds 1.01m in the year to December 31
1991.
Mr Emile Woolf, chairman, said the result had been achieved in spite of the
worldwide recession.
Earnings per share emerged at 10.53p (8.98p) and the directors propose to
pay an enhanced final dividend of 3.1p (2.75p) for a 4.31p (3.85p) total.
Turnover for the year advanced to Pounds 4.59m (Pounds 4.42m).
