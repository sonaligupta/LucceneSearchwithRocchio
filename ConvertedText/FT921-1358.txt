FT  25 MAR 92 / World News in Brief: Kurdish protests in Germany:


About 300 Kurds demonstrating against action by security forces against
Kurdish rebels in Turkey occupied the North Rhine-Westphalia state
parliament for 30 minutes before leaving peacefully. Earlier a Turkish bank
in Wiesbaden was firebombed.
