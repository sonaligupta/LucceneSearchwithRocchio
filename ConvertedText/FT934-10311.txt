FT  05 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Airliner skids into harbour


All 296 people aboard a Boeing 747 of Taiwan's China Airlines scrambled down
escape chutes to safety after the airliner skidded off a runway into Hong
Kong harbour during a severe tropical storm. Twenty-three people were taken
to hospital with minor injuries and shock.
