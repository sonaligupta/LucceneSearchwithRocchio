FT  20 APR 94 / Pounds 7m land sale


Land with outline planning consent for 1,000 houses on the edge of
Caerphilly, south Wales, has been sold for more than Pounds 7m to Cofton
Land and Property (Caerphilly). The 147-acre site was owned by Shireview, an
Ipswich-based development company now in receivership.
