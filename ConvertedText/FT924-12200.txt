FT  22 OCT 92 / Tea group raises own security force


   By REUTER


   GUWAHATI, INDIA


The Indian Tea Association (ITA) will raise its own security force to
protect estates in the north-east state of Assam, which is plagued by
insurgencies, ITA chairman Hemendra Prasad Baruah said, Reuter reports from
Guwahati, India.
He said the organisation of Indian tea growers would recruit a force with an
initial strength of 7,000 to 8,000 men drawn from retired soldiers.
