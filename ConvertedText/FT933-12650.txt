FT  23 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Welsh houses to be sold off


THE STOCK of 1,259 houses owned by the Development Board for Rural Wales is
to be sold to housing associations or local authorities under plans
announced yesterday by Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary.
Most of the houses are in Newtown, Powys. Mr Redwood said tenants' views
would be taken into account in drawing up a shortlist of prospective
landlords and valuation would be determined independently.
