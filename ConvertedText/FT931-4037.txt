FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Losses of IPounds 1.86m for changed Reflex


Reflex Investments, the Dublin-based company which is switching from
computer leasing to provision of software, incurred a pre-tax loss of
IPounds 1.86m (Pounds 1.9m) in the eight months to December 31, compared
with IPounds 2.44m in the previous 12 months.
The figure included exceptional expansion costs of IPounds 409,000 (IPounds
1.93m). Extraordinary reorganisation costs of IPounds 2.67m (IPounds 1.35m)
left the loss for the period at IPounds 4.86m (IPounds 3.52m).
Turnover was IPounds 5.58m (IPounds 13.5m). Losses per share were 10.81p
(13.52p).
