FT  12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Pochin's mounts recovery to Pounds 1.6m


Pochin's, the Cheshire-based contracting and plant hire group, saw pre-tax
profits recover to Pounds 1.6m, against Pounds 1.08m, in the six months to
November 30.
Turnover was Pounds 24.4m (Pounds 14.2m) resulting from large design and
build projects and the company's own developments.
Earnings per share were 110.5p (73.5p) and the interim dividend is an
unchanged 8p.
