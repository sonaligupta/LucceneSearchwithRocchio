FT  30 APR 94 / UK Company News: British Assets raises dividend


Net asset value of British Assets Trust was virtually unchanged at 106p at
March 31 1994 compared with 105.9p a year earlier.
The second quarterly dividend is raised from 1.07p to 1.09p per share making
2.18p (2.14p) to date.
Revenue before tax for the six months to March 31 was Pounds 11.92m (Pounds
12.14m) with net earnings coming through at Pounds 6.9m (Pounds 7m).
Dividends required a transfer from reserves of Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 1.2m).
Earnings were 1.8p (1.82p).
