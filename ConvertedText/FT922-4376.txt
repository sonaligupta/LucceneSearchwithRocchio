FT  05 JUN 92 / Executives jailed for Pounds 8m fraud


TWO executives from Action Games and Toys, one of Britain's largest
independent companies, were jailed yesterday for an Pounds 8m fraud aimed at
keeping the firm alive.
Mr Brian George, managing director, of Henley, Oxfordshire, was sentenced at
Oxford Crown Court to 4 1/2 years and Mr Roger Richardson, former head of
finance, now living in St Cyprien, France, to three years.
