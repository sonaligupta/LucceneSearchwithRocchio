FT  12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Burmine


BURMINE, Australian mining company, has declared its offer for Europa, the
UK-quoted mining finance house, unconditional having received valid
acceptances representing 74 per cent of Europa's issued capital. Burmine
also received acceptances in respect of 70 per cent of issued capital of
Austmin Gold and declared its offer for this Australian mining company
unconditional. This enables the three-way merger to go ahead.
