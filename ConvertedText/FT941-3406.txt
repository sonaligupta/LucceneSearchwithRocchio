FT  16 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Central European Growth Fund


CENTRAL EUROPEAN Growth Fund has received applications in respect of 24.7m
ordinary shares (with warrants attached). Accordingly, the international
placing has been increased and 175m ordinary shares (with warrants) have
been placed firm, making the total size of the issue 200m shares (with
warrants). Some Dollars 200m (Pounds 137m) has been raised. Dealings are
expected to start on March 21.
