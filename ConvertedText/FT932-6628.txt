FT  27 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Monks net asset value improves


Net asset value of Monks Investment Trust improved during the year to April
30 from 409p to 472.2p per share.
Net revenue advanced to Pounds 6.12m (Pounds 5.76m), equivalent to earnings
per share of 7.89p (7.43p). The dividend is being held at 6.7p via a
proposed unchanged final of 4.7p.
