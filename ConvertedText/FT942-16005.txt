FT  13 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Kissinger launches S Africa mediation


Former US secretary of state Henry Kissinger (left) launched an
international mediation mission in South Africa by calling on all parties to
ensure its efforts were not in vain. Kissinger, flanked by six other
mediators, told a news conference in Johannesburg that South Africa's
transition to black majority rule was important for the whole world.
