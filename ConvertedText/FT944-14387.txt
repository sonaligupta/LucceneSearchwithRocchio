FT  24 OCT 94 / Tory party funding linked to Kuwait


The Conservative party received ten of thousands of pounds in secret
contributions from a merchant bank with close links to the Kuwait
government. According to documents obtained by the Financial Times, the bank
was financed to a substantial extent by a Panamanian corporation which
received about Pounds 100m from the Kuwait government.
