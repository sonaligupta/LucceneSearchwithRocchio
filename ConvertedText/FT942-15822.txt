FT  13 APR 94 / International Company News: Static profits at TF1


TF1, the largest French television channel, posted static profits last year
despite the downturn in the advertising industry.
The company mustered net profits of FFr459m in 1993, 2 per cent up on 1992's
FFr451m, on turnover up 4 per cent over the same period at FFr7.76bn from
FFr7.43bn.
