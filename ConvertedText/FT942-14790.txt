FT  19 APR 94 / Management (The Growing Business): BVCA members invest
Pounds 1.4bn - In a Nutshell


UK venture capitalists invested Pounds 1.4bn in 1,202 companies worldwide in
1993, almost the same amount as the previous year, according to the British
Venture Capital Association.
Nineteen per cent of the number of financings were in start-ups and early
stage businesses. By value management buy-outs and buy-ins accounted for
Pounds 769m, or 62 per cent of the amount invested. The average size of
MBO/MBI increased by 38 per cent to Pounds 3.3m.
