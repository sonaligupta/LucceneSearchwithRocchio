FT  13 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Jupiter net assets rise at 16 months


Net assets per share of Jupiter European Investment Trust amounted to 89.4p
at the end of 16 months to August 31 1993, compared with 86.02p four months
earlier.
Net revenue surged from Pounds 14,247 for the year ended April 30 1992 to
Pounds 509,224 giving earnings of 4.54p (0.24p) per share. The dividend is
stepped up to 2.1p net (0.1p) with a final for the 16 months of 1.1p.
