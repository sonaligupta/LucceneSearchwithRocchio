FT  12 MAR 94 / Finance and the Family: High income trust launch - At a
glance


Abtrust is launching a new investment trust, the Abtrust High Income Trust,
which aims to offer a yield of about 7 per cent by investing in
high-yielding shares of other investment trusts, mainly the income shares of
split capital trusts. It will also offer the potential for some capital
growth.
