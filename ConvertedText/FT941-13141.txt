FT  27 JAN 94 / FO spends Pounds 10m on education


The Foreign Office spends nearly Pounds 10m a year on private education and
boarding school allowances for the children of its personnel, Mr Alastair
Goodlad, foreign office minister, said last night.
In a parliamentary written answer he said that in 1992-93 the department
spent Pounds 7.88m on 744 children attending boarding schools in the UK and
a further Pounds 2m on those educated locally at their parents' post.
The corresponding figures in 1988-89 were Pounds 7.17m and Pounds 1.54m.
