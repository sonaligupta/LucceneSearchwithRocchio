FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief


SERCO is to acquire THA, a Canadian aviation company, for Pounds 1.25m, of
which Pounds 1.13m is payable on completion. The vendors are James F
Hickling management consultants of Ottawa and US aviation consultancy Jerry
Thompson & Associates.
