FT  01 DEC 94 / Yesterday


The first Beatles album for 24 years has gone on sale. 'The Beatles: Live At
The BBC', released yesterday, contains 56 songs from 39 radio shows between
1962 and 1965, many of which were never commercially recorded. The
photograph above, taken by Norman Parkinson in 1963, is from a retrospective
of his work which opened yesterday at Hamilton's Gallery in London
