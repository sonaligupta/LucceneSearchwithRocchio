FT  28 APR 94 / UK Company News: Scottish National asset value down


The Scottish National Trust had a net asset value per capital share of 81.2p
at March 31 1994 compared with 94.9p six months earlier.
Net revenue for the six months fell from Pounds 6.6m to Pounds 6m. Earnings
per income share were 3.09p (3.48p) and a second quarterly dividend of 1.55p
maintains the total for the six months at 3.1p.
