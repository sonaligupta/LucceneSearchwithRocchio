FT  06 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Mid Southern Water achieves Pounds 7.47m


In its first set of results since converting to a public company last
August, Mid Southern Water announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 7.47m for the
12 months to March 31. In the previous 15 months profits amounted to Pounds
5.78m.
Turnover was Pounds 31.9m (Pounds 30.9m). A final dividend of 2.6p is
proposed for a total of 87.8p. Earnings per share were 200.2p.
Mr Patrick Packham, chairman, said the company's strong financial
performance had allowed price increases in April 1992 to be restricted to
10.8 per cent compared with 21.2 per cent in April 1991.
He added that Mid Southern's capital expenditure had totalled Pounds 8.93m
in the year and that it was constructing, at an estimated cost of Pounds
24m, treatment works on the River Thames at Bray.
