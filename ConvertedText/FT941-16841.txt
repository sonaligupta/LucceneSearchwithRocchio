FT  07 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Asda Property buys retail centre


Asda Property has completed the purchase of the Wheatley Centre in
Doncaster, South Yorkshire, from the receivers, Touche Ross. Consideration
amounted to Pounds 5.08m cash.
The 175,000 sq ft development is half vacant. Annual rental income from
existing tenants is about Pounds 450,000.
