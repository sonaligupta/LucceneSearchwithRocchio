FT  01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Monks Investment


Monks Investment Trust, reported net assets down 4 per cent to 568.2p at
October 31, against 591.6p six months earlier.
Net revenue for the half year grew to Pounds 4.32m (Pounds 3.53m). Earnings
per share were 5.57p (4.55p) and the interim dividend has been held at 2p.
