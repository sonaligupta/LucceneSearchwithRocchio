FT  23 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Biotechnology Invs


Biotechnology Investments saw its net assets fall in the year to May by 10
per cent to Dollars 182m (Pounds 117m). Net asset value per share fell from
Dollars 3.46 to Dollars 3.28.
However, this outperformed the biotechnology sector as a whole, which fell
by 20 per cent over the same period, Lord Armstrong of Ilminster, chairman,
said.
The group, which is quoted on the London stock exchange, has most of its
investments in the US and expresses its results in dollars.
