FT  30 APR 94 / Cost tracking


A total of 13 of the largest railway stations have so far had their signs
repainted or replaced in the colours of Railtrack at a cost of Pounds
87,921, Mr Roger Freeman, transport minister, said yesterday. Railtrack took
over British Rail's infrastructure at the beginning of the month.
