FT  20 MAY 92 / World News in Brief: Hope of US rate cut


The Dow Jones industrial average closed at a record 3,397.99, up 21.96, as
the biggest monthly drop in US housing starts for more than eight years
renewed speculation that the Federal Reserve might signal a small cut in
interest rates.
