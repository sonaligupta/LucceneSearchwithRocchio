FT  27 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Warnford Invests falls to Pounds 7.66m


Pre-tax profits of Warnford Investments, the property investment group, fell
by 6 per cent from Pounds 8.17m to Pounds 7.66m in the year ended December
25.
Turnover fell from Pounds 12.5m to Pounds 12.1m. Net attributable profits
came to Pounds 5.24m (Pounds 5.57m) and earnings per share were 13.65p
(14.51p). The final dividend has been raised to 4.75p for a total of 7.5p
(7.25p).
The group's investment properties were valued on an open market basis at
Pounds 89.9m (Pounds 103.3m) as at December 25.
