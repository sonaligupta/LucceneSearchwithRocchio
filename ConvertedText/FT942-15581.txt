FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: New City & Comm


New City and Commercial Investment Trust had an undiluted net asset value of
161.46p per share as at January 31.
The figure represented an advance of 66 per cent on the value at February 8
1993 - when the trust commenced trading following the restructuring of the
City and Commercial Investment Trust.
If diluted for exercise of warrants, the value improved 55 per cent to
151.22p.
Earnings per share for the period were 4.544p; a proposed final dividend of
2.8p lifts the total to 4.1p.
