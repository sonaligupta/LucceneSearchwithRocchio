FT  27 JUN 92 / Call for action over gas pay


UNION leaders are recommending 45,000 white-collar workers at British Gas to
take industrial action after rejecting a 4.3 per cent pay offer.
Meanwhile, the UCW communication workers' union yesterday rejected a BT
offer of 4.1 per cent to 20,000 telephone operators.
