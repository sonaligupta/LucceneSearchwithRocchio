FT  16 MAR 94 / Jobs hope dashed


Hopes that up to 700 jobs could be created at Lockerbie, Dumfries and
Galloway, or at Llandrinio, central Wales, were dashed yesterday when the
Fortex Group of New Zealand shelved plans to build a large lamb processing
plant. Fortex blamed disappointing half-year results.
