FT  29 APR 94 / 10% rise in top brands advertising


Advertising spending on the top 100 brands rose by nearly 10 per cent last
year to just more than Pounds 1bn, a survey by Campaign, the advertising
trade magazine, has found. Tesco, the retail chain, topped the list spending
Pounds 27.4m on advertising, just beating McDonald's, the hamburger chain,
which increased its spending by 43 per cent to Pounds 27.3m.
The fastest-growing brand last year was Alliance & Leicester mortgages,
which increased spending more than sevenfold to more than Pounds 4m.
