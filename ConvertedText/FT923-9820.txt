FT  03 AUG 92 / UK Company News: African Lakes in the red


LOSSES of Pounds 45,042 before tax were announced by the African Lakes
Corporation for the half year to March 31.
The group, which has interests in motor trading, agriculture, mining,
engineering and computer supplies, achieved profits of Pounds 81,322 last
time.
Although turnover improved to Pounds 22.7m (Pounds 19.7m) profit margins
were substantially reduced. Losses per share emerged at 5.01p (1.49p).
