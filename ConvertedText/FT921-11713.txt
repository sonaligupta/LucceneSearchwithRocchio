FT  30 JAN 92 / World News in Brief: Agent 'saved 200 lives'


Brian Nelson, British agent who worked among loyalists in Ulster, may have
saved 200 lives, including that of Sinn Fein president Gerry Adams, Belfast
crown court heard. Nelson, 44, awaits sentence after admitting conspiracy to
murder and other charges.
