FT  27 JAN 94 / Four killed on training schemes


Four people died and 183 suffered serious injury on government-funded
training schemes in the first six months of last year, according to
government figures published by the Unemployment Unit, the research and
lobbying organisation.
It said two trainees died in the first six months of 1992 - the figure for
the whole year was three. The figures cover Youth Training, the scheme for
young people, and Training for Work, the programme for unemployed adults.
