FT  23 NOV 92 / Palestinians hopeful on talks


   By REUTER


   TUNIS


Palestinians are likely to attend the next round of the peace talks with
Israel without demanding a delay, Mr Haider Abdel-Shafi, chief negotiator,
said yesterday, Reuter reports from Tunis.
He said Jordan, Syria and Lebanon had already decided to go to the eighth
round of the peace talks, planned in Washington for December 7.
