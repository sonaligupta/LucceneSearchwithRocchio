FT  14 APR 94 / UK Company News: Woodchester Invs


Woodchester Investments has bought in 4.68m ordinary shares at 130p. It
intends to cancel all the shares purchased leaving the total number in issue
at 205.33m. As a result, Credit Lyonnais's stake will increase to 51.75 per
cent.
