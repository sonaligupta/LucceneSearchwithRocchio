FT  27 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Welpac


Welpac, the DIY and gardening hardware and consumer products group, reported
a pre-tax loss of of Pounds 684,000 for the six months to July 31.
The outcome compared with losses of Pounds 248,000 last time and Pounds
305,000 for the full year to January 31 1992.
The latest deficit was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 273,000
(Pounds 38,000) for reorganisation and redundancy, and a net interest charge
of Pounds 298,000 (Pounds 361,000). Losses were 0.87p (0.79p) per share;
turnover rose from Pounds 4.65m to Pounds 7.73m.
