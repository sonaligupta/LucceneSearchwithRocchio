FT  29 JAN 92 / Pollution challenge


SOLICITORS acting for Teesside families who believe that emissions from ICI
and British Steel plants have harmed their children's health are to ask the
High Court to force the companies to release information on their airborne
discharges.
Leigh Day and Co, the London-based firm, said yesterday that ICI and British
Steel declined its request for information to be supplied voluntarily.
