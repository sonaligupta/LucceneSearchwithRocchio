FT  24 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Sharpe & Fisher falls to Pounds 836,000


Sharpe & Fisher, the building supplies and property company, suffered a fall
in profits from Pounds 1.61m to Pounds 836,000 pre-tax for the year to
end-December.
Sales fell by 7 per cent to Pounds 43.8m.
Cost cutting and tighter control of working capital saw a further 10 per
cent cut in staff levels. The action taken resulted in a small trading
profit in the second half.
Earnings emerged at 5.4p (6.3p) per share. The total dividend for the year
is maintained at 4p via a proposed final of 2.5p.
