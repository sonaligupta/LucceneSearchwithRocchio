FT  06 AUG 93 / International Company News: Douglas Holding says earnings
have met expectations


   By REUTER


   FRANKFURT


Douglas Holding, the German perfume and fashion retailer, said it's earnings
this year had met expectations, Reuter reports from Frankfurt.
Douglas said it was difficult to predict for the year since much depended on
Christmas sales. It posted a group operating profit for the first six months
of 1993 of DM43.7m (Dollars 25.7m) after DM43m in the same 1992 period.
