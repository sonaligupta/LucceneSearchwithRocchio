FT  16 MAR 94 / EU approves BMW purchase of Rover


The Commission approved BMW's purchase of the UK carmaker Rover from British
Aerospace yesterday. Also, in its first ever ruling in the newspaper
publishing sector, it approved the Mirror Group-led consortium's purchase of
Newspaper Publishing, which publishes the Independent and Independent on
Sunday in the UK.
