FT  02 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Boys' murder trial starts


Two schoolboys were alleged to have murdered Liverpool toddler James Bulger
by beating him with bricks, stones and a piece of metal before laying his
body across a railway track, as their trial started at Preston Crown Court.
The boys, 10 at the time of the attack, have pleaded not guilty to murder.
