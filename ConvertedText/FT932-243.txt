FT  30 JUN 93 / Ex-Fiat official under inquiry


   By HAIG SIMONIAN


Mr Vittorio Ghidella, the former managing director of Fiat's cars
subsidiary, has been issued with a cautionary warrant by magistrates
investigating alleged irregularities involving a subsidiary of the Efim
state holding company, now in voluntary liquidation, writes Haig Simonian.
