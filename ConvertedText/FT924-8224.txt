FT  12 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Chamberlin & Hill shows 25% advance


Chamberlin & Hill, the ferrous metal foundry, cables and switchgear group,
achieved a 25 per cent advance in the six months to September 30.
From turnover up from Pounds 9.04m to Pounds 9.95m, and after paying
interest of Pounds 96,000 (Pounds 72,000), pre-tax profits came through
Pounds 147,000 higher at Pounds 728,000.
Earnings came out at 6.82p (5.44p) and the interim dividend is maintained at
1.75p.
