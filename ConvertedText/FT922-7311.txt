FT  19 MAY 92 / Nadir judgment


A JUDGE at Stafford Crown Court yesterday reserved judgment for a week on an
application by Mr Asil Nadir, former Polly Peck chairman, to have charges
against him dismissed. Mr Nadir is charged with 69 counts of theft and false
accounting totalling Pounds 100m.
