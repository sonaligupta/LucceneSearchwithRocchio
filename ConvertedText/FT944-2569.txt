FT  16 DEC 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Video connects Internet novices


   By VANESSA HOULDER


Cyberia, the London cafe where customers get hitched up to the Internet, has
produced a video to smooth the learning curve for novices of the net.
Internet: The Cyberian Connection, which was produced with Purple Training,
is a step-by-step guide to Internet services and how to get connected.
Purple Training: UK, tel 0181 742 0607; fax 0181 994 3650.
