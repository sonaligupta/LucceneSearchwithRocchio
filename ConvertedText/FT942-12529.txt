FT  29 APR 94 / Lecturers settle


The long-running dispute over new contracts for lecturers at the City of
Bath College was settled yesterday. Staff voted to accept contracts which
increase the working year from 38 weeks to 42 weeks. The contracts have been
revised to include improved safeguards on weekend and evening work.
