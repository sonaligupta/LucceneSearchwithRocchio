FT  20 APR 91 / Sao Paulo riots


   By VICTORIA GRIFFITH


   SAO PAULO


The Brazilian city of Sao Paulo exploded in riots on Thursday over the
deterioration in public transport which has left thousands without means to
get to work, writes Victoria Griffith in Sao Paulo.
Four thousand people attacked 69 public buses and sacked several shops.
