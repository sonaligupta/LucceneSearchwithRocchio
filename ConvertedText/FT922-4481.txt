FT  05 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Reduced deficit at URS Intl


URS International, the US professional services group quoted in London,
incurred a reduced deficit of Dollars 1.09m (Pounds 600,000) for the 1991
year.
The outcome compared with a restated loss of Dollars 2.42m in 1990, and came
from turnover of Dollars 206,000 (Dollars 1.79m). Losses per share halved to
4 cents.
