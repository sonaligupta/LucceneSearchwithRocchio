FT  24 MAR 92 / International Company News: Stanley Works buys two units


   By AP-DJ


STANLEY Works, the US tools manufacturer, has acquired two tool companies
with combined sales of about Dollars 80m, AP-DJ reports.
The businesses, both units of AXIA of Oak Brook, Illinois, include Goldblatt
Tool, which makes masonry, tile and drywall tools, and employs 180 people at
facilities in Kansas and California.
The business also includes Mail Media, which markets Jensen Tools through
catalogue sales.
