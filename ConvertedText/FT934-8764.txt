FT  13 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa


THE market was firmer ahead of the weekend on good demand for a broad range
of issues. The gold index added 7 to 1,983, industrials 14 to 4,621, and the
overall index 29 to 4,110. De Beers added 75 cents to R86.
