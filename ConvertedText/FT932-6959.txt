FT  26 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Rand Mines shares


Rand Mines Properties, Barlow Rand's property development and gold recovery
company, is ending its listing on the London Stock Exchange with effect from
May 31, but they will be dealt with under Rule 535.4.
All the shares will continue to be listed and traded on the Johannesburg SE.
